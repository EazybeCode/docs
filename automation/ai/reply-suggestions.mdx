## Overview

Eazybe's AI suggestions help you craft better messages faster. Type your draft, and AI provides polished alternatives with different tones and styles.

## How It Works

1. **You type** a draft message (minimum 10 characters)
2. **AI analyzes** the context and content
3. **You receive** multiple rephrased options
4. **You choose** the best version or edit further

## Using AI Suggestions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable AI Suggestions">
    Ensure the **AI Suggestions** toggle is turned ON in your Eazybe settings or the chat toolbar.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Type Your Message">
    In any WhatsApp chat, type your message draft. Must be at least 10 characters.
    
    Example: "hey can you send me the details"
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click Rephrase">
    Click the **Rephrase** button (magic wand icon) or use the keyboard shortcut.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose a Suggestion">
    AI provides multiple options:
    - **Professional**: "Could you please share the details at your earliest convenience?"
    - **Friendly**: "Hey! Would you mind sending over those details when you get a chance? üòä"
    - **Concise**: "Please send the details."
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Send or Edit">
    Click a suggestion to insert it, edit if needed, then send.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## AI Suggestion Types

### Tone Variations

| Tone | Best For |
|------|----------|
| Professional | B2B, formal clients |
| Friendly | B2C, casual relationships |
| Concise | Quick responses, busy contacts |
| Persuasive | Sales, follow-ups |
| Empathetic | Support, complaints |

### Use Cases

**Sales Outreach**
- Original: "wanted to follow up on our call"
- AI: "I wanted to follow up on our recent conversation. Have you had a chance to review the proposal?"

**Customer Support**
- Original: "sorry about that"
- AI: "I sincerely apologize for the inconvenience. Let me help resolve this for you right away."

**Scheduling**
- Original: "can we meet tomorrow"
- AI: "Would you be available for a meeting tomorrow? I'm flexible with timing."

## Settings

### Enabling/Disabling

1. Click the **Settings** icon in Eazybe
2. Go to **AI Features**
3. Toggle **AI Suggestions** on or off

### Customizing Tone

Set your default tone preference:
1. Settings ‚Üí AI Features ‚Üí Default Tone
2. Choose: Professional, Friendly, or Neutral
3. AI will prioritize suggestions matching your preference
