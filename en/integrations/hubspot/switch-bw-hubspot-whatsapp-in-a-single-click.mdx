### Move back and forth between WhatsApp and HubSpot seamlessly

#### Go to HubSpot Button
This Go to HubSpot button provides a quick access to your HubSpot account. 

- It can be found beside the contact name at the top or within the **Profile** and will take you directly to the respective contact's page on HubSpot.

  

- The **redirect buttons** in the **Deals** and **Call Logs** sections similarly direct you to their respective HubSpot pages.

#### GoBacktoWhatsApp Button
EazyBe offers a **Go back to WhatsApp** button which is available on HubSpot. 

Clicking on this button will instantly return you to the WhatsApp conversation of the corresponding HubSpot contact.
