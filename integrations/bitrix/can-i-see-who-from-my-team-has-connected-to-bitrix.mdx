Yes, the organization's admin can view the employees who have connected their Bitrix accounts to the EazyBe WhatsApp Extension by logging into the [EazyBe Workspace](https://workspace.eazybe.com/).


The admin must log in to their account on the EazyBe Workspace.


### Go to the Dashboard Section.
