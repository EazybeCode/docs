## Overview

Internal notes let you add context to conversations that's visible only to your team‚Äînot the customer. Use notes for:

## Adding Notes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Contact Profile">
    In any WhatsApp chat, click the **User Profile** button in the Eazybe sidebar
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Navigate to Docs">
    Click the **Docs** tab (fourth section)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Write Your Note">
    Enter any relevant information about the conversation or customer
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save** to store the note
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Note Types

### Contact Notes

Attached to the customer profile, visible across all their conversations:
- "VIP customer - always prioritize"
- "Prefers communication in Spanish"
- "Previous complaint about shipping in June"

### Conversation Notes

Specific to a single conversation:
- "Promised 10% discount if they order by Friday"
- "Waiting for approval from their manager"
- "Technical issue escalated to dev team"

## Viewing Notes

Notes appear in:
- **User Profile** sidebar in the extension
- **Contact Detail** panel in Team Inbox
- **CRM** (if integrated) as activity or notes

## Team Handoff Example

When handing off a conversation:

```
HANDOFF NOTE - Dec 15

Customer interested in Enterprise plan.
- Budget: $5000/year
- Decision maker: Yes
- Timeline: End of Q1
- Concern: Data security

Next step: Send security whitepaper and schedule demo.

- Priya
```
