Do you have something important going on, but worry that you might forget where the last conversation ended? Maybe the conversation paused before reaching a conclusion, and you want to keep track of it for later.

No worries, with the **EazyBe Notes section**, you can create **Follow-up Notes **and save important messages for future reference.

- ### Open the WhatsApp Chat

Open the WhatsApp chat of your **Zoho Contact**.
### Click on the ZOHO Icon

In the right sidebar of the extension, click on the **Zoho icon**.

  <img src="/images/1217631435.png" alt="" />

- ### Access the Notes Section

The second header under the **Contact section** will be the **Notes section**.
### Create a New Note

Click on the **New Note** button.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1113034097.png" alt="" />
</Frame>
### Fill in the Details

Enter the details for your follow-up note, then click on the **Save Note** button.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1113034349.png" alt="" />
</Frame>
### Manage Note

Once, the note is created, you can **view**, **edit** or **delete** the Note whenever needed. You can also create a new note by clicking on the **Add Note** button and repeating the same process again. This allows you to easily keep track of multiple follow-up notes for each contact, ensuring that all important details are captured for future reference.
