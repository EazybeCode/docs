Do you have a pending task that you want to save and track easily? Don’t worry, the **Tasks Section** of EazyBe’s Zoho Integration can help you stay organized.

Here's how:

- 
#### Open the WhatsApp Chat
Open the WhatsApp chat of your ZOHO contact.

- 
#### Click on the Zoho Icon
In the right sidebar of the extension, click on the ZOHO icon.

  

- 
#### Access the Tasks Section
The second header under the **Contact Section** is the **Tasks Section**. Click on it to access the **Tasks Section**.

- 
#### Create a New Task
Click on the **New Task** button.

  

- 
#### Enter Task Details
Fill in the task details and click on the **Save Task** button.

  

- 
#### View your Task
Once saved, you can **view** your task

- 
#### Manage your Task
You can **Edit or Delete** the task by clicking on the three dots next to the task.
