## Verification & Account Status

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I get the Green Tick on my WhatsApp profile?" icon="circle-check">
    Yes! Official WABA accounts connected through Eazybe qualify for Meta's Green Tick verification.

    **Requirements:**
    - Your WABA account must be active and in good standing
    - Your business must meet Meta's notability requirements
    - You can apply for verification once your account is set up

    <Info>
    The Green Tick is Meta's official verification badge that confirms your business identity to customers.
    </Info>

## Message Templates

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Why was my Message Template rejected?" icon="triangle-exclamation">
    Meta enforces strict content policies for WhatsApp message templates. Common rejection reasons include:

    | Reason | Description |
    |--------|-------------|
    | **Promotional content** | Using promotional language in non-marketing template categories |
    | **Sensitive information** | Requesting passwords, PINs, or financial details |
    | **Formatting errors** | Incorrect variable syntax or placeholder formatting |
    | **Policy violations** | Content that violates WhatsApp Business Policy |

    <Warning>
    Always review Meta's template guidelines before submitting. You can edit and resubmit rejected templates.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## CSV & Contact Upload

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="My CSV upload failed. Why?" icon="file-csv">
    CSV upload failures typically occur due to formatting issues. Ensure your file meets these requirements:

    **File Requirements:**
    - File format must be `.csv`
    - Must contain a column labeled **"Phone"**
    - Phone numbers must include country codes
    - Do **NOT** include the "+" symbol

    **Correct format example:**
    ```
    Phone
    91xxxxxxxxxx
    1xxxxxxxxxx
    44xxxxxxxxxx
    ```

    <Tip>
    For India, use format `91xxxxxxxxxx`. For USA, use `1xxxxxxxxxx`.
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## WABA Coexistence

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Why did my WABA Coexistence account disconnect?" icon="link-slash">
    WABA Coexistence allows you to use both the WhatsApp mobile app and WABA simultaneously. However, certain actions can cause disconnection:

    **Common disconnection triggers:**

    1. **Device switching** - Moving to a new mobile device without proper migration
    2. **Simultaneous sessions** - Using the mobile app and WhatsApp Web at the same time
    3. **Extended inactivity** - No activity for more than 14 days
    4. **Spam violations** - Sending bulk unsolicited messages that violate WhatsApp Business Policy
    5. **App switching** - Moving your number between consumer WhatsApp and Business app

    <Warning>
    To maintain coexistence, avoid these triggers and ensure regular activity on your account.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
