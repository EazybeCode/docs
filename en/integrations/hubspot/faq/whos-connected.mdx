## Can I See Who From My Team Has Connected to HubSpot?

<Check>
**Yes!** Organization administrators can access the Eazybe Workspace to view which team members have connected their HubSpot accounts to the WhatsApp Extension.
</Check>

---

## How to View Connected Team Members

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log In to Eazybe Workspace">
    The admin must log in to their account on the Eazybe Workspace.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Go to the Dashboard Section">
    Navigate to the Dashboard section where you can view comprehensive team HubSpot usage data.

    ![Dashboard showing HubSpot integration status](/images/1178894946.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check the CRM Integrated Column">
    The HubSpot integration status for each team member appears in a table column labeled **"CRM integrated"**.
  </Step>
</Steps>
