**Zoho Vista Mini-CRM inside WhatsApp**

The Zoho Vista Mini-CRM is an innovative feature that brings your Zoho CRM functionalities directly into your WhatsApp interface. This seamless integration allows you to manage leads, contacts, and deals effortlessly while staying focused on your conversations.

Here’s what you can do with the Zoho Vista Mini-CRM:

**Create Zoho Prospectos:** Instantly add new leads from your WhatsApp chats.

- **Edit Zoho Lead Profiles:** Update lead information directly as you chat.
​
- **Log Zoho Lead Notas:** Add quick notes to your leads for better tracking and follow-ups.
- **Create Zoho Contactos:** Easily create new contacts straight from your WhatsApp interface.
- **View Zoho Contact Profiles:** Get instant access to comprehensive contact details.
- **Edit Zoho Contact Profiles:** Make updates to your contacts in real-time.
- **Add Zoho Contact Notas:** Log notes for your contacts to keep your communication organized.
- **Assign Zoho Contact Tareas:** Stay on top of your workflow by creating tasks linked to your contacts.
- **Create Zoho Contact Negocios:** Initiate new deals right from your WhatsApp conversations.
- **Manage Zoho Contact Negocios:** View, update, or track your deals without switching apps.

This feature is designed to save time, enhance productivity, and provide sales and support teams with the tools they need—right within WhatsApp. It’s CRM functionality at your fingertips!
