EazyBe Workspace is designed especially for you to manage your organization's profile seamlessly and with ease.

## Accessing the Workspace

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Organization Button">
    Click on the **Organization button** on the right sidebar to begin accessing the workspace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Login to Workspace">
    A new tab will open up. Login using your desired account credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Workspace Interface">
    You will see the EazyBe Workspace interface where you can manage your organization settings.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Workspace Features

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Team Management" icon="users">
    Manage team members, roles, and permissions for your organization.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Organization Settings" icon="gear">
    Configure organization-wide settings and preferences.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Team Inbox" icon="inbox">
    Access shared conversations and collaborate with your team.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Analytics" icon="chart-line">
    View insights and reports about your team's performance.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
  The workspace provides a centralized location to manage all aspects of your organization's EazyBe setup.
</Tip>
