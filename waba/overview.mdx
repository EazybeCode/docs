## What is WhatsApp Business API?

WhatsApp Business API (WABA) is Meta's official solution for medium and large businesses to communicate with customers at scale.

## WABA vs. Regular WhatsApp

| Feature | Personal/Business App | WABA |
|---------|----------------------|------|
| Broadcast limit | 256 contacts | Unlimited |
| CRM workflow triggers | ❌ | ✅ |
| Message templates | ❌ | ✅ Required |
| Per-message cost | Free | Pay per conversation |
| Multiple users | ❌ | ✅ |
