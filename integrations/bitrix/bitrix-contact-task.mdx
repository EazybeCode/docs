Do you have a pending task you want to save so you can track it anytime? Don’t worry! The Task Section of EazyBe’s Bitrix Integration comes to your rescue.


- 
#### Open the WhatsApp Chat
Open the WhatsApp chat of your Bitrix contact.
​


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1224494345.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


- 
#### Click on the Bitrix Icon
Click on the Bitrix icon located in the right sidebar of the EazyBe extension.
​


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1224494696.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


- 
#### Access the Task Section
The last header under the Contact Section will be the **Tasks section**. Click on it to access the section.
​


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1224497216.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


- 
#### Add a Task
Click on the **Add Task** button.
​


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1224498450.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


- 
#### Enter the Details
Fill in the necessary details and click on the **Save** button.
​


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1224501321.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


- 
#### View Your Created Task
You can see your created task.
​


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1224503000.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


- 
#### Manage Your Task
You have the option to either **view** or **hide** notes. You can also edit or delete the created task by clicking on the three dots.
​


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1224503222.png" alt="" />
</Frame>
