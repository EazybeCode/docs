In this guide, you'll learn how to integrate Freshdesk with the EazyBe extension to streamline your WhatsApp customer support workflow.

## Prerequisites

Before starting, ensure you have:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Latest EazyBe Extension" icon="puzzle-piece">
    Make sure you have the latest version of the EazyBe WhatsApp extension installed.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Freshdesk Account" icon="headset">
    An active Freshdesk account with admin access to generate API keys.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Getting Your Freshdesk Credentials

Before connecting EazyBe, you need to obtain your Freshdesk domain name and API key.

### Step 1: Extract Your Domain/Helpdesk Name

Your Domain/Helpdesk name can be obtained from your Freshdesk URL as shown below:

![Freshdesk Domain Extraction](/images/1318277663.png)

### Step 2: Navigate to Account Settings

Click on the **My Account** icon positioned in the top right corner of your Freshdesk dashboard.

![My Account Settings](/images/1318278504.png)

### Step 3: Generate Your API Key

Open your profile settings page and complete the CAPTCHA check to retrieve your API key.

![API Key Generation](/images/1318278989.png)

<Note>
  Keep your API key secure and do not share it publicly. You'll need this key to complete the integration.
</Note>

## Connecting Freshdesk to EazyBe

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open WhatsApp Web">
    Access WhatsApp Web and ensure the EazyBe extension is active.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Integrations">
    Click on the **Integrations** button on the right-side bar in WhatsApp Web.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Integration Options">
    Click the button to view the integration popup.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Freshdesk">
    Find the **Freshdesk** section and click **Connect**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Your Account">
    Select your Freshdesk account from the options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Account Details">
    Enter your Freshdesk domain name and API key that you obtained earlier.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Integration">
    Once completed, you'll receive confirmation that the integration was successful.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  After successful integration, you can manage Freshdesk tickets directly from your WhatsApp conversations.
</Tip>
