Dynamic WhatsApp Labels allow you to automatically organize your WhatsApp contacts based on real-time HubSpot CRM data. Once set up, these labels are created and updated automatically, giving your team immediate visibility into key customer segments directly within WhatsAppâ€”without any manual effort.

This feature ensures that your WhatsApp communication stays aligned with the most current customer information in HubSpot.

## **About Dynamic Labels**

Dynamic Labels are WhatsApp labels generated and maintained based on rules you define using HubSpot properties. When a contact in HubSpot matches the criteria you specify, they are automatically added to the corresponding WhatsApp label. If a contact no longer meets the criteria, they are removed.

This ensures all labels remain accurate and up to date.

Follow the steps below to create a new Dynamic WhatsApp Label:

Enter a clear, descriptive name for the label.

After saving:

- A new label is automatically created in your WhatsApp account.

- The label continues to update automatically as data changes in HubSpot.

## **Benefits of Dynamic Labels**

- **Automated Organization:** Eliminates the need to manually manage WhatsApp labels.
- **Real-Time Sync:** Labels update automatically as HubSpot records change.
- **Improved Message Targeting:** Ensures your team always communicates with the correct customer segments.
- **Increased Efficiency:** Reduces time spent searching and sorting through contacts.
