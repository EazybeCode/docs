## Overview

Every conversation in the Revenue Inbox has a **state** that indicates its current status. Understanding these states helps you prioritize and manage your team's workload effectively.

## Filtering by State

In the Team Inbox:

1. Click the **Status** filter dropdown
2. Select the state(s) you want to view
3. See only matching conversations

| Filter | Shows |
|--------|-------|
| **All** | Everything |
| **Unreplied** | Waiting for team response |
| **Critical** | High-priority escalations |
| **Active** | Recent activity |
| **Follow-up** | Scheduled reminders due |
