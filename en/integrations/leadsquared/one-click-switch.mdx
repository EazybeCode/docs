EazyBe provides seamless navigation between WhatsApp and LeadSquared, allowing you to switch between platforms instantly without losing context.

## Go to LeadSquared Button

This feature provides quick access to your LeadSquared account directly from WhatsApp.

<Info>
  The button appears in the following locations:
  - **Contact View**: Adjacent to the contact name or within the Profile section, directing you to the corresponding lead's page
  - **Opportunity Page**: Positioned beside the Opportunity name or next to the Profile in the Opportunity's profile section
</Info>

![Go to LeadSquared Button](/images/1188142566.png)

## Go Back to WhatsApp Button

EazyBe provides this button on LeadSquared, enabling you to instantly return to the WhatsApp conversation of the corresponding LeadSquared lead.

### Button Locations

The "Go Back to WhatsApp" button is available in:

- **Manage Leads Page**: Located in the Phone Number column
- **Individual Lead's Page**: Accessible directly on the LeadSquared lead profile

![Go Back to WhatsApp Button](/images/1188143669.png)

<Tip>
  Use these navigation buttons to maintain context while switching between customer conversations in WhatsApp and their lead records in LeadSquared.
</Tip>
