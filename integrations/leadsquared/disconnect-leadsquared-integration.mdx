Did you accidentally integrate the wrong LeadSquared account with your WhatsApp extension, or do you need to disconnect your Leadsquared integration? No worries, we've got you covered!
There are two ways to disconnect Leadsquared from the EazyBe WhatsApp extension:


#### A) Disconnect via the EazyBe WhatsApp extension.

- Click on the **Integrations** icon.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1187954407.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


- Under the Leadsquared section, click the **Disconnect** button.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1224210005.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


- A confirmation pop-up will appear. Click **Disconnect** to confirm, and your Leadsquared account will be successfully disconnected from the EazyBe WhatsApp extension.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1224210197.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


#### B) Disconnect via the EazyBe Workspace

- Click on the **Organisation** button in the right sidebar.


- The **Organisation** page will open in the **EazyBe Workspace**.


- Click on the **Integrations** button in the left sidebar.


- Under the **LeadSquared CRM integration** section, click on the **Configure** button.


- You will see a red **Unlink LeadSquared** button. Click on it.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1187981717.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


- A confirmation pop-up will appear. Click **Unlink** to finalize the disconnection. 


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1187982300.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


After successfully disconnecting, the LeadSquared icon will no longer appear in the right sidebar of the extension. 

To reconnect LeadSquared, simply follow the same steps you used during the initial setup. For detailed instructions, visit [How to connect LeadSquared with EazyBe WhatsApp Extension](https://help.eazybe.com/en/articles/9707595-how-to-integrate-leadsquared-with-the-eazybe-extension).
