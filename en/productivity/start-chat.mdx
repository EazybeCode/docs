Eazybe enables you to initiate WhatsApp conversations with any number without saving it as a contact first. This is perfect for quick outreach to new leads or one-time communications.

## How to Start a New Chat

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click the Start Chat Icon">
    Click the **Start a new chat** icon in the Eazybe interface.

    ![Start Chat Icon](/images/1112598009.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the Phone Number">
    Select the country from the dropdown or include the country code prefix (e.g., +91, +1) before the number. Then enter the contact's phone number.

    ![Enter Phone Number](/images/1112598819.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign a Contact Name (Optional)">
    Optionally, assign a contact name for easy reference. This helps you identify the conversation later.

    ![Assign Contact Name](/images/1112599393.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Messaging">
    Click **Start** to begin messaging. Your conversation will open immediately.

    ![Start Messaging](/images/1112599780.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I message international numbers?">
    You can either select the country flag from the dropdown or include the country code as a prefix before the number (e.g., +91 for India, +1 for USA).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Where are contact details stored?">
    Contact details are stored within the User Profile section. They are not automatically added to your phone contacts.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do I need to save the contact first?">
    No, you can directly enter the recipient's number and start chatting without saving it to your contacts.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Tip>
This feature is especially useful for sales teams who need to quickly reach out to new leads without cluttering their phone contacts.
</Tip>
