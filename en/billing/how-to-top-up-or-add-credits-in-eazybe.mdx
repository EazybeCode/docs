### Summary
This article explains Eazybe credits, which are used for sending WhatsApp messages via the platform including broadcasts and automation. It describes the process for checking your balance, topping up, and maintaining a minimum credit balance to ensure uninterrupted messaging service.

---

### What Are Eazybe Credits?
Eazybe credits are the virtual balance that pays for sending messages through WhatsApp API integration. Each message or broadcast uses credits depending on your usage and integration with Meta pricing.

---

### Types of Messages
Messages sent from Eazybe can include:

- **Broadcasts:** Mass messaging to contacts for marketing, updates, or promotions.
- **Automated WhatsApp Messages:** Transactional notifications and customer updates triggered by your CRM or business processes.
- **Templates:** Pre-approved WhatsApp messages used for utility, marketing, or authentication.

Meta applies different charges per message based on message type and recipient country.

---

### Ensuring Sufficient Credits
To keep sending messages smoothly, you must maintain a minimum balance in your Eazybe account (recommended $10 or more in credits). If your credits run out, broadcasts and automations will be paused until you top up.

---

### How Do I Top Up or Add More Credits?
To add credits to your Eazybe account, follow these steps:

- **Log in to Eazybe:** Access your Eazybe dashboard.
- **Navigate to Billing:** On the sidebar, click "Billing" under your settings.

  

- **Enter Top-Up Amount:** In the Credits section, type the amount you wish to add.

  

- **Complete Purchase:** Click "Purchase Credits" to finalize. Your new credit balance will appear instantly after payment.
