Did you know that **EazyBe** allows you the flexibility to **add Call Logs** for your Salesforce contacts? If you’re wondering how to do this, let’s dive in!

- 
#### Open the WhatsApp Chat
Open the WhatsApp chat of your **Salesforce contact**.

- 
#### Click on the Salesforce Icon
Click on the **Salesforce icon** in the right sidebar of the extension.

  

- 
#### Access the Call Logs Section
In the **Contact Section**, scroll to the bottom where you’ll find the **last header** labeled **Call Logs**. Click on it to access the section.

- 
#### Add a New Call Log
Click on the **Add Call Log** button

  

- 
#### Save Call Log
Enter the necessary details and then click **Save Call Log button**.

  

- 
#### View Your Created Call Log
Once saved, you can view your created call log.

  

- 
#### Edit or Delete Call Log
You can also **edit** or **delete** the created call log by clicking on the **three dots** next to it.
