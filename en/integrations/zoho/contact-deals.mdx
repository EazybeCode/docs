Zoho Deals gives you a clear view of the business opportunities you're pursuing, allowing you to track their progress from start to finish. Manage all your contact-related deals directly from WhatsApp.

## How to Create Contact Deals

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the WhatsApp Chat">
    Access the WhatsApp conversation with the Zoho Contact for whom you want to create a deal.

    ![Open WhatsApp Chat](/images/1217632664.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click on the Zoho Icon">
    In the right sidebar of the EazyBe extension, locate and click on the **Zoho icon** to access your CRM features.

    ![Click Zoho Icon](/images/1113036676.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access the Deals Section">
    Navigate to the **Deals Section** header under the Contact information area.

    ![Deals Section](/images/1113036955.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create a New Deal">
    Click the **New Deal** button to initiate deal creation.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fill in Deal Details and Save">
    Enter the required deal information. You can customize additional properties by clicking the **Edit** button, then save your deal.

    ![Save Deal](/images/1113037242.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing Your Deals

Once created, you can perform the following actions on your deals:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="View" icon="eye">
    Access and review all saved deals for the contact
  </Card>
  <Card title="Edit" icon="pen">
    Modify existing deals to update information or deal stage
  </Card>
  <Card title="Delete" icon="trash">
    Remove deals that are closed or no longer relevant
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
