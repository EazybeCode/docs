## What Are Marketing Templates?

Marketing templates are used for **promotional messages** designed to:
- Drive sales and conversions
- Announce new products or features
- Re-engage inactive customers
- Share offers and discounts

<Warning>
Marketing templates have the **highest cost** per message and require explicit opt-in from recipients.
</Warning>

## Best Practices
