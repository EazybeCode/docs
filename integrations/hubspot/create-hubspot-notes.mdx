No worries, with the **EazyBe Notes section**, you can create **Follow-up Notes **and save important messages for future reference.

#### Select the Contact
Go to the WhatsApp chat of the contact for whom you want to create a follow-up note. Make sure the contact is linked to a HubSpot Contact.


- 
#### Access the Notes Section
Click on the **HubSpot icon** in the right sidebar. The third button on the top bar will take you to the **Notes section**.

#### 5. Attach WhatsApp Messages to Notes (Optional)
You can choose specific messages to attach to your notes. Click on the **Attach_WhatsApp_messages_optional** button and select the starting and ending messages you want to store the follow-up notes for. 


#### 6. Finalize your Notes
Once you're done selecting messages, you can delete any unnecessary ones and click

**Continue**. Then, add any additional notes and save them.
