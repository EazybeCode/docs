Do you want to note down the important details of your conversation with your Zoho Lead? If yes, we’ve got you covered!

- 
#### Open the WhatsApp Chat
Open the WhatsApp chat of your Zoho lead.

- 
#### Click on the Zoho Icon
In the right-side bar of the EazyBe extension, click on the Zoho icon

  

- 
#### Access the Notes Section
The second header under the Lead section will be the **Notes section**.

- 
#### Create a New Note
Click on the New Note button.

  

- 
#### Fill in the Details
Fill in the details and click on the Save Note button.

  

Once created, you can view, edit or delete the Note as needed.
