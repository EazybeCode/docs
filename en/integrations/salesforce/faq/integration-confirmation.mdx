Once your Salesforce integration with the Eazybe WhatsApp extension completes, you will be redirected back to WhatsApp.

## How to Verify Successful Integration

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check the Right Sidebar">
    After the integration process completes, look for the **Salesforce icon** in the right sidebar of your WhatsApp interface.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm the Icon is Visible">
    If you can see the Salesforce icon, it indicates that the integration has been successfully completed.

    ![Salesforce Icon in Sidebar](/images/1223682039.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Check>
  **Success Indicator:** The presence of the Salesforce icon in your right sidebar confirms that Eazybe is properly connected to your Salesforce account.
</Check>
