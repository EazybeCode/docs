- Open WhatsApp Web on your device with Eazybe active.
- Find the "Scheduled" or "Failed" label created by Eazybe.
- Review unsent/failed messages and their details.
- Correct issues (wrong number, integration, or network) as applicable.
​

In case workflow messages did not get sent, please check the scheduled labels in WhatsApp to keep track of failed messages.
