<Info>
**Yes!** EazyBe offers a free trial to test Cloud WhatsApp before committing to a subscription.
</Info>

## Trial Details

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Initial Trial" icon="clock">
    **4 days** free trial for all new users
  </Card>
  <Card title="Extended Trial" icon="star">
    Get an additional **4 days** by submitting a rating
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up">
    Create your EazyBe account and connect the extension to WhatsApp Web.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start Trial">
    Your **4-day free trial** begins automatically when you activate Cloud features.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Extend Your Trial (Optional)">
    Submit a rating for EazyBe to receive an additional **4 days** of trial access.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Subscribe">
    After the trial period, subscribe to a Teams Plan to continue using Cloud WhatsApp.
  </Step>
</Steps>
