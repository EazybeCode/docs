## Overview

The Broadcast Rate List outlines the current per-message pricing for WhatsApp broadcast communications across all supported regions. This reference helps teams accurately forecast messaging costs and maintain compliance with Meta's billing structure.

<Note>
Rates are effective from **January 1st, 2026**. Pricing varies by message type (Marketing vs Utility) and destination country.
</Note>
