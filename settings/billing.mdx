## Accessing Billing

1. Click **Settings** in sidebar
2. Select **Billing** (Admin only)

## Managing Subscription

### Upgrade Plan

1. Go to Billing
2. Click **Upgrade**
3. Select new plan
4. Enter payment details
5. Confirm

### Add Seats

1. Go to Billing
2. Click **Add Seats**
3. Enter number of additional seats
4. See prorated cost
5. Confirm

### Cancel Subscription

1. Go to Billing
2. Click **Cancel Plan**
3. Select reason
4. Confirm cancellation

<Warning>
  Cancellation takes effect at billing period end. You retain access until then.
</Warning>

## Payment Methods

Accepted methods:
- Credit/Debit cards
- UPI (India)
- Net Banking (India)
- Wire transfer (Enterprise)

### Update Payment

1. Go to Billing → Payment Method
2. Click **Update**
3. Enter new details
4. Save

## Invoices

Download past invoices:
1. Go to Billing → Invoices
2. Click invoice to download PDF

## WABA Credits

Separate from subscription:
- Go to **Wallet**
- Add credits for messaging
- View usage history
