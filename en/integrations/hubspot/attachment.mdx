## Does Eazybe Support Attachments Backup?

<Check>
**Yes!** Eazybe provides comprehensive backup support for multiple file formats when syncing WhatsApp conversations with HubSpot.
</Check>

---

## Supported Attachment Types

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Images" icon="image">
    Photos and screenshots shared in chats are fully backed up.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Documents" icon="file">
    Files such as PDFs, Word documents, and spreadsheets are preserved.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Audio Files" icon="microphone">
    Voice messages and other audio recordings are included in backups.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Videos" icon="video">
    Video clips shared in conversations are backed up.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Info>
**Other Files:** Any other types of files shared in WhatsApp chats are also backed up to HubSpot.
</Info>

---

## Where Are Attachments Stored?

All backed up files are preserved within the **WhatsApp Activity object** linked to each contact's HubSpot record. This creates a comprehensive archive of conversations and attachments.
