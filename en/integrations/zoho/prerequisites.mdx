## Requirements Checklist

<Steps>
  <Step title="Active Zoho Account">
    Ensure you have an **active Zoho account** that is set up and functioning properly. Your Zoho CRM should be accessible and ready for integration.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Install Eazybe WhatsApp Extension">
    Ensure that your **Eazybe WhatsApp extension** is properly installed and connected to WhatsApp Web. The extension must be active for the integration to work.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Grant Necessary Permissions">
    Ensure you **grant the necessary permissions** to connect Zoho with WhatsApp. This allows Eazybe to sync your conversations and contacts between both platforms.
  </Step>
</Steps>
