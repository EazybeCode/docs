---
title: 'Contact Capture to Bitrix24'
description: 'Auto-create Bitrix24 contacts from WhatsApp'
---

<Info>
**Setup Time**: 10 minutes | **Difficulty**: Easy
</Info>

## Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable Auto-Create">
    **Settings** → **Bitrix24** → Toggle **Auto-create contacts**
    
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bitrix/auto-create-toggle.png" alt="Auto-create" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure Mapping">
    | WhatsApp | Bitrix24 Field |
    |----------|----------------|
    | Phone | Phone |
    | Name | Name |
    | - | Source = "WhatsApp" |
  </Step>
</Steps>
