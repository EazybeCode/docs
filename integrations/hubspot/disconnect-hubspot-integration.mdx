Did you integrate the wrong HubSpot account or simply want to disconnect the integration? No worries, we've got you covered! 

There are two ways to disconnect HubSpot from the EazyBe WhatsApp extension:


#### A) Disconnect via the EazyBe WhatsApp extension.

- Click on the **Integrations** icon.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1185347379.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


- Under the **HubSpot** section, click the **Disconnect** button.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1185347699.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


- A confirmation pop-up will appear. Click **Disconnect **to confirm, and your HubSpot account will be successfully disconnected from the EazyBe WhatsApp extension.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1185347862.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


#### B) Disconnect via the EazyBe Workspace

- Click on the **Organisation** button in the right sidebar.


- The **Organisation** page will open in the **EazyBe Workspace**.


- Click on the **Integrations** button in the left sidebar.


- Under the **HubSpot CRM integration** section, click on the **Configure** button.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1182339219.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


- You'll see a red **Unlink **button. Click on it.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1182354561.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


- A confirmation pop-up will appear. Click **Unlink** to finalize the disconnection.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1175917277.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


After successfully disconnecting, the HubSpot icon will no longer appear in the right sidebar of the extension.

To reconnect HubSpot, simply follow the same steps you used during the initial setup. For detailed instructions, visit [How to Install and Connect EazyBe to your HubSpot account](https://help.eazybe.com/en/articles/9771596-how-to-install-and-connect-eazybe-to-your-hubspot-account).
