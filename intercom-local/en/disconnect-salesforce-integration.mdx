Did you integrate the wrong Salesforce account or simply want to disconnect the integration? No worries, we've got you covered!

There are two ways to disconnect Salesforce from the EazyBe WhatsApp extension:

### A) Disconnect via the EazyBe WhatsApp extension.

- Click on the **Integrations** icon.

  

- Under the **Salesforce** section, click the **Disconnect** button.

  

- A confirmation pop-up will appear. Click **Disconnect **to confirm, and your Salesforce account will be successfully disconnected from the EazyBe WhatsApp extension.

  

### B) Disconnect via the EazyBe Workspace

- Click on the **Organisation** button in the right sidebar.
- The **Organisation** page will open in the **EazyBe Workspace**.
- Click on the **Integrations** button in the left sidebar.
Under the **Salesforce CRM integration** section, click on the **Configure** button.

  

- You'll see a red **Unlink **button. Click on it.

  

- A confirmation pop-up will appear. Click **Unlink** to finalize the disconnection.

  

After successfully disconnecting, the Salesforce icon will no longer appear in the right sidebar of the extension.

To reconnect Salesforce, simply follow the same steps you used during the initial setup. For detailed instructions, visit [How to Install and Connect EazyBe to your Salesforce account](https://help.eazybe.com/en/articles/9929131-how-to-install-and-connect-eazybe-with-salesforce-account).
