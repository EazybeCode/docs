#### Move back and forth between WhatsApp and LeadSquared seamlessly

#### Go to LeadSquared Button
This Go to LeadSquared button provides a quick access to your LeadSquared account.

- It can be found beside the contact name at the top or within the **Profile** and will take you directly to the respective lead's page on LeadSquared.
- This button can also be found on the **Opportunity page**, located either beside the **Opportunity name** or next to the **Profile** in the Opportunity's profile section.

#### GoBacktoWhatsApp Button
EazyBe offers a **Go back to WhatsApp** button which is available on LeadSquared.

Clicking on this button will instantly take you back to the WhatsApp conversation of the corresponding LeadSquared lead.

This button is available in the Manage Leads page in the LeadSquared and can be seen in the Phone Number column of the respective lead.

  

The button is also available on a respective Lead's page on LeadSquared.
