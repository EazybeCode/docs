## Overview

Integrating HubSpot with your Eazybe WhatsApp extension is simple and quick. Before you begin, ensure you meet the following prerequisites for a smooth setup process.

<Note>
Meeting all prerequisites ensures a seamless integration experience and prevents common setup issues.
</Note>

---

## Requirements Checklist

<Steps>
  <Step title="Active HubSpot Account">
    Ensure your HubSpot account is set up and functioning properly. You'll need access to your HubSpot portal to complete the integration.

    <Info>
    Both **free** and **paid** HubSpot plans support the Eazybe integration.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Install Eazybe WhatsApp Extension">
    Make sure your Eazybe WhatsApp extension is properly installed and connected to WhatsApp Web.

    If you haven't installed the extension yet, visit the [Chrome Web Store](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/eazybe) to download it.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Required Permissions">
    Ensure you have the necessary permissions from your Administrator to connect HubSpot to WhatsApp:

    - **App Marketplace Access** — Permission to install and manage connected apps
    - **File Access Permissions** — Permission to access and manage files within HubSpot
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Quick Reference

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **HubSpot Account** | Active account (free or paid) |
| **Eazybe Extension** | Installed and connected to WhatsApp Web |
| **Admin Permissions** | App Marketplace and File Access |
