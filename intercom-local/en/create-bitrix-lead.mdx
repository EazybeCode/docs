- 
#### Open the WhatsApp Chat of the Lead

Open the WhatsApp chat of the respective Bitrix lead.
​

  

#### 2. Click on the Bitrix icon
Click on the **Bitrix** icon in the right sidebar of the extension.
​

  

#### 3. Link to an Existing Bitrix Lead
If the lead already exists in Bitrix, click on this option and select the lead from the dropdown.
​

  

#### 4. Create a New Lead
If the lead is new and hasn't been created on Bitrix yet, click on the **Create Lead** button.
​

  

#### 5. Fill in the Lead Details

- Enter the required details in the fields provided.
- You can customize the fields by clicking on the **Edit** button and selecting the desired user properties.
​

  

#### 6. View Lead on Bitrix
Once the Lead is created or linked, you have the option to view it directly in Bitrix by clicking on the **View on Bitrix** button under the Profile section of the lead.
