## Overview

As an administrator, you can manage which team members have access to the Salesforce integration and control their permissions.

## Checking Team Connections

You can view who from your team has connected to Salesforce through the EazyBe extension.

### Steps to View Connected Users

1. Open the EazyBe extension in WhatsApp Web
2. Navigate to **Settings** then **Integrations** then **Salesforce**
3. Click on **Team Connections** to see a list of connected users
4. View the last sync time for each team member

## Managing User Access

### Granting Access

- Ensure users have the EazyBe Chrome extension installed
- Users must have valid Salesforce credentials
- Admin can invite team members through the EazyBe workspace

### Revoking Access

- Navigate to workspace settings
- Select the user whose access you want to revoke
- Click **Disconnect** to remove their Salesforce integration
