This guide explains how to manage pending tasks for Salesforce Leads using the EazyBe WhatsApp Extension. Track and save tasks directly from your WhatsApp conversations.

## Creating a Lead Task

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the WhatsApp Chat">
    Access the WhatsApp conversation associated with your Salesforce Lead contact.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Salesforce Integration">
    Locate and select the Salesforce icon positioned in the right sidebar of the browser extension.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Tasks Section">
    Under the Lead Section, find the third header labeled **Tasks** and click to open it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create New Task">
    Click the **New Task** button to initiate task creation.

    ![New Task Button](/images/1116539979.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Task">
    Input the task details and select **Save Task** to store the entry.

    ![Save Task Form](/images/1116540562.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing Tasks

Once a task is created, you can view and manage it directly from the Tasks section.

![Task Management Options](/images/1116541113.png)

Use the three-dot menu to:
- **Edit** - Modify task details
- **Delete** - Remove the task

<Info>
  Tasks are synced directly to Salesforce when you save. The task information will be reflected in your Salesforce Lead record.
</Info>
