This guide walks you through the process of updating the number of employees in your Workspace billing settings. Adjusting employee counts ensures accurate billing for your subscription.

## Updating Employee Count

Follow these steps to update the number of employees in your Workspace:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Workspace Settings">
    Navigate to your Workspace settings by clicking on the settings icon in your dashboard.

    ![Step 1 - Access Workspace Settings](/images/intercom/1546465582-1.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Go to Billing Section">
    Locate and click on the **Billing** section to access your payment and subscription settings.

    ![Step 2 - Billing Section](/images/intercom/1546465583-2.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Update Employee Count">
    Find the employee count field and enter the updated number of employees in your organization.

    ![Step 3 - Update Employee Count](/images/intercom/1546465585-3.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm Changes">
    Review your changes and click **Save** or **Confirm** to apply the updated employee count to your billing.

    ![Step 4 - Confirm Changes](/images/intercom/1546465584-4.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Your billing will be automatically adjusted based on the new employee count. Changes typically take effect in the next billing cycle.
</Info>

## Important Notes

- **Billing adjustments** - Increasing or decreasing employee count will affect your subscription cost
- **Pro-rated charges** - Mid-cycle changes may result in pro-rated charges or credits
- **Minimum seats** - Some plans have minimum seat requirements

<Tip>
  Keep your employee count accurate to avoid overpaying or having team members lose access to EazyBe features.
</Tip>
