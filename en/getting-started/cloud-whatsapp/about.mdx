## What is Cloud?

**Cloud** allows you to sync WhatsApp conversations and schedule messages from your mobile without opening a laptop. Previously, backing up chats or sending scheduled messages required keeping your laptop on and WhatsApp Web open.

Now, with Cloud, you can manage everything from your **mobile device**, eliminating desk dependency.
