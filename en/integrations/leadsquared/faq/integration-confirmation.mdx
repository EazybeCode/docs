After completing the LeadSquared integration with EazyBe's WhatsApp extension, you'll be redirected back to WhatsApp. Here's how to verify the integration was successful.

## Verify Successful Integration

<Steps>
  <Step title="Look for the LeadSquared Icon">
    After the integration process completes, check your WhatsApp Web interface for the LeadSquared icon in the right sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Icon Visibility">
    If you can see the LeadSquared icon, it indicates that the integration has been successfully completed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

![LeadSquared Integration Confirmation](/images/1224453905.png)

<Info>
  The LeadSquared icon appearing in the sidebar confirms that EazyBe is now connected to your LeadSquared account and ready to sync your WhatsApp conversations.
</Info>
