---
title: "I have disconnected the Cloud WhatsApp, how can I be sure that the disconnection is successful?"
description: ""
---

There are two ways to check if your Cloud WhatsApp has been disconnected:

- The cloud icon in the extension will turn **red**.
- In the EazyBe workspace, the status next to your account name will change to **Stopped** and the Cloud Connection button will display **Restart WhatsApp**.
