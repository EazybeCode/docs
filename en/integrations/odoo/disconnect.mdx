## Overview

If you no longer need the Odoo integration or want to connect a different account, you can easily disconnect the integration from the Eazybe panel.

<Note>
  Disconnecting the integration will stop syncing data between WhatsApp and
  Odoo. Existing data in Odoo will remain unchanged.
</Note>

---

## Disconnecting Odoo

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open WhatsApp Web with Eazybe Extension">
    Open WhatsApp Web and ensure the Eazybe extension is active.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Navigate to Integrations">
    Click on the **CRM/Integrations** button in the Eazybe panel.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Odoo">
    Navigate to **Odoo** from the connected integrations.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Disconnect">
    Click on the **Disconnect** button to remove the integration.
    <img src="/images/odoo-13.png" alt="Disconnect Button" />

    <Warning>
    After disconnecting, you will need to reconnect to use Odoo features again.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm Disconnection">
    Confirm the disconnection when prompted.
    <Check>
    Your Odoo account has been successfully disconnected.
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## What Happens After Disconnecting

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Data Remains" icon="database">
    All contacts, leads, notes, and chat backups created in Odoo during the
    integration will remain in your Odoo account.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Sync Stops" icon="arrows-rotate">
    New WhatsApp conversations will no longer sync to Odoo after
    disconnection.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

---

## Reconnecting

If you want to reconnect Odoo after disconnecting:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Follow Connection Steps">
    Follow the [Account
    Connection](/en/integrations/odoo/account-connection) guide to
    reconnect your Odoo account.
  </Step>
</Steps>
