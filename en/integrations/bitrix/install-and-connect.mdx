Follow these steps to install the Eazybe extension and connect it to your Bitrix24 account.

## Installation Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open WhatsApp Web">
    Navigate to [WhatsApp Web](https://web.whatsapp.com) and ensure your WhatsApp account is connected.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access Integrations">
    Click on the **Integrations** button in the Eazybe extension panel to view available CRM integrations.

    ![Integrations Button](/images/1112649892.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Bitrix24">
    Find **Bitrix24** in the list of available integrations and click the **Connect** button.

    ![Connect Bitrix24](/images/1195253698.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Bitrix Marketplace">
    You will be redirected to the Bitrix24 Marketplace. Click **Install** to add the Eazybe application to your Bitrix24 account.

    ![Bitrix Marketplace](/images/1204587764.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Accept Permissions">
    Review the required permissions and click **Accept** to authorize the integration. This allows Eazybe to sync data with your Bitrix24 CRM.

    ![Accept Permissions](/images/1204585520.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify Connection">
    Once permissions are accepted, return to WhatsApp Web. Your Bitrix24 integration should now be active and ready to use.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  After connecting, you can start syncing WhatsApp contacts with your Bitrix24 CRM and access customer information directly from your chats.
</Tip>
