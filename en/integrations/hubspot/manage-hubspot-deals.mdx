You can easily manage the deals associated with a HubSpot contact directly from your WhatsApp chat.

#### Steps to Manage Deals:

- **Open the WhatsApp chat** of the HubSpot contact you want to manage deals for.
- Click on the **HubSpot icon** in the right sidebar.
- Click on the **Deals Section** under the 4th header.
- To view or manage a specific deal, click on the **redirect button** beside the deal name to go to the respective deal's page in HubSpot.

#### Editing a Deal:

- To make changes, click on the **edit icon** next to the deal.
- You can edit properties like **pipeline** and **deal stage** as the deal progresses.
- To add more properties, click the **Edit button** at the top and choose from the range of available properties.

After making changes, click **Save** to update the deal properties.
