## Overview

Saved Replies (Quick Replies) let you create pre-written responses for common scenarios, reducing response time and ensuring consistent messaging across your team.

## Creating Quick Replies

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Quick Replies">
    In the Eazybe sidebar, click the **Quick Reply** icon
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create New">
    Click **+ Add Quick Reply**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Write Your Message">
    Enter the response text. You can include:
    - Plain text
    - Emojis
    - Variables (like {{name}})
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Attachment (Optional)">
    Attach an image, document, or voice note (max 20MB)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Visibility">
    Choose who can see this reply:
    - **Personal** - Only you
    - **Shared** - Entire organization
    - **Team** - Specific teams only
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save">
    Click Save to create the quick reply
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Using Quick Replies

1. Open any WhatsApp chat
2. Click the **Quick Reply** button or type `/`
3. Search or browse available replies
4. Click to insert into chat
5. Customize if needed, then send
