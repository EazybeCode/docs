title: "Where can I check Last sync time for my team using HubSpot integration?"
description: ""
---

Admin can check the last sync time for their team on [EazyBe workspace](https://workspace.eazybe.com/). 

- Admin should log in to the EazyBe Çalışma Alanı using their credentials.
Click on the **Kontrol Paneli** button.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1185349004.png" alt="" />
</Frame>
In the **Last Chat Synced** column, you will see the last sync time of HubSpot chats for all your employees.
