## Overview

The Unreplied Chats AI Agent automatically monitors your team's WhatsApp conversations, identifies which ones need immediate attention, and alerts your team based on urgency.

## How It Works

The AI agent scans every unreplied chat and applies a priority label based on:

- **Keywords**: urgent, cancel, refund, help, ASAP, complaint
- **Customer Value**: High-value customers from your CRM
- **Time Sensitivity**: How long the message has been waiting
- **Sentiment Analysis**: Detecting frustration or urgency in tone
