## What is Eazybe?

Eazybe is a Chrome extension that transforms WhatsApp into a powerful sales tool by integrating it with your CRM. Monitor your team's conversations, sync chats automatically, and never lose a lead again.

## Why Eazybe?

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Never Lose a Lead">
    Every WhatsApp conversation is automatically synced to your CRM. No manual data entry, no forgotten follow-ups.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Monitor Your Team">
    Revenue Inbox gives you complete visibility into all team conversations without accessing personal phones.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Work From WhatsApp">
    Create contacts, deals, tasks, and tickets directly from WhatsApp using our Mini-CRM sidebar.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Scale with Automation">
    Send broadcast campaigns, automate follow-ups, and use AI to prioritize hot leads.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
