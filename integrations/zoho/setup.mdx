## Zoho CRM Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Integrations">
    Click **Integrations** in Eazybe panel
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Zoho CRM">

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/zoho/zoho-connect.png" alt="Connect to Zoho" />
</Frame>

    Click **Connect to Zoho CRM**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize">

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/zoho/zoho-permissions.png" alt="Zoho permissions" />
</Frame>

    Login and grant permissions
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure">
    Set sync preferences
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify">
    Open a chat to verify connection
  </Step>
</Steps>
