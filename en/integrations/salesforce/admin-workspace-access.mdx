Administrators have comprehensive access to the Eazybe workspace, enabling them to monitor and oversee team performance metrics and organizational information.

## Viewing Team Details

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the Organisation Page">
    Click the **Organisation** button located in the right sidebar.

    ![Organisation Button](/images/1185334534.png)

    <Note>
      This action opens a new Chrome tab. Administrator credentials are required for login.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access the Dashboard">
    In the workspace, locate and click the **Dashboard** button in the left sidebar.

    ![Dashboard Button](/images/1196823525.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Check Team Details

Admins can monitor the following information:

- **Last activity** of team members
- **Last chat synced time**
- **CRM integrations** status across the organization

![Team Details View](/images/1196824541.png)

<Tip>
  The **Analytics** tab provides detailed messaging analytics for either the entire team or specific personnel.
</Tip>

![Analytics Tab](/images/1182373085.png)
