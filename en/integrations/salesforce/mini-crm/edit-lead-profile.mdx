This guide explains how to modify a Lead profile connected to Salesforce through the EazyBe WhatsApp Extension.

## Editing Lead Profile Properties

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the WhatsApp Chat">
    Open the WhatsApp chat of the respective Salesforce Lead you want to edit.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click the Edit Button">
    Click the **Edit** button in the Contact Card of the Profile Section that appears in the Mini-CRM panel.

    ![Edit Button in Profile Section](/images/1223672830.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit Lead Details">
    A dialog window opens allowing you to:
    - Edit or select user properties as required
    - Specify whether each selected property is required or optional

    ![Edit Lead Properties Dialog](/images/1223673014.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save the Changes">
    Click **Apply** to update the Contact card with the properties you have selected. Edit the property fields as needed and click **Save** to finalize your changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Changes are synced directly to Salesforce when you save. The updated information will be reflected in your Salesforce Lead record.
</Info>
