Yes, EazyBe provides a free trial period for new users to test the Zoho CRM integration before committing to a subscription.

## Trial Period Details

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Initial Trial" icon="clock">
    **4 days** of full access to test all integration features
  </Card>
  <Card title="Extended Trial" icon="star">
    **Additional 4 days** available if you leave a rating
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How to Get the Extended Trial

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start Your Trial">
    Install EazyBe and connect your Zoho CRM account to begin your initial 4-day trial period.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Test the Features">
    Explore all integration features including contact sync, message backup, and Mini-CRM view during your trial.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Leave a Rating">
    If you'd like more time to evaluate, leave a rating for the EazyBe extension to receive an additional 4 days.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  During the trial period, you have full access to all Zoho integration features. This gives you enough time to evaluate whether EazyBe meets your business needs.
</Info>

<Tip>
  Make the most of your trial by testing key features like contact syncing, WhatsApp message backup to Zoho, and the Mini-CRM view functionality.
</Tip>
