The LeadSquared Administrator Dashboard provides detailed analytics on the leads connected to your LeadSquared account. It offers comprehensive insights to help you track lead activities and measure engagement.

## Dashboard Sections

### 1. Key Lead Metrics

Get insights into new leads, engagement levels, and active lead percentages.

![Key Lead Metrics](/images/1141013430.png)

### 2. Lead by Sources

View different acquisition channels and understand where your leads are coming from within specified timeframes.

![Lead by Sources](/images/1141013656.png)

### 3. Overall Lead Funnel

Get an overview of active stages and track lead progression through your sales funnel.

![Overall Lead Funnel](/images/1141013954.png)

### 4. Top New Leads

Highlights the most promising prospects within given time periods.

![Top New Leads](/images/1141014331.png)

### 5. Recently Sent Email Campaigns

Coverage of recent email efforts with recipient counts, open rates, and click-through data.

![Email Campaigns](/images/1141009994.png)

### 6. Email Summary

Overview of emails sent across various channels.

![Email Summary](/images/1141011145.png)

<Tip>
  You can create custom dashlets by clicking on the **Add Dashlet** button in the top right corner of the dashboard.
</Tip>
