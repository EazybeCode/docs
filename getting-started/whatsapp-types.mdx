## Overview

WhatsApp offers different account types for personal and business use. Understanding the differences helps you choose the right setup for your needs.

## Comparison Table

| Feature | Personal WhatsApp | Business App | Business API (WABA) |
|---------|-------------------|--------------|---------------------|
| Cost | Free | Free | Pay per conversation |
| Broadcast Limit | 256 contacts | 256 contacts | Unlimited |
| Automation | ❌ None | Basic auto-reply | ✅ Full automation |
| CRM Sync via Eazybe | ✅ Yes | ✅ Yes | ✅ Yes |
| Revenue Inbox | ✅ Yes | ✅ Yes | ✅ Yes |
| Workflow Triggers | ❌ No | ❌ No | ✅ Yes |
| Message Templates | ❌ No | ❌ No | ✅ Required |
| Green Tick Badge | ❌ No | ❌ No | ✅ Available |
| Multiple Users | ❌ No | ❌ No | ✅ Yes |

## Personal WhatsApp

Standard WhatsApp account for personal use.

**Best for:**
- Individual salespeople
- Small teams just getting started
- Basic chat backup needs

**Eazybe Features Available:**
- ✅ CRM contact sync
- ✅ Chat backup to CRM
- ✅ Revenue Inbox monitoring
- ✅ Quick replies
- ✅ Labels and organization
- ❌ Broadcasting beyond 256
- ❌ Workflow automation

## WhatsApp Business App

Free app designed for small businesses.

**Best for:**
- Small businesses (1-10 employees)
- Teams needing business profile features
- Basic automation needs

**Eazybe Features Available:**
- ✅ All Personal WhatsApp features
- ✅ Business profile information
- ✅ WhatsApp Business labels sync
- ✅ Catalog integration
- ❌ Large-scale broadcasting
- ❌ Advanced workflow automation

**How to Get:**
1. Download WhatsApp Business from App Store or Play Store
2. Register with your business phone number
3. Set up your business profile

## WhatsApp Business API (WABA)

Official API for medium to large businesses.

**Best for:**
- Teams with 10+ salespeople
- High-volume messaging needs
- Companies requiring automation
- Businesses needing verified badge

**Eazybe Features Available:**
- ✅ All Business App features
- ✅ Unlimited broadcasting
- ✅ CRM workflow automation
- ✅ Message templates
- ✅ Advanced analytics
- ✅ Green tick verification (optional)
- ✅ Multiple user access

**How to Get:**
See our [WABA Setup Guide](/waba/getting-started) for detailed instructions.

## Which Should You Choose?

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Solo/Small Team">
    **Recommendation: Personal WhatsApp or Business App**
    
    If you're an individual salesperson or small team:
    - Start with your existing WhatsApp
    - Eazybe will sync all chats to your CRM
    - Upgrade to Business App for business profile features
    - No additional costs
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Growing Team (5-20)">
    **Recommendation: Business App + Eazybe**
    
    For growing sales teams:
    - Use WhatsApp Business App for the professional profile
    - Eazybe provides team monitoring via Revenue Inbox
    - Consider WABA if you need workflow automation
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Large Team (20+)">
    **Recommendation: WhatsApp Business API**
    
    For enterprise sales teams:
    - WABA enables unlimited broadcasting
    - Automate messages from CRM workflows
    - Full API access for custom integrations
    - Verified badge builds trust
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="High-Volume Outreach">
    **Recommendation: WhatsApp Business API**
    
    If you send 500+ messages daily:
    - Personal/Business App will get rate-limited
    - WABA has higher limits (up to 100K/day)
    - Template-based messaging ensures compliance
    - Analytics for campaign performance
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Migrating Between Account Types

### Personal to Business App

<Steps>
  <Step title="Backup Current Chats">
    WhatsApp → Settings → Chats → Chat Backup
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install Business App">
    Download WhatsApp Business from your app store
  </Step>
  <Step title="Transfer Data">
    Follow the prompts to transfer from Personal WhatsApp
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reconnect Eazybe">
    Scan the QR code again with WhatsApp Business
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Business App to WABA

<Steps>
  <Step title="Keep Business App">
    With Eazybe's Coexistence feature, you can use both simultaneously
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Up WABA">
    Follow our [WABA Setup Guide](/waba/getting-started)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable Coexistence">
    Configure [Coexistence Mode](/getting-started/coexistence-mode) to use app + API together
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  **Coexistence Mode** is unique to Eazybe. It lets you use WhatsApp Business App for personal conversations while using the API for broadcasts and automation.
</Info>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I use my personal number for WABA?">
    Yes, but you'll need to migrate it to WABA. With Coexistence, you can keep using the Business App on the same number.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Will I lose my chat history when upgrading?">
    - Personal → Business App: Chat history transfers automatically
    - Business App → WABA with Coexistence: History is preserved
    - Business App → WABA without Coexistence: You'll start fresh on API
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What does WABA cost?">
    Meta charges per conversation:
    - Marketing: ~$0.05-0.15 per conversation
    - Utility: ~$0.02-0.05 per conversation
    - Service: Free for 24 hours
    
    Exact pricing varies by country. [See pricing details →](/waba/wallet)
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Do I need WABA for Eazybe?">
    No! Eazybe works with all WhatsApp types. WABA is only needed for:
    - Broadcasting to 256+ contacts
    - CRM workflow automation
    - Advanced API features
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
