- 
#### Open the WhatsApp Chat of the Contact
Open the WhatsApp chat of the respective Bitrix contact.
​


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1224312745.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


- 
#### Click on the Bitrix icon
Click on the **Bitrix** icon in the right sidebar of the extension.
​


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1224332367.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


- 
#### Link to an Existing Bitrix Contact
If the contact already exists in Bitrix, click on this option and select the contact from the dropdown.
​


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1224335845.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


- 
#### Create a New Contact
If the contact is new and hasn't been created on Bitrix yet, click on the **Create Contact** button.
​


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1224417577.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


- 
#### Fill in the Contact Details

Enter the required details in the fields provided.


- You can customize the fields by clicking on the **Edit** button and selecting the desired user properties.
​


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1224419844.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


- 
#### View Contact on Bitrix
Once the contact is created or linked, you have the option to view it directly in Bitrix by clicking on the **View on Bitrix** button under the Profile section of the contact.
