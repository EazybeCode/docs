Admin has full access to the [EazyBe workspace](https://workspace.eazybe.com/), allowing them to view and manage team details and analytics. 

Here’s how:


#### Viewing Team Details:

- Access the Organisation Page:
Click on the **Organisation button** in the right sidebar. This will open a new Chrome tab


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1185334534.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


- Admin must log in using their credentials.


- View Employees:
Once logged in, the Organisation page will open.

  <img src="/images/1182359490.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


3. Access the Dashboard:
- In the workspace, click on the **Dashboard button** in the left sidebar.

  <img src="/images/1185349844.png" alt="" />

**4. Check Team details:**


- In the Dashboard, the admin can view the **last activity**, **last chat synced time**, and **CRM integrations** for all employees.
