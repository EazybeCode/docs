## Overview

When a contact doesn't already exist in your Zoho CRM, you can quickly create a new lead directly from your WhatsApp conversation using the Eazybe extension. This saves time and ensures your leads are captured immediately.

## How to Create a Zoho Lead

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open WhatsApp Chat">
    Launch the WhatsApp conversation with the contact you want to add as a lead in Zoho CRM.

    ![Open WhatsApp Chat](/images/1112651537.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access Zoho Icon">
    Click on the **Zoho icon** in the right sidebar of the Eazybe extension to open the Mini-CRM panel.

    ![Access Zoho Icon](/images/1112651863.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete Lead Details">
    Fill in the required and necessary details for the new lead:
    - Contact name
    - Email address
    - Phone number
    - Company information
    - Any additional custom fields

    Once all information is entered, click on the **Create Lead** button.

    ![Complete Lead Details](/images/1112656078.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirmation">
    Upon successful completion, the lead is added to your Zoho CRM system. You can now manage this lead directly from the Mini-CRM view.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Make sure to fill in all required fields to ensure the lead is properly created in your Zoho CRM.
</Tip>
