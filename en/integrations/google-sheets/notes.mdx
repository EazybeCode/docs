Do you want to write follow-up notes for conversations left midway so you can keep things on track? EazyBe has you covered.

## How to Create and Manage Notes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the WhatsApp Chat">
    Access the WhatsApp chat of your Google Sheet contact.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access the Google Sheets Panel">
    Click on the **Google Sheet icon** in the right-side bar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Locate the Notes Section">
    Find the **Notes** section in the interface.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Follow-Up Notes">
    Enter your follow-up comments and click **Save**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Auto-Generated Timestamp">
    The system automatically generates a timestamp when the note is created.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit or Delete Notes">
    You can edit or delete your notes as needed using the available options.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Access your notes anytime by clicking the **View in Sheets** button in the follow-up notes section.
</Tip>
