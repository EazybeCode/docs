ZOHO Anlaşmalar gives you a clear view of the business opportunities you're pursuing, allowing you to track their progress from start to finish. They help you organize all the essential details related to each deal, from its value and current stage in the pipeline to the associated contact information, ensuring that you stay on top of every deal.

- ### Open the WhatsApp Chat

Open the WhatsApp chat of your Zoho contact.
### Click on the ZOHO Icon

In the right sidebar of the extension, click on the **ZOHO icon**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1217632664.png" alt="" />
</Frame>
- ### Access the Anlaşmalar Section

The final header under the Contact section is the **Anlaşmalar Sectio**n, click on it to access the section.
### Create a New Deal

Click on the **New Deal** button.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1113036676.png" alt="" />
</Frame>
### Fill in the Deal Details

Enter the necessary deal details. You can also select **custom properties **by clicking on the **Edit button**. Once the details are completed, click on the **Save Deal** button.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1113036955.png" alt="" />
</Frame>
### Manage the Deal

Your deal will be created, and you can:

- **Edit** the deal by clicking the three dots and selecting **Edit**.
- **Delete** the deal by clicking the three dots and selecting **Delete**.
- **Add new deal** by clicking the **Add Deal** button and repeating the process.
