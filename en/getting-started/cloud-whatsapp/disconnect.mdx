---
title: "Disconnect Cloud"
description: "Learn how to disconnect EazyBe Cloud WhatsApp through the Workspace or the Chrome Extension"
---

<Warning>
Once disconnected, you will no longer be able to sync chats or schedule messages while your laptop is closed.
</Warning>

There are two ways to disconnect your Cloud WhatsApp:

## Method 1: Disconnect through EazyBe Workspace

<Steps>
  <Step title="Locate the Stop Connection Button">
    In your EazyBe Workspace, find the account you want to disconnect and click the **Stop Connection** button.

    ![Stop Connection Button](/images/1203746395.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm Disconnection">
    The status will change to **Stopped** and the button will convert to "Restart WhatsApp".

    ![Disconnected Status](/images/1203758350.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify in Extension">
    The cloud icon in the extension will turn **red**, confirming the disconnection.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Method 2: Disconnect through EazyBe Extension

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Cloud Menu">
    Click the cloud icon in the EazyBe WhatsApp extension.

    ![Cloud Icon in Extension](/images/1203747726.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Disconnect">
    In the popup menu, click the **Disconnect** option.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify Disconnection">
    The cloud icon color will change, and the workspace status will update to **Stopped** with the button showing "Restart WhatsApp".

    ![Workspace Status Updated](/images/1203758090.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>
