## Overview

Send personalized WhatsApp campaigns to thousands of contacts using your CRM lists. This playbook covers everything from list creation to campaign analytics.

<Info>
**Setup Time**: 30 minutes  
**Difficulty**: Medium  
**Requires**: WhatsApp Business API (WABA)
</Info>

## The Problem

Email campaigns have 20% open rates. Your messages get lost in spam. Meanwhile, WhatsApp has:
- 98% open rate
- 45-60% response rate
- Messages read within minutes

But sending bulk WhatsApp manually is impossible.

## The Solution

Broadcast campaigns sent directly from your CRM:

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Warm up new numbers">
    New WABA numbers have sending limits. Start with 250/day, then scale up over 2 weeks.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Segment your audience">
    Don't blast everyone. Send relevant messages to targeted segments for better response rates.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Time your campaigns">
    Best times: 10am-12pm and 3pm-5pm local time. Avoid weekends for B2B.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Include opt-out">
    Always include "Reply STOP to opt out" to comply with WhatsApp policies.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Test before sending">
    Send test message to yourself and team before launching to full list.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Handling Responses

Broadcasts generate replies. Be prepared:

1. **Assign team members** to handle incoming responses
2. **Use Quick Replies** for common questions
3. **Set up AI suggestions** to help reps respond faster
4. **Track response times** to ensure quick follow-up

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Low delivery rate?">
    - Invalid phone numbers in list
    - Contacts haven't opted in
    - Number blocked by recipients
    - WABA quality rating dropped
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Template rejected?">
    Common reasons:
    - Promotional content in utility template
    - Missing opt-out option
    - Variable placeholders incorrect
    - Violates WhatsApp commerce policy
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Sending limit reached?">
    New numbers have limits. Wait 24 hours or request limit increase from Meta.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
