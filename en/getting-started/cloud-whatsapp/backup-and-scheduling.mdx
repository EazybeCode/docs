<Info>
**Yes!** With EazyBe Cloud WhatsApp, your messages will still be backed up and scheduled even if your **laptop is closed** or inactive.
</Info>

## How It Works

Your scheduled messages will be sent at the **right time**, regardless of whether your laptop is on or off.

The cloud automatically manages these tasks, ensuring seamless functionality without needing your device to be active.

  - **Automatic Backups**: Messages are backed up even when your laptop is closed
  - **Reliable Scheduling**: Scheduled messages are sent at the exact time you set
  - **No Device Dependency**: The cloud handles everything independently
