The User Profile section of the Google Sheet contact displays the basic contact details. To access and edit the user profile section, ensure that the contact has already been created.

## How to Access the User Profile

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the WhatsApp Chat">
    Open the WhatsApp chat of your Google Sheet contact.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access the Google Sheets Panel">
    Click on the **Google Sheet icon** in the right-side bar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View the User Profile">
    The User Profile section of the contact will appear, showing the basic contact details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit Profile Details">
    You can view, edit, and add new properties. Click **Save** to preserve the details.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  You can also view the contact directly in Google Sheets by clicking the **View in Sheets** button.
</Tip>
