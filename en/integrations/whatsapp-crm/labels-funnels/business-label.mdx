Organize your WhatsApp chats using business labels through the EazyBe Chrome Extension. Create, view, manage, and filter conversations by category, status, or priority.

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install Eazybe Extension">
    Download and install the **Eazybe Chrome Extension** from the Chrome Web Store if you haven't already.

    ![Eazybe Extension](/images/1448167169.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Settings">
    Open WhatsApp Web, navigate to the Eazybe sidebar at the bottom, and click **Settings**.

    ![Access Settings](/images/1448165535.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable Business Labels">
    Locate and activate the **"Show/Create Business Labels"** option to enable the feature.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Creating and Managing Labels

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create New Label">
    Click **"+ Create New Label"**, enter a name, assign a color, and save your new label.

    ![Create Label](/images/1448165803.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign Labels to Chats">
    Open a chat, click the **Label Icon** in the Eazybe toolbar, and select the appropriate labels for that conversation.

    ![Assign Labels](/images/1448166630.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Filter Chats by Label">
    Use the **Label Filter** to quickly find conversations by specific categories, status, or priority.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit or Delete Labels">
    Modify labels via the **edit icon** or remove them via the **delete icon** in the Labels section.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Label Organization Tips

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="By Status" icon="circle-check">
    Create labels like "New Lead", "In Progress", "Completed" to track conversation status.
  </Card>
  <Card title="By Priority" icon="flag">
    Use labels like "High Priority", "Medium", "Low" to prioritize your responses.
  </Card>
  <Card title="By Category" icon="folder">
    Organize by "Sales", "Support", "Billing" to route conversations to the right team.
  </Card>
  <Card title="By Source" icon="globe">
    Track where leads come from with labels like "Website", "Referral", "Ad Campaign".
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
  Color-code your labels to quickly identify conversation types at a glance in your chat list.
</Tip>
