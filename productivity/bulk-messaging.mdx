## Overview

Bulk messaging lets you send the same message to multiple contacts at once.

<Warning>
  **Important**: WhatsApp limits bulk messaging. For large broadcasts (100+ contacts), use [WABA Broadcasting](/waba/broadcasting/overview).
</Warning>

## Methods

### Method 1: Multi-Select in Revenue Inbox

1. Open Revenue Inbox
2. Select multiple conversations (checkboxes)
3. Click **Bulk Actions** → **Send Message**
4. Compose and send

### Method 2: Label-Based Send

1. Apply label to target contacts
2. Go to Labels → Select label
3. Click **Message All**
4. Compose and send

### Method 3: WABA Broadcasting

For large-scale campaigns:
- No contact limits
- Approved templates
- Analytics included

[Learn about WABA Broadcasting →](/waba/broadcasting/overview)

## Limits

| Method | Contacts | Risk Level |
|--------|----------|------------|
| Multi-select | Up to 50 | Low |
| Label-based | Up to 100 | Medium |
| WABA Broadcast | Unlimited | Safest |

## Best Practices

<Tip>
  Personalize bulk messages with variables to avoid looking like spam.
</Tip>

### Avoid Account Blocks

- Don't send identical messages to many contacts
- Space out sends over time
- Use WABA for large campaigns
- Monitor block/report rates
