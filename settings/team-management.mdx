## Overview

Add employees to your Eazybe organization to enable team collaboration, shared resources, and centralized monitoring through the Revenue Inbox.

## Inviting Team Members

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Organization Settings">
    Click the **Organization** button in the Eazybe sidebar, or go directly to [workspace.eazybe.com/organization](https://workspace.eazybe.com/organization).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Navigate to Employees">
    Click **Employees** in the left sidebar.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Employees">
    Click **+ Add Employees** button.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Details">
    For each employee, provide:
    - **Name** (required)
    - **Email** (required)
    - **WhatsApp Number** (required)
    - **Team** (optional)
    - **Role** (optional)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Send Invites">
    Click **Send Invites** to email invitations to all added employees.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What Happens After Invite

1. **Employee receives email** with invitation link
2. **Employee installs** Eazybe Chrome extension
3. **Employee connects** WhatsApp Web
4. **Employee joins** your organization using the invite code
5. **Chats start syncing** to Revenue Inbox

## Employee Onboarding

Share these steps with new team members:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Accept Invite">
    Click the link in the invitation email.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Install Extension">
    Download Eazybe from Chrome Web Store if not already installed.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Open WhatsApp Web">
    Go to web.whatsapp.com and scan QR code.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Organization Code">
    In Eazybe, click **Join Organization** and enter the code from the invite.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start Using">
    Access shared Quick Replies, Labels, and connect to CRM.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing Employees

### Viewing Employee Status

In the Dashboard, view each employee's:
- **Last Activity**: When they last used Eazybe
- **Last Chat Synced**: When their chats last synced
- **CRM Integration**: Which CRM they've connected

### Editing Employee Details

1. Go to **Employees** section
2. Click the employee's name
3. Click **Edit**
4. Update details
5. Save

### Removing Employees

1. Go to **Employees** section
2. Find the employee
3. Click **Remove** or **Delete**
4. Confirm removal

<Warning>
  Removing an employee stops their chat sync immediately. Previously synced data remains in your CRM.
</Warning>

## Teams

Organize employees into teams for better management:

### Creating a Team

1. Go to **Teams** in settings
2. Click **+ Create Team**
3. Enter team name (e.g., "Sales", "Support")
4. Add team members
5. Save

### Team Benefits

- **Shared Resources**: Quick Replies and Labels can be team-specific
- **Filtered Views**: View Revenue Inbox by team
- **Analytics**: See team-level performance metrics
- **Permissions**: Set different access levels per team

## Increasing Seats

Need more employee seats?

### From Workspace
1. Go to **Accounts** → **Billing**
2. Click **Add Seats** or **Upgrade Plan**
3. Select the number of additional seats
4. Complete payment

### From Extension
1. Click the **Upgrade Plan** button in Eazybe
2. Follow the prompts to add seats

## Data & Privacy

### Where is Employee Data Stored?

- **Chat backups**: Stored in Admin's Google Drive (not Eazybe servers)
- **CRM sync**: Stored in your connected CRM
- **No data on Eazybe servers**: We don't store message content

### Employee Privacy

- Employees know their chats are monitored (transparent policy)
- Admin can see chats synced to Revenue Inbox
- Personal WhatsApp conversations can be excluded via label settings

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What details are required to add an employee?">
    Name, Email, and WhatsApp Number are mandatory. Additional details like team and role are optional.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can employees access each other's chats?">
    No, regular employees can only see their own chats. Managers can see their team's chats. Admins can see all chats.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How do I verify invites were sent?">
    Check the employee status in the dashboard. You'll see "Invite Sent" until they accept and join.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What happens when an employee leaves the company?">
    Remove them from the organization. Their previously synced chats remain in your CRM for record-keeping.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can employees disconnect their CRM?">
    Employees can disconnect their personal CRM integration. Admins can enforce CRM connection policies.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
