Create and manage contacts in your Google Sheet directly from WhatsApp Web using EazyBe's Google Sheets integration.

## How to Create a Contact

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the WhatsApp Chat">
    Open the WhatsApp chat of the respective contact you want to save.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access the Google Sheets Panel">
    Click on the **Google Sheet icon** in the right-side bar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create the Contact">
    Click on the **Create Contact** button in the popup.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in Contact Details">
    Add the details of the contact in the given fields.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Custom Properties (Optional)">
    You can add a new property by clicking on the **+** icon below and following the process specified in the dialog box.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save the Contact">
    Click on the **Save** button below to save the contact details in your Google Sheet.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  You can directly view your contacts by clicking on the **View in Sheets** button after saving.
</Tip>
