## Key Capabilities

### Contact Sync
Automatically match WhatsApp contacts with CRM records using phone number matching.

### Chat Backup
Every WhatsApp conversation is backed up to your CRM timeline for full visibility.

### Mini-CRM View
Access and edit CRM data (contacts, deals, tasks) directly from WhatsApp without switching apps.

### Workflows
Trigger WhatsApp messages automatically when CRM events occur (deal stage change, new lead, etc.)
