## Data Controls

### Export Your Data

Download all your Eazybe data:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Data & Privacy**
2. Click **Export Data**
3. Choose what to include:
   - Account info
   - Team structure
   - Settings
   - Analytics
4. Click **Generate Export**
5. Download when ready

### Delete Account

Permanently delete your account:

<Warning>
  This action cannot be undone. Export your data first.
</Warning>

1. Go to **Settings** → **Data & Privacy**
2. Click **Delete Account**
3. Enter your password
4. Type "DELETE" to confirm
5. Click **Permanently Delete**

## Privacy Settings

### Chat Backup

Control what gets backed up:

| Setting | Options |
|---------|---------|
| **Backup location** | Your Google Drive |
| **Exclude labels** | Mark labels as "Don't Sync" |
| **Attachments** | Include or exclude media |

### CRM Sync

Control what syncs to CRM:

| Setting | Options |
|---------|---------|
| **Auto-create contacts** | On/Off |
| **Sync attachments** | On/Off |
| **Activity details** | Full or summary |

### AI Features

Control AI processing:

| Setting | Impact |
|---------|--------|
| **AI Suggestions** | On/Off |
| **Conversation Insights** | On/Off |
| **Priority Classification** | On/Off |

## GDPR Tools

### Right to Access

Request your data via export feature or email privacy@eazybe.com

### Right to Erasure

Delete your account to remove data from Eazybe servers.

### Right to Portability

Export data in standard formats (JSON, CSV).

## Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention |
|-----------|-----------|
| Chat backups | Indefinite (your Google Drive) |
| Account data | Until deletion |
| Analytics | 2 years |
| Logs | 90 days |
