title: 'Chat Backup to Bitrix24'
description: 'Log WhatsApp messages to Bitrix24'
---

<Info>
**Setup Time**: 5 minutes | **Difficulty**: Easy
</Info>

## Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable Backup">
    **Settings** → **Bitrix24** → Enable **Chat Backup**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure">
    Messages logged as activities
  </Step>
</Steps>
