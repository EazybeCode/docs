## Overview

Click to Chat (Start Chat with a Number) lets you instantly message any phone number on WhatsApp without adding them to your phone's contacts first. Perfect for quick one-time conversations or testing new leads.

## How to Use

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Start Chat">
    Click the **Start a new chat** icon in the Eazybe toolbar
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Phone Number">
    - Select the country code from the dropdown, OR
    - Enter the full number with country code
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Name (Optional)">
    Give the contact a name for easy reference
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start Chatting">
    Click **Start** to open the conversation
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
**Verify First:** Before cold messaging, ensure the number is opted-in to receive messages from businesses to avoid being reported as spam.
</Tip>

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I need to save the contact to message them?">
    No! That's the whole point of Click to Chat. You can message any WhatsApp number directly without saving it to your contacts.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Will the contact be permanently saved?">
    The contact won't be saved to your phone's contacts. However, the conversation will appear in your WhatsApp chat list until you delete it.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I add the number to my contacts later?">
    Yes, you can save the contact at any time through WhatsApp's native interface.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
