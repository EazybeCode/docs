There are two ways to disconnect cloud from the EazyBe WhatsApp Extension. The main drawback is that you will no longer be able to sync chats or schedule messages while your laptop is closed. 


- 
#### Disconnect via the EazyBe Workspace

If cloud is connected, you can disconnect it by clicking the **Stop Connection **button next to the account for which you want to disconnect Cloud. 


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1203746395.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


- Once disconnected, the status will change to **Stopped,** and the Cloud Connection button will change to **Restart WhatsApp**. 


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1203758350.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


- Additionally, the **cloud icon** in the extension will turn red, indicating that Cloud is no longer connected.


- 
#### Disconnect via the EazyBe extension
If your cloud is connected, you can also disconnect it via the extension. 


Click on the **cloud icon** in the EazyBe WhatsApp extension.


- In the popup that appears, click the **Disconnect** button.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1203747726.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


- Once disconnected, the cloud icon's color will change, and the status and cloud connection in front of the WhatsApp account in EazyBe workspace will change to **Stopped** and **Restart WhatsApp** respectively.
