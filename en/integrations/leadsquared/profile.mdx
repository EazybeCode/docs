---

The Profile section displays basic contact details and is the first section shown when creating a LeadSquared Lead through the EazyBe WhatsApp Extension.

## Viewing and Editing Lead Profile

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Lead's WhatsApp Chat">
    Access the WhatsApp conversation of the LeadSquared lead whose profile you want to view or edit.

    ![WhatsApp Lead Conversation](/images/1224273638.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access the Profile Section">
    Click the **LeadSquared icon** in the right sidebar to view the profile section under the Lead section.

    ![LeadSquared Profile Section](/images/1224273800.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Edit Lead Properties">
    View and modify user properties as needed. Click **"Apply"** to confirm your changes.

    <Info>
      For detailed instructions on editing lead properties, see the [Create and Customize Lead](/en/integrations/leadsquared/create-and-customize-lead) guide.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save Lead Details">
    Click **"Save"** to finalize all modifications to the lead profile.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  The Profile section provides quick access to essential lead information, allowing you to manage contacts efficiently without leaving WhatsApp.
</Tip>
