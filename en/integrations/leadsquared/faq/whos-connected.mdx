title: "Who's Connected"
description: "How to see who from your team has connected to LeadSquared"
---

As an organization admin, you can view which employees have connected their LeadSquared accounts to the EazyBe WhatsApp Extension through the EazyBe Workspace.

## View Connected Team Members

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to EazyBe Workspace">
    Access your admin account on the EazyBe Workspace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Dashboard">
    Go to the **Dashboard** section within the EazyBe Workspace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Integration Status">
    The dashboard provides complete information on your team's LeadSquared usage. Look for the **CRM Integrated** column in the table - the LeadSquared icon will be visible for team members who have connected their accounts.
  </Step>
</Steps>

![Team LeadSquared Integration Status](/images/1188147935.png)

<Info>
  Only organization admins have access to view the team's integration status. Regular team members cannot see other members' connection status.
</Info>

<Tip>
  Use this dashboard view to quickly identify which team members still need to connect their LeadSquared accounts and follow up with them to ensure full team adoption.
</Tip>
