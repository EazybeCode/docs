You should have the Zoho Lead created on the EazyBe WhatsApp extension. To edit a particular lead that has been created on Zoho:

- Open the WhatsApp chat of your respective Zoho Lead.
- Click on the edit button as given below in the contact card that opens up.
- You will see a pop-up window where you can edit or select the user properties as per your requirements. You can also re-arrange the property display order. You can select whether the added property is required or optional.
- Click on Apply and you can see the properties you have selected in the Contact card, edit the properties and click on Save.
