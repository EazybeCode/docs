If your WhatsApp conversations are not syncing with LeadSquared despite ongoing message exchanges, follow this troubleshooting guide to resolve the issue.

## Verify WhatsApp Chats Activity is Enabled

The most common cause of sync issues is the **WhatsApp Chats by EazyBe** activity type not being enabled in LeadSquared.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access a Lead Page">
    Navigate to any LeadSquared Lead page. You can do this by:
    - Clicking the **View Lead on LeadSquared** button in EazyBe
    - Or navigating directly in LeadSquared
  </Step>
  <Step title="Locate Activity History">
    On the Lead page, find the **Activity History** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Activity Type Dropdown">
    Click on the **Activity Type** dropdown menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable WhatsApp Chats">
    Verify that the checkbox for **WhatsApp Chats by EazyBe** is selected. If it's not checked, enable it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

![Activity Type Configuration](/images/1188126933.png)

<Warning>
  If the WhatsApp Chats by EazyBe option is not visible in the Activity Type dropdown, contact your LeadSquared administrator to ensure the activity type has been properly configured.
</Warning>

<Info>
  After enabling the activity type, your WhatsApp conversations should start syncing. Previously exchanged messages may take some time to appear in the activity history.
</Info>
