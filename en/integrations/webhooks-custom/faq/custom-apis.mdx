Yes! EazyBe is committed to supporting your unique integration needs. If you have a specific use case that requires a custom API, we'd love to hear from you.

## How to Request a Custom API

<Steps>
  <Step title="Describe Your Use Case">
    Clearly outline what you're trying to achieve and how an API would help your workflow.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Contact Our Team">
    Reach out to us with your requirements via email or through our support channels.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review & Development">
    Our team will review your request and work with you to develop a solution that fits your needs.
  </Step>
</Steps>
