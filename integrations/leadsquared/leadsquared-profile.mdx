The profile section of a LeadSquared Lead will display the basic contact details and is the first section that opens when a LeadSquared Lead is created via the EazyBe WhatsApp Extension. To access the Profile section


- 
#### Open the Lead's WhatsApp Chat
Open the WhatsApp chat of the **Leadsquared lead**.


- 
#### Access the Profile Section
Click on the **LeadSquared** icon in the right sidebar. The first section that appears under the **Lead section** is the **Profile section**.

</Frame>


- 
#### Edit Lead Properties
You can view and edit user properties for your lead. Once you've made your changes, click the **Apply** button. If you need more details on editing lead properties refer to Edit LeadSquared Lead Properties.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1224273800.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


- 
#### Save Lead Details
After completing your edits, click **Save** to finalize the changes.
