## How to Disconnect Your Zoho Integration

<Info>
This guide explains how to disconnect your Zoho CRM integration from Eazybe if you need to remove an incorrect account or completely unlink the integration.
</Info>

---

## Method 1: Via Eazybe WhatsApp Extension

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Integrations Panel">
    Click the **"Integrations"** icon in your Eazybe WhatsApp Web extension to access your connected CRM integrations.

    ![Integrations Icon](/images/1185347379.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Disconnect Zoho">
    Locate the **Zoho** section and click the **"Disconnect"** button to remove the integration.

    ![Disconnect Zoho Button](/images/1223635543.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm Disconnection">
    A confirmation popup will appear. Click **"Confirm"** to complete the disconnection process.

    ![Confirm Disconnection](/images/1223636790.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Method 2: Via Eazybe Workspace

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Organisation Settings">
    Click the **"Organisation"** button in the right sidebar of your Eazybe workspace.

    ![Organisation Button](/images/1190893424.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Navigate to Integrations">
    In the left sidebar, click on **"Integrations"** to view all connected CRM integrations.

    ![Integrations Section](/images/1190895253.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Unlink Zoho CRM">
    Locate the **Zoho CRM** integration section, click the **"Configure"** button, then click the red **"Unlink"** button. Confirm via the popup to complete the disconnection.

    ![Unlink Zoho CRM](/images/1190896448.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## What Happens After Disconnection

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Icon Removal" icon="circle-minus">
    The Zoho icon will disappear from the extension's right sidebar.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Reconnection" icon="rotate">
    You can reconnect anytime by following the initial setup procedure.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
