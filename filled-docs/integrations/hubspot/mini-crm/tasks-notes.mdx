## **Create HubSpot Tasks**- **Select the Contact
​**Open the WhatsApp chat of the contact for whom you want to create a task. Ensure the contact is linked to a HubSpot contact.

- **Access the Tasks Section
​**Click on the HubSpot icon in the right sidebar and select the **Tasks** section (second button on the top bar).

- **Add a Task
​**Click on the **Add Task** button to create a new task.

## **Create HubSpot Notes**- **Select the Contact
​**Open the WhatsApp chat of the contact for whom you want to create notes. Ensure the contact is linked to a HubSpot contact.

- **Access the Notes Section
​**Click on the HubSpot icon in the right sidebar and select the **Notes** section (third button on the top bar).

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1329213973.png" alt="" />
</Frame>

**3.  Add Notes
​**Write your follow-up notes in the provided text box and click **Save** to store them.
​

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1329220020.png" alt="" />
</Frame>

**4. View and Add More Notes
​**View your saved notes in the Notes section. You can add additional notes by clicking the **Add** button.
​

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1329220374.png" alt="" />
</Frame>

**5. Attach WhatsApp Messages (Optional)
​**Select specific messages to attach to your notes by clicking **Attach WhatsApp Messages**. Choose the starting and ending messages, delete unnecessary ones, and click **Continue** to finalize.
​

**6. Finalize and Save Notes
​**Add any additional details and click **Save** to complete your notes.
