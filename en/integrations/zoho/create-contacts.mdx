Create new Zoho Contacts directly from your WhatsApp conversations using the EazyBe extension. Seamlessly capture contact information without switching between applications.

## How to Create a Zoho Contact

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the WhatsApp Chat">
    Access the WhatsApp conversation with the person you want to add as a new contact in Zoho.

    ![Open WhatsApp Chat](/images/1217630187.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click on the Zoho Icon">
    In the right-side panel of the EazyBe extension, locate and click on the **Zoho icon** to access your CRM features.

    ![Click Zoho Icon](/images/1112999241.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access the Contact Section">
    Click on the **Contact** header within the Zoho popup window to open the contact creation form.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fill in the Contact Details">
    Complete all necessary fields with the contact's information.

    <Tip>
      Use the **Edit** button to customize user properties and add additional fields as needed.
    </Tip>

    ![Fill Contact Details](/images/1191106658.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create the Contact">
    Click the **Create Contact** button to finalize and save the new contact entry to Zoho.

    ![Create Contact](/images/1191106657.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Viewing Your Contact in Zoho

After creating the contact, you can easily access it in Zoho:

<Card title="View Contact on Zoho" icon="arrow-up-right-from-square">
  Click the **View Contact on Zoho** button in the contact's profile section to open the full contact record directly in Zoho CRM.
</Card>

<Info>
  The contact will be immediately available in your Zoho CRM with all the information you entered during creation.
</Info>
