**What is the purpose of joining an organization on Eazybe?**

*Joining an organization enables sharing of Quick Replies and Labels among team members, facilitating easy access and consistent messaging. It also allows collaboration on follow-ups and tasks visible to everyone.*

**How can I acquire the Organization Code to join an organization on Eazybe?**

*To obtain the Organization Code, seek assistance from your administrator. They can navigate to the My Account section and find the code there.*

**Is my data secure when I join an organization on Eazybe?**

*Eazybe does not store any data on its servers. All your data is backed up with your WhatsApp number and stored securely in your admin's Google Drive.*

**Where are my chats backed up when I join an organization?**

*Chats are backed up in your admin's Google Drive, and you can view them from the Team Inbox.*

**Can team members access and collaborate on shared resources like Quick Replies and Labels?**

*Yes, joining an organization allows team members to share Quick Replies and Labels, fostering consistent messaging and collaboration.*

**Can I access follow-ups and tasks created by other team members after joining an organization?**

*Yes, joining an organization enables visibility into follow-ups and tasks created by other team members, promoting transparency and collaboration.*
