EazyBe enables seamless navigation between Zoho CRM and WhatsApp messaging platforms. With single-click buttons, you can instantly jump between your CRM data and WhatsApp conversations without losing context.

## Go to Zoho Button

The **Go to Zoho** button provides quick access to your Zoho account directly from WhatsApp.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Locate the Go to Zoho Button">
    The button appears beside the contact or lead name at the top of the EazyBe panel, or within the Profile Section.

    ![Go to Zoho Button](/images/1223656037.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click to Navigate">
    Click the button to jump directly to the corresponding contact or lead page in Zoho CRM. This allows you to view full CRM details without manually searching for the record.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  This feature helps you quickly access detailed CRM information while managing WhatsApp conversations, improving your workflow efficiency.
</Info>

## Go Back to WhatsApp Button

EazyBe also provides a complementary button on Zoho that instantly returns you to the WhatsApp conversation.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the WhatsApp Button in Zoho">
    The **Go to WhatsApp** button displays consistently across:
    - Contact pages
    - Lead pages
    - Phone column in Leads, Accounts, and Contacts modules

    ![WhatsApp Button on Contact Page](/images/1192546385.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Return to WhatsApp">
    Click the button to instantly return to the WhatsApp conversation of the corresponding Zoho Lead or Contact.

    ![WhatsApp Button in Module View](/images/1192566656.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Use these navigation buttons to maintain context when switching between platforms. You can quickly check CRM details and return to the conversation without losing your place.
</Tip>
