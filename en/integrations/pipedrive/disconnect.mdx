## Overview

If you no longer need the Pipedrive integration or want to connect a different account, you can easily disconnect the integration from the Eazybe panel.

<Note>
Disconnecting the integration will stop syncing data between WhatsApp and Pipedrive. Existing data in Pipedrive will remain unchanged.
</Note>

---

## Disconnecting Pipedrive

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open WhatsApp Web with Eazybe Extension">
    Open WhatsApp Web and ensure the Eazybe extension is active.

  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Pipedrive">

---

## What Happens After Disconnecting

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Data Remains" icon="database">
    All contacts, notes, tasks, and deals created in Pipedrive during the integration will remain in your Pipedrive account.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Sync Stops" icon="arrows-rotate">
    New WhatsApp conversations will no longer sync to Pipedrive after disconnection.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

---

## Reconnecting

If you want to reconnect Pipedrive after disconnecting:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Follow Connection Steps">
    Follow the [Account Connection](/en/integrations/pipedrive/account-connection) guide to reconnect your Pipedrive account.
  </Step>
</Steps>
