## Timing & Frequency

### Optimal Send Times

| Audience | Best Times |
|----------|------------|
| B2C (India) | 10 AM - 12 PM, 7 PM - 9 PM |
| B2B | 10 AM - 11 AM, 2 PM - 4 PM |
| Global | Test and segment by timezone |

### Frequency Guidelines

| Type | Recommended Frequency |
|------|----------------------|
| Marketing promotions | 2-4 per month |
| Transactional updates | As needed |
| Newsletters | Weekly or bi-weekly |

<Warning>
Over-messaging leads to blocks and unsubscribes!
</Warning>

## Personalization

Use variables effectively:

| Variable | Impact |
|----------|--------|
| **Name** | +15% open rate |
| **Recent purchase** | +25% click rate |
| **Location** | +20% relevance |
| **Loyalty status** | +30% conversion |

## A/B Testing

Test these elements:
- Message length (short vs detailed)
- Emoji usage (with vs without)
- CTA wording
- Send time
- Button text

<Tip>
Split your list 50/50 and compare results. Apply learnings to future campaigns.
</Tip>

## Measuring Success

Track these KPIs:

| KPI | Target | Action if Low |
|-----|--------|---------------|
| Delivery Rate | >95% | Clean contact list |
| Read Rate | >70% | Improve subject/timing |
| Click Rate | >10% | Better CTA/offer |
| Response Rate | >5% | More engaging content |
| Unsubscribe Rate | <2% | Reduce frequency |
