## Prerequisites

Before starting any playbook:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Eazybe Connected">
    Chrome extension installed and connected to WhatsApp
  </Step>
  <Step title="HubSpot Connected">
    Eazybe connected to HubSpot via OAuth
  </Step>
  <Step title="Permissions">
    HubSpot user with appropriate permissions (varies by playbook)
  </Step>
</Steps>
