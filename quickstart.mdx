## Overview

This guide will help you set up Eazybe and start managing your team's WhatsApp conversations.

## Prerequisites

- Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge, or Brave browser
- WhatsApp Business account (personal WhatsApp also works)
- Google account (for chat backup)
- CRM account (optional but recommended)

## Step 1: Install Chrome Extension

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Chrome Web Store">
    Go to [Eazybe on Chrome Web Store](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/eazybe)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Add to Chrome">
    Click the **Add to Chrome** button
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Installation">
    Click **Add extension** in the popup
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
The extension also works on Microsoft Edge and Brave browsers.
</Info>

## Step 2: Connect WhatsApp

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open WhatsApp Web">
    Go to [web.whatsapp.com](https://web.whatsapp.com)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scan QR Code">
    Scan with your WhatsApp mobile app
  </Step>
  <Step title="Eazybe Activates">
    You'll see the Eazybe sidebar appear on the right
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 3: Create Your Workspace

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up">
    Click **Sign Up** in the Eazybe sidebar
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Workspace">
    Name your workspace (usually your company name)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Google Drive">
    Authorize Google Drive for automatic chat backup
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 4: Invite Your Team

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings">
    Navigate to **Settings** → **Team**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Invite">
    Click **+ Invite Team Member**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Details">
    Enter email and assign role (Admin, Manager, or Employee)
  </Step>
</Steps>

Each team member will need to:
1. Install the Chrome extension
2. Connect their WhatsApp
3. Join your workspace

## Step 5: Connect Your CRM (Optional)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Integrations">
    Navigate to **Settings** → **Integrations**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select CRM">
    Choose HubSpot, Salesforce, Zoho, or others
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize">
    Click Connect and authorize access
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What You'll See

After setup, your WhatsApp Web will have:

- ✅ **Eazybe Sidebar** - CRM data, labels, quick replies
- ✅ **Mini-CRM Panel** - View/edit contacts, deals, tasks
- ✅ **Smart Labels** - Organize conversations
- ✅ **Quick Replies** - Send saved templates instantly
