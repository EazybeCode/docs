Seamlessly integrate your Google Calendar with EazyBe to manage your schedule directly from your WhatsApp conversations.

## Accessing the Calendar

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Calendar">
    Click on the **Calendar icon** in the right-side bar to access the calendar feature.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Integration Option">
    You'll see two options:
    - **Authenticate with Google** - Connect your Google Calendar for full functionality
    - **Proceed without Google Calendar** - Use basic event creation features
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Using Calendar Without Google Integration

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Event">
    Click the **plus button** to create a new event.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Event Details">
    Select the **date and time** for your event and add a **description**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Event">
    Save your event to track it in the calendar.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Using Calendar With Google Integration

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign In to Google">
    Sign in using your Google account to connect your calendar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Notifications">
    A notification popup will appear (enabled by default). Configure your notification preferences.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage Events">
    With Google Calendar connected, you can:
    - **Add events** directly to your Google Calendar
    - **Add Google Meet links** to your events
    - **Share events** with WhatsApp contacts
    - **Add contacts** from Gmail and WhatsApp
  </Step>
</Steps>
