## Overview

Eazybe's AI Reply Suggestions help you craft better messages faster. Type a draft, and AI provides polished alternatives with different tones and styles.

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Type Message">
    Type your draft message (minimum 10 characters)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Rephrase">
    Click the **Rephrase** button (magic wand icon)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Option">
    Select from multiple tone variations
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send">
    Click to insert, edit if needed, then send
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Real Examples

### Sales Outreach

**Original**: "wanted to follow up on our call"

**AI Suggestions**:
- *Professional*: "I wanted to follow up on our recent conversation. Have you had a chance to review the proposal?"
- *Friendly*: "Hey! Just checking in about our chat. Any thoughts on what we discussed?"
- *Concise*: "Following up on our call - any questions?"

### Customer Support

**Original**: "sorry about that"

**AI Suggestions**:
- *Empathetic*: "I sincerely apologize for the inconvenience. Let me help resolve this for you right away."
- *Professional*: "We apologize for this issue. Here's how we'll make it right..."
- *Concise*: "Apologies for that. Here's the fix..."
