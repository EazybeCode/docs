## WhatsApp API Pricing

WhatsApp charges per conversation based on message category and destination country.

### Conversation Categories

| Category | Description | Cost |
|----------|-------------|------|
| **Marketing** | Promotions, offers, announcements | Higher |
| **Utility** | Order updates, alerts, confirmations | Medium |
| **Authentication** | OTP, verification codes | Lower |
| **Service** | Customer support responses | Free (first 1000/month) |

<Info>
Eazybe adds no markup to Meta's WhatsApp pricing. You pay exactly what Meta charges.
</Info>
