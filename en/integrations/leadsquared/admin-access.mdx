Administrators can access the EazyBe workspace to view and manage team details and analytics for the LeadSquared integration.

## Viewing Team Details

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the Organisation Page">
    Click the **Organisation** button in the right sidebar to open a new tab.

    ![Organisation Button](/images/1185334534.png)

    Log in using your admin credentials. The Organisation page will load after successful authentication.

    ![Organisation Page](/images/1221517523.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access the Dashboard">
    In the workspace, click the **Dashboard** button in the left sidebar.

    ![Dashboard Button](/images/1221518650.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check Team Details">
    View important team information including:
    - **Last activity** - When team members were last active
    - **Last chat synced time** - Most recent sync timestamp
    - **CRM integrations** - Connected integrations for all employees

    ![Team Details](/images/1182373085.png)

    Access the **Analytics** tab for comprehensive message analytics, either team-wide or for individual team members.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Only users with admin credentials can access the Organisation page and view team-wide analytics.
</Info>
