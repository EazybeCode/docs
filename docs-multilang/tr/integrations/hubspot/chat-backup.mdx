You can view the back up of your WhatsApp chats within HubSpot in several areas such as:

### HubSpot Contact Page:

Each synced WhatsApp conversation will appear under the **WhatsApp Activity** section of the HubSpot contact's timeline. You can view all message backups and interactions stored here.

</Frame>
### HubSpot Anlaşmalar Section:

If a contact has deals associated with them, the chats will appear in the latest deal on the **Anlaşmalar Page**.

  <img src="/images/1178986333.png" alt="" />
</Frame>
### HubSpot Company's Page:

The chats will also appear on the company's page in HubSpot.
