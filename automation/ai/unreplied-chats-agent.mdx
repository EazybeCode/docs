## Overview

The Unreplied Chats AI Agent automatically monitors your team's WhatsApp conversations, identifies which ones need immediate attention, and alerts your team based on urgency.

<Note>
  This feature helps sales and support teams focus on high-priority conversations without manually checking every chat.
</Note>

## How It Works

### Automatic Chat Categorization

The AI agent scans every unreplied chat and applies a priority label based on:

- **Keywords**: urgent, cancel, refund, help, ASAP, complaint
- **Customer Value**: High-value customers from your CRM
- **Time Sensitivity**: How long the message has been waiting
- **Sentiment Analysis**: Detecting frustration or urgency in tone

### Real-Time Status Updates

Team members see visual labels beside chats in:
- **Revenue Inbox** (Team Inbox)
- **Chrome Extension** chat list
- **Dashboard** overview

### Scheduled Admin Notifications

Designated administrators receive WhatsApp message summaries every three hours listing unreplied chats, grouped by criticality.
