- Open the WhatsApp chat of your Bitrix lead.


- Click on the Bitrix icon on the right-side bar.


- The second header will be of the activity section.


- Click on the Add activity button.


- Fill in the required fields. You can also add Notes in the activity section.


- Click on Save button and your activity will be created.
