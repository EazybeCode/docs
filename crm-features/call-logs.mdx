Let’s learn how to add Call Logs.


- 
Go to the contact for which you want to create a Call Log for.

- 
Click on the HubSpot icon. The fifth button in the top bar will take you to the Call Logs section.

 

- 
Click on the New Call Logs button.

- 
You’ll see a list of fields.

 

- 
Fill the Call Outcome – Select suitable option from the list provided.

- 
Similarly, select Direction, Date & Time and Describe the Call in the text-box provided.

 

- 
Click on the Save Call Log button and your call log will be created.
