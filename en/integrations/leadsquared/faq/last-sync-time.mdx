Administrators can monitor the most recent synchronization timestamp for their team through the EazyBe Workspace platform.

## Checking Last Sync Time

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log into EazyBe Workspace">
    Access the EazyBe Workspace using your admin credentials at [workspace.eazybe.com](https://workspace.eazybe.com/).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Dashboard">
    Locate and select the **Dashboard** button from the main menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Sync Status">
    Look for the **Last Chat Synced** column in the team overview table. This displays synchronization timestamps for all team members' chats.
  </Step>
</Steps>

![EazyBe Workspace Dashboard](/images/1221525291.png)

![Last Chat Synced Column](/images/1216414775.png)

<Info>
  The **Last Chat Synced** column shows when each team member's WhatsApp chats were last synchronized with LeadSquared. This helps you monitor sync activity across your entire team.
</Info>

<Tip>
  If you notice that a team member's sync time is outdated, check if they have the EazyBe extension active and their WhatsApp Web is connected.
</Tip>
