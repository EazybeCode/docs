## How to Join an Organization

<Steps>
  <Step title="Obtain the Organization Code">
    Request the Organization Code from your administrator. The admin can find this code in the **My Account** section.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Join Organization">
    Click the **Join another Organization** button in your account settings.

  <Step title="Link to the Organization">

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is my data secure?">
    Yes. Eazybe does not store any data on its servers. All chat backups are stored securely in the admin's Google Drive.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Where are chat backups stored?">
    Chats are backed up in the admin's Google Drive and are accessible via the Team Inbox feature.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What can I share with my team?">
    Once you join an organization, you can share Quick Replies and Labels with team members, promoting consistent messaging across the organization.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
