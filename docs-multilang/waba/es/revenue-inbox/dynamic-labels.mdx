## Overview

Dynamic Labels automatically sync categorization between Eazybe and your connected CRM, ensuring consistent organization across both platforms.

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="CRM Integration Active">
    Connect your CRM (HubSpot, Zoho, Salesforce, etc.) to Eazybe
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Field Mapping">
    Map CRM fields to Eazybe labels (e.g., Lifecycle Stage â†’ Label)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Automatic Sync">
    When CRM field changes, WhatsApp label updates automatically
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Bi-directional">
    Changes in Eazybe also sync back to CRM
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Supported CRM Fields

### HubSpot

| HubSpot Field | Eazybe Label |
|---------------|--------------|
| Lifecycle Stage | Lead, Customer, etc. |
| Lead Status | New, Open, In Progress |
| Deal Stage | Proposal, Negotiation, Won |
| Contact Owner | Assigned team member |

### Zoho CRM

| Zoho Field | Eazybe Label |
|------------|--------------|
| Lead Status | Various stages |
| Lead Source | Where they came from |
| Lead Owner | Assigned rep |
