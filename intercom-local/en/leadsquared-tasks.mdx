Do you have a pending task, you want to save it so that you can track it anytime you want. Don’t worry, The Task Section of the EazyBe’s Leadsquared’s Integration comes to your rescue

- 
#### Open the Lead's WhatsApp Chat
Open the WhatsApp chat of the Leadsquared lead for whom you want to create a task.

- 
#### Access the Tasks Section
Click on the **LeadSquared** icon in the right sidebar. The third header under the **Lead section** is the **Tasks section**, Click on it and access the tasks.

- 
#### Add a New Task
Click on the **Add Task** button

  

- 
#### Save the Created Task
Enter the necessary details for the task, ensuring that the end date is not before the deadline date. Click **Save** to create the task.

  

- 
#### Edit or Delete a Task
Once the task is created, you can **Edit** or **Delete** it by clicking on the three dots as given below.

- 
#### Create Additional Tasks
You can create more tasks for your LeadSquared lead by clicking the Add Task button again and repeating the process.

  

**Note**: The tasks you create in the EazyBe WhatsApp extension will also appear in the **Tasks** section of LeadSquared, linked to the specific lead.
