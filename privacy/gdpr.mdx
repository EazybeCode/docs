## GDPR Overview

Eazybe is fully GDPR compliant for EU customers. We act as a data processor for your organization's WhatsApp communications.

## Your Rights Under GDPR

| Right | Description | How to Exercise |
|-------|-------------|-----------------|
| **Right to Access** | Request a copy of your data | Email [privacy@eazybe.com](mailto:privacy@eazybe.com) |
| **Right to Erasure** | Request deletion of your data | Delete account in settings or email us |
| **Right to Portability** | Export your data | Use workspace export feature |
| **Right to Rectification** | Correct inaccurate data | Update in account settings |
| **Right to Object** | Object to specific processing | Disable features in settings |
| **Right to Restriction** | Limit how data is used | Contact us with specific requests |

## Data Processing Agreement (DPA)

For business customers requiring a formal DPA:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Request DPA">
    Email [dpa@eazybe.com](mailto:dpa@eazybe.com) with your organization name
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive Agreement">
    We'll send a pre-signed DPA within 2 business days
  </Step>
  <Step title="Countersign">
    Sign and return for your records
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach:

1. **Detection**: Immediate investigation begins
2. **Assessment**: Impact analysis within 24 hours
3. **Notification**: Affected customers notified within 72 hours
4. **Reporting**: Supervisory authority notified if required
5. **Remediation**: Full incident report and prevention measures

## Cross-Border Transfers

For data transfers outside the EU:
- Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) in place
- Adequacy decisions respected
- Privacy Shield alternatives implemented
