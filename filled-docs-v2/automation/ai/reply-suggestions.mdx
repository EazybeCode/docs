---
title: "Reply Suggestions"
description: "AI Features - reply-suggestions"
---

Eazybe has a built-in AI suggestion to seamlessly message your clients. To use this feature,

- Open the WhatsApp chat of any contact.
​
- Ensure that the AI suggestions button is switched on.
​
- Type in your text, you should type at least 10 characters.
​
- Click on rephrase and you’ll see text suggestions given by AI.
​
- You may send the message and in case you want more suggestions, click on the rephrase button again.
