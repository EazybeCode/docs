The profile section of the Salesforce Lead will show the basic contact details. To access and edit the profile section, you should ensure that the Lead is created.

- Open the WhatsApp chat of your Salesforce lead.
- Click on the Salesforce icon on the right-side bar.
- The first header that opens up under the Lead section will be the profile one

  

- You can view and edit user properties for your contact

  

- You can save the changes by clicking on the Save button below.
