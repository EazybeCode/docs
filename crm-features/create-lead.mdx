How to create a new lead on Zoho


- Open the WhatsApp chat of the respective contact.


- Click on the Zoho icon in the right side-bar.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1112651537.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


- The New Lead Section will open up.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1112651863.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


- Click on the Create Lead button at the bottom and your lead will be created.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1112656078.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


- You can directly view the Lead in Zoho by clicking on the View Lead on Zoho in profile section of the lead as given below.
