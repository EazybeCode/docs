Manage your WhatsApp chats and labels efficiently by syncing them to the Team Inbox for collaborative access.

## Setting Up Backup and Sync

<Steps>
  <Step title="Activate Backup and Sync">
    Click the **Backup and Sync** button located in the right sidebar to begin the setup process.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Labels to Sync">
    Choose which labels you want to synchronize:
    - Select **specific labels** for targeted synchronization
    - Or choose **all labels** to sync everything to the Team Inbox
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Your Selections">
    Click **Save** to apply your synchronization settings. You can optionally view synced chats directly in the Team Inbox by opening a new tab.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Accessing the Team Inbox

There are multiple ways to access the Team Inbox:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Via Organisation Button">
    Click the **Organisation button** in the right sidebar to access the EazyBe workspace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Team Inbox">
    Within the EazyBe workspace, select **Team Inbox** from the left sidebar to view all synchronized WhatsApp conversations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Alternative: Quick Access">
    Click the **Team Inbox button** directly in the right sidebar to open it in a new tab within the EazyBe workspace.
  </Step>
</Steps>
