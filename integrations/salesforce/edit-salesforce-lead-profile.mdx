To edit a Lead that is connected to a Salesforce Lead through the EazyBe WhatsApp Extension:


- 
#### Open the WhatsApp Chat
Open the WhatsApp chat of the respective Salesforce Lead.


- 
#### Click on the Edit button
Click on the edit button in the Contact Card of the **Profile Section** that appears.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1223672830.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


- 
#### Edit Lead Details
A pop-up window will open, allowing you to:


**Edit or select user properties** as required.


- **Specify whether each selected property is required or optional**.


- 
#### Save the Changes
Click on Apply to update the Contact card with the properties that you have selected. Edit the properties field as and where required and click on Save to finalize your changes.
