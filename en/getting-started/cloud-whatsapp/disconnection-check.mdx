---
title: "Disconnection Check"
description: "How to verify that your EazyBe Cloud WhatsApp disconnection was successful"
---

There are two ways to verify that your Cloud WhatsApp has been successfully disconnected:

## Method 1: Check the Cloud Icon

The cloud icon in the EazyBe extension will turn **red** when disconnection is successful.

<Tip>
A red cloud icon confirms your Cloud WhatsApp is no longer connected.
</Tip>

---

## Method 2: Check Your Workspace

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open EazyBe Workspace">
    Navigate to your EazyBe Workspace dashboard.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check Status Indicator">
    The status indicator will show **Stopped**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify Button Text">
    The Cloud Connection button will display **Restart WhatsApp**.

    ![Disconnection Status in Workspace](/images/1203770785.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>
