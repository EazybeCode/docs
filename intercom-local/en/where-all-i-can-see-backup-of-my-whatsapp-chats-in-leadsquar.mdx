You can view the back up of your WhatsApp Chats with your LeadSquared Leads in:

- 
#### Leads Page
The chats are backup in the Leads page of the respective LeadSquared Lead.

  

- 
#### Leads Opportunities Page
The chats are also backed up in the Opportunities page of the LeadSquared Lead.
