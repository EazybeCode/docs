EazyBe is committed to GDPR compliance and protecting your data when using the LeadSquared integration.

## Data Protection Commitment

<Info>
  EazyBe follows strict data protection guidelines to ensure your WhatsApp conversations and LeadSquared data remain secure and compliant with privacy regulations.
</Info>

## Data Processing Agreement

If you require a Data Processing Agreement (DPA) for your organization, please raise a request at **hey@eazybe.com**.

<Tip>
  A DPA is typically required for organizations that need to comply with GDPR or other data protection regulations when using third-party services that process personal data.
</Tip>

## Key Privacy Features

- **Secure Data Transfer**: All data between WhatsApp, EazyBe, and LeadSquared is encrypted in transit
- **Access Controls**: Only authorized team members can access synced conversation data
- **Data Minimization**: Only necessary data is collected and processed for the integration to function
