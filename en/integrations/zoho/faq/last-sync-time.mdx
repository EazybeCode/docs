title: "Last Sync Time"
description: "Learn how to check the last synchronization time for your team's Zoho integration"
---

As an administrator, you can monitor your team's latest sync activity by accessing the EazyBe Workspace dashboard, which displays synchronization timestamps for all connected employees.

## How to Check Last Sync Time

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign into EazyBe Workspace">
    Log into your EazyBe Workspace using your administrator credentials.

    ![EazyBe Workspace Login](/images/1192712762.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Navigate to the Dashboard">
    Once logged in, navigate to the **Dashboard** section from the main menu.
  </Step>

  <Step title="View Last Chat Synced">
    Locate the **"Last Chat Synced"** column in the dashboard to view synchronization times for all team members.

    ![Dashboard Last Chat Synced Column](/images/1192718667.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  The "Last Chat Synced" column shows the timestamp of the most recent WhatsApp chat synchronization for each team member connected to Zoho.
</Info>

<Tip>
  Regularly monitoring sync times helps ensure all team members are properly connected and their chat data is being backed up to Zoho CRM.
</Tip>
