Once, you've successfully connected HubSpot with the EazyBe WhatsApp extension, you can access the HubSpot Mini-CRM view by clicking on the HubSpot icon in the right sidebar of the extension.

Once connected, the icon will appear and you can perform various functions, like:

#### 
1. [Create HubSpot Contact](https://help.eazybe.com/en/articles/9779696-create-hubspot-contact)

#### 2. [Edit HubSpot Contact](https://help.eazybe.com/en/articles/9779755-edit-hubspot-contact)

#### 3. [Create HubSpot Tasks](https://help.eazybe.com/en/articles/9779758-create-hubspot-tasks)

#### 4. [Create HubSpot Notes](https://help.eazybe.com/en/articles/9779766-create-hubspot-notes)

#### 5. [Create HubSpot Deals](https://help.eazybe.com/en/articles/9779840-create-hubspot-deals)

#### 6. [Manage HubSpot Deals](https://help.eazybe.com/en/articles/9854525-manage-hubspot-deals)

#### 7. [Create HubSpot Call Logs](https://help.eazybe.com/en/articles/9779779-create-hubspot-call-logs)

#### 8. [Create HubSpot Tickets](https://help.eazybe.com/en/articles/9779839-create-hubspot-tickets)
