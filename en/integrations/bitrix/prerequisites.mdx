Before integrating Bitrix24 with the Eazybe WhatsApp extension, ensure you meet the following requirements.

## Requirements

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Active Bitrix Account" icon="building">
    Ensure that your Bitrix24 account is set up and functioning properly.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Eazybe Extension" icon="puzzle-piece">
    Ensure that your Eazybe WhatsApp Extension is properly connected to WhatsApp Web.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Permissions" icon="key">
    Ensure you have the necessary permissions to connect Bitrix24 with WhatsApp.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Integration Overview

To integrate Bitrix24 with Eazybe, ensure that the extension is linked to your Bitrix24 account.

![Bitrix Integration](/images/1224289849.png)

<Note>
  The necessary permissions will be prompted during the integration process. Make sure to accept all required permissions for seamless functionality.
</Note>
