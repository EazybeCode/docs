Create new contacts in Bitrix or link existing ones directly from your WhatsApp conversations using the Eazybe extension's Mini CRM view.

## Creating or Linking a Bitrix Contact

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the WhatsApp Chat">
    Open the WhatsApp chat of the respective contact you want to create or link in Bitrix.

    ![Open WhatsApp chat](/images/1224312745.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click on the Bitrix Icon">
    Click on the **Bitrix** icon in the right sidebar of the extension.

    ![Bitrix icon in sidebar](/images/1224332367.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Link to an Existing Contact">
    If the contact already exists in Bitrix, click on the **Link to Existing Contact** option and select the contact from the dropdown.

    ![Link existing contact](/images/1224335845.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a New Contact">
    If the contact is new and hasn't been created in Bitrix yet, click on the **Create Contact** button.

    ![Create new contact button](/images/1224417577.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in the Contact Details">
    Enter the required details in the fields provided.

    ![Contact details form](/images/1224419844.png)

    <Tip>
    You can customize the fields by clicking on the **Edit** button and selecting the desired user properties.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Contact on Bitrix">
    Once the contact is created or linked, you can view it directly in Bitrix by clicking on the **View on Bitrix** button under the Profile section.
  </Step>
</Steps>
