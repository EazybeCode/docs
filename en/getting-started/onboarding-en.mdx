## Introduction

This guide will walk you through the entire Eazybe setup process, from creating your account and verifying your email to establishing your organization.

**This guide covers:**
- Creating and verifying your account
- Setting up your workspace
- Chrome Extension installation (optional)
- WhatsApp API connection (optional)
- CRM connection (optional)
- Understanding your free trial
- Platform navigation overview

---

## Getting Started

<Note>
Start your journey at: [app.eazybe.com/signin](https://app.eazybe.com/signin)
</Note>

---

## Account Creation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the Sign-in Page">
    Navigate to [app.eazybe.com/signin](https://app.eazybe.com/signin) to begin your onboarding journey.

    ![Sign-in Page](/images/onboarding-image1.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Your Email">
    Enter your email address or sign in with Google. Then click on **Get Started**.

    ![Email Entry](/images/onboarding-image2.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify Your Email">
    Enter the verification code you received on your email, then click on **Verify**.

    ![Email Verification](/images/onboarding-image3.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Set Up Your Workspace

<Steps>
  <Step title="Join or Create an Organization">
    You can join your existing organization using the **organization code** or create a new organization. Then click on **Continue**.

    ![Organization Setup](/images/onboarding-image4.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create New Organization">
    To create a new organization, enter your organization name, upload an organization logo (optional), and then click on **Create Organization**.

    ![Create Organization](/images/onboarding-image5.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>
