In this guide, let us learn how to integrate LeadSquared with the EazyBe extension.

Before we begin, ensure that you have the latest version of our WhatsApp Extension installed and running on your device, and ensure that your Leadsquared account is active

- Once you've logged into your WhatsApp web account you will see the **Integrations **button on the right-side bar.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1187950382.png" alt="" />
</Frame>

- Click on the button, and a popup window will appear.

- In the popup, click on the **Connect** button under the **LeadSquared section.**

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1224209473.png" alt="" />
</Frame>

- A **Connect to LeadSquared** modal will appear, asking for the **Host Name**, **Access Key** and **Secret key** of your LeadSquared account.

**Note: **You can find these details by navigating to the **Settings** in your LeadSquared UI** **

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1138799386.png" alt="" />
</Frame>

**Step 2: **Click on **API and Webhooks**

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1138799394.png" alt="" />
</Frame>

**Step 3: **Copy the **API Host** to get the **Host Name** for your LeadSquared account.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1183716346.png" alt="" />
</Frame>

**Step 4: **Click on **Secret Key** and you will find **Access ID** and **Secret Key**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1138799405.png" alt="" />
</Frame>

- Congratulations, you have successfully integrated LeadSquared with the EazyBe extension.
