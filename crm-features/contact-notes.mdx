Do you want to note down the Notes of the conversation with your Contact? If yes, we have got you covered. 


- Open the WhatsApp chat of your Zoho Contact.


- Click on the Zoho icon on the right-side bar.


- The second header under the Contact section will be the Notes one.


- Click on the New Note button.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1113034097.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


- Fill in the details and click on the Save Note button.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1113034349.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


- The Note will be created and you can see, edit or delete the created Note.
