## Introdução

WhatsApp Business Etiquetas help you organize your chats by categorizing them based on their status, priority, or any other classification you need. With the Eazybe Chrome Extension, you can easily create, view, and manage business labels within WhatsApp Web, improving your workflow efficiency
​

## Enabling Business Etiquetas no Eazybe

Follow these steps to access and use WhatsApp Business Etiquetas using the Eazybe Chrome Extension:
​

## Step 1: Install the Eazybe Chrome Extension

Ensure that you have installed the [Eazybe Chrome Extension](https://eazybe.com) on your browser. If not, download and install it from the Chrome Web Store.
​

## Step 2: Open Eazybe Settings

- Open WhatsApp Web in your browser.
- Navigate to the bottom of the Eazybe sidebar.
- Click on **Settings**.

## Step 3: Enable Business Etiquetas

- In the Settings menu, find the option **"Show/Create Business Etiquetas"**.
- Click to enable this option.

Once enabled, you can:

- **View Existing Etiquetas**: All existing labels created via WhatsApp Business will be visible within the chat interface.

**Create New Etiquetas**:

Click on the **"+ Create New Label"** option.

​
- **Assign Etiquetas to Chats**:

Open any WhatsApp chat.
Click on the **Label Icon** in the Eazybe toolbar.

  <img src="/images/1448166630.png" alt="" />

- Select the appropriate label(s) to categorize the chat.
​
- **Filter Chats by Etiquetas**:

Use the **Label Filter** to quickly find and manage conversations based on specific labels.

## Step 5: Editing or Deleting Etiquetas

- To edit an existing label, navigate to the Etiquetas section and click on the edit icon next to the label.
- To delete a label, click on the delete icon. (Note: This will remove the label from all associated chats.)

## Benefits of Using Etiquetas with Eazybe

- **Better Organization**: Easily manage conversations by categorizing them based on customer status, follow-ups, or priority levels.
- **Quick Access**: Instantly filter and find chats related to specific labels.
- **Seamless Workflow**: Improve productivity by keeping track of customer interactions more effectively.
