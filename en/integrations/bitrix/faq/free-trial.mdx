## Trial Availability

Yes, we offer a free trial for new users to test the Bitrix integration!

<Card title="Free Trial Period" icon="clock">
  **Initial Trial:** 4 days free to explore all features

  **Bonus Extension:** Get an additional 4 days by rating us
</Card>

## How It Works

1. **Sign up** for EazyBe and connect your Bitrix account
2. **Enjoy 4 days** of full access to all integration features
3. **Rate us** to extend your trial by another 4 days
4. **Total:** Up to 8 days of free trial access

<Tip>
Use the trial period to test all features including contact syncing, chat backup, and CRM integration to ensure EazyBe meets your needs.
</Tip>

## What's Included in the Trial

During your free trial, you get access to:

- Full Bitrix integration capabilities
- WhatsApp chat synchronization
- Contact and lead management
- Activity logging and timeline updates
- All Mini CRM features

<Note>
No credit card is required to start your free trial. You can upgrade to a paid plan anytime during or after the trial period.
</Note>
