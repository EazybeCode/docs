---
title: "Where can I check Last sync time for my team using Salesforce Integration?"
description: ""
---

Admin can check the last sync time for their team on [EazyBe workspace](https://workspace.eazybe.com/).

- Admin should log in to the EazyBe Workspace using their credentials.
- Click on the **Dashboard** button.

  

- In the **Last Chat Synced** column, you will see the last sync time of the Salesforce chats for all your employees.
