## Overview

This guide will help you set up Eazybe and start managing your team's WhatsApp conversations.

## Prerequisites

- WhatsApp Business account (personal WhatsApp also works)
- Google account (for chat backup)
- CRM account (optional but recommended)

## Step 1: Create Your Workspace

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up">
    Go to [eazybe.com](https://eazybe.com) and create your account
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Workspace">
    Name your workspace (usually your company name)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Up Google Drive">
    Connect Google Drive for automatic chat backup
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 2: Connect WhatsApp

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open WhatsApp Web">
    Eazybe works alongside WhatsApp Web
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scan QR Code">
    Scan with your WhatsApp mobile app
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Connection">
    You'll see "Connected" status in Eazybe
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
Your existing WhatsApp conversations will start syncing automatically.
</Info>

## Step 3: Invite Your Team

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings">
    Navigate to **Settings** → **Team**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Invite">
    Click **+ Invite Team Member**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Details">
    Enter email and assign role (Admin, Manager, or Employee)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 4: Connect Your CRM (Optional)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Integrations">
    Navigate to **Settings** → **Integrations**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select CRM">
    Choose HubSpot, Salesforce, Zoho, or others
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize">
    Click Connect and authorize access
  </Step>
</Steps>
