<Note>
Ensure the WhatsApp contact is already created in HubSpot before creating a deal for them.

## Creating HubSpot Deals

Follow these steps to create a new deal for your HubSpot contacts directly from WhatsApp:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose Your Contact">
    Open the WhatsApp chat of the contact for whom you want to create a deal. Make sure the contact is already created in HubSpot.

    <img src="/images/1112636242.png" alt="Select WhatsApp Contact" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Navigate to Deals Section">
    Click on the **HubSpot icon** in the right sidebar. The fourth button in the top bar will take you to the **Deals** section.

    <img src="/images/1112635692.png" alt="Navigate to Deals Section" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Initiate New Deal">
    Click on the **New Deal** button to start creating a deal for this contact.

    <img src="/images/1112635878.png" alt="New Deal Button" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Customize Deal Properties">
    Select and customize additional properties for your deal:
    - Deal name
    - Pipeline selection
    - Deal stage
    - Deal amount
    - Close date
    - Custom properties

    Reorder properties as needed for your workflow, then click **Apply**.

    <img src="/images/1112636418.png" alt="Customize Deal Properties" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete Deal Details">
    Fill in all necessary fields with the relevant information, then click **Save Deal** to create the deal in HubSpot.

    <img src="/images/1330527736.png" alt="Save Deal" />
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Check>
The deal is now created and automatically linked to the contact in your HubSpot CRM.
</Check>

## Managing Existing Deals

Once deals are created, you can easily view and edit them from the Mini-CRM panel:

### Viewing Deals

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="View in HubSpot" icon="arrow-up-right-from-square">
    Click the redirect button next to the deal name to open it directly in HubSpot for detailed viewing.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Quick Overview" icon="eye">
    See deal stage, amount, and close date at a glance in the Mini-CRM panel.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Editing Deals

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Edit Mode">
    Click the **Edit** icon next to the deal you want to modify.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Update Properties">
    Modify deal properties as needed:
    - Change the pipeline
    - Update the deal stage
    - Adjust the deal amount
    - Modify the close date
    - Update custom properties
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click **Save** to update the deal in HubSpot.
  </Step>
</Steps>
