## Backup & Sync

Eazybe automatically backs up WhatsApp conversations to your Team Inbox and connected CRM.

### Extension Backup

For Chrome Extension users:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Backup & Sync">
    In the Eazybe sidebar, click the **Backup and Sync** button
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Labels">
    Choose which labels to sync to Team Inbox:
    - Select specific labels, or
    - Select "All labels"
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Settings">
    Click **Save** to apply
  </Step>
  <Step title="View in Team Inbox">
    Click **View chats in Team Inbox** to open the workspace
  </Step>
</Steps>

### What Gets Backed Up

| Content | Backed Up |
|---------|-----------|
| Text messages | ✅ Yes |
| Images | ✅ Yes |
| Documents | ✅ Yes |
| Voice notes | ✅ Yes |
| Contact cards | ✅ Yes |
| Location | ✅ Yes |

### CRM Backup

Chats are also backed up to your connected CRM:

| CRM | Backup Location |
|-----|-----------------|
| HubSpot | Contact Activity Timeline |
| Zoho | Notes / Activity |
| Salesforce | Activity History |
| LeadSquared | Activity History |
| Bitrix24 | Timeline |

### Cloud Backup

For 24/7 backup even when laptop is closed:

1. Subscribe to **Teams Plan**
2. Go to Workspace → Organization
3. Click **Start Connection** for Cloud
4. Scan QR code with WhatsApp mobile app
5. Cloud icon turns green when connected

<Info>
Cloud backup syncs chats every 3 minutes, even when your laptop is closed.
</Info>

### Sync Settings by Label

Control which chats sync to CRM:

1. Click **Backup and Sync** in sidebar
2. Click on your CRM (e.g., HubSpot)
3. For labels you don't want synced, click **Don't Sync**
4. To re-enable, click **Sync**

<Warning>
Initial sync includes last 3 days of chat history. Future messages sync every 3 minutes.
</Warning>
