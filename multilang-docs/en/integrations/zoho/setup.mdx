This guide will walk you through the required steps to connect the EazyBe WhatsApp Extension with ZOHO and granting the necessary permissions for integration.

- ### Open the EazyBe WhatsApp Extension

As a first step, click on the **Integrations **button on your EazyBe WhatsApp web extension.

<Frame>
<img src="/images/1112649892.png" alt="" />
</Frame>
- ### Connect to ZOHO

Click on the **Connect button** below ZOHO integration. This button will redirect you to your ZOHO account.

<Frame>
<img src="/images/1112650071.png" alt="" />
</Frame>
- ### Select your ZOHO Account

Choose the account that you wish to connect with the EazyBe extension.

<Frame>
<img src="/images/1190703200.png" alt="" />
</Frame>
- ### Grant Necessary Permissions

When prompted, click on the Accept button to grant the necessary permissions required by EazyBe for a seamless integration of your ZOHO account with WhatsApp. These permissions will allow you to view and sync your WhatsApp chats with ZOHO.

<Frame>
<img src="/images/1190704611.png" alt="" />
</Frame>
- ### Successful Integration

Congratulations! You have successfully connected your EazyBe WhatsApp Extension with ZOHO.
