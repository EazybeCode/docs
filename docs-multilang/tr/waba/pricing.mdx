- **Message category** (Marketing, Utility, Authentication)
- **Recipient's country** (based on phone number country code)

## Free Messages

Not all messages cost money! These are **FREE**:

| Scenario | Cost |
|----------|------|
| Customer messages you first | **Free** |
| You reply within 24 hours (any message type) | **Free** |
| Utility template within active service window | **Free** |

<Info>
**24-Hour Service Window:** When a customer messages you, a 24-hour window opens. During this time, all your replies (including utility templates) are free. The window resets with each new customer message.
</Info>
