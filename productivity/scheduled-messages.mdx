## Overview

Schedule messages to be sent automatically at a specific date and time. Perfect for follow-ups, reminders, and time-zone-optimized outreach.

## Scheduling a Message

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Chat">
    Navigate to the WhatsApp conversation where you want to schedule a message.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click Schedule">
    Click the **Schedule** icon (clock) in the Eazybe toolbar above the message input.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Compose Your Message">
    Type your message in the text field. You can also:
    - Select from Quick Replies
    - Add an attachment (max 20MB, one file)
    - Record a voice note
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Date and Time">
    - Select the **date** from the calendar
    - Choose the **time** for delivery
    - Timezone is based on your system settings
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Optional: Save to Quick Replies">
    Check the box to save this message as a Quick Reply for future use.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Schedule">
    Click **Schedule** to confirm. The message will be sent automatically at the specified time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Recurring Messages

Send the same message on a regular schedule:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Scheduled Message">
    Follow steps 1-4 above.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enable Recurring">
    Toggle on **Set as Recurring Message**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Frequency">
    Choose how often to send:
    - Daily
    - Weekly (select days)
    - Monthly (select date)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set End Date (Optional)">
    Choose when to stop recurring messages, or leave open-ended.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Recurring messages are great for weekly check-ins, monthly reminders, or daily team updates.
</Tip>

## Managing Scheduled Messages

### Viewing Scheduled Messages

1. Click the **Schedule** icon in the Eazybe sidebar
2. View all pending scheduled messages
3. Filter by contact or date

### Editing a Scheduled Message

1. Find the message in the scheduled list
2. Click **Edit**
3. Modify the message, time, or date
4. Click **Save**

### Sending Immediately

Changed your mind? Send a scheduled message right now:

1. Find the message in the scheduled list
2. Click **Send Now**
3. Message is sent immediately and removed from schedule

### Canceling a Scheduled Message

1. Find the message in the scheduled list
2. Click **Delete** or **Cancel**
3. Confirm cancellation

## Requirements

For scheduled messages to send successfully:

<Check>Eazybe extension must be installed</Check>
<Check>Chrome browser must be running (can be minimized)</Check>
<Check>WhatsApp Web must be connected</Check>
<Check>Computer must be on and connected to internet</Check>

<Warning>
  If your computer is off or WhatsApp Web is disconnected at the scheduled time, the message will attempt to send when connection is restored.
</Warning>

## Use Cases

### Sales Follow-ups
Schedule a follow-up 3 days after sending a proposal:
> "Hi [Name], just checking in on the proposal I sent. Do you have any questions?"

### Timezone Optimization
Schedule messages to arrive during the recipient's business hours:
- Schedule at 9 AM recipient's time
- Avoid sending during their night

### Reminder Sequences
Set up a series of reminders:
- Day 1: Initial outreach
- Day 3: Follow-up
- Day 7: Final reminder

### Event Reminders
Schedule reminders before meetings:
> "Reminder: We have a call scheduled for tomorrow at 2 PM. Looking forward to speaking with you!"

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What happens if WhatsApp is disconnected at send time?">
    The message queues and attempts to send when WhatsApp Web reconnects. With Cloud enabled, messages send regardless of your connection status.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I schedule messages to multiple contacts?">
    Scheduled messages are per-conversation. For bulk scheduling, consider WABA Broadcasting.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Is there a limit to scheduled messages?">
    No hard limit, but we recommend keeping the queue manageable for best performance.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I add Quick Reply to scheduled message?">
    Yes, you can select from your Quick Replies when composing a scheduled message.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
