You can easily add an activity in Bitrix using Eazybe. This allows you to keep track of activities related to your Bitrix leads directly from your WhatsApp chats.

## Adding a Lead Activity

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the WhatsApp Chat">
    Open the WhatsApp chat of the Bitrix lead for whom you want to add an activity.

    ![Open WhatsApp chat](/images/1224469309.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click on the Bitrix Icon">
    Click on the **Bitrix** icon located in the right sidebar of the Eazybe extension.

    ![Bitrix icon in sidebar](/images/1224470433.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access the Activity Section">
    The second header under the Bitrix integration will be the **Activity** section.

    ![Activity section](/images/1224471678.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Activity">
    Click on the **Add Activity** button.

    ![Add activity button](/images/1224473999.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in Required Fields">
    Fill in the necessary details in the provided fields. You can also add **Notes** in the activity section for additional context.

    ![Activity form fields](/images/1224476358.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save the Activity">
    Click on the **Save** button, and your activity will be created.

    ![Save activity](/images/1224478141.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage Your Activity">
    Once the activity is saved, it will be displayed in the UI. You will have options to:
    - **View Note** - See any notes attached to the activity
    - **Postpone** - Delay the activity by a certain amount of time
    - **Edit** or **Delete** - Modify or remove the activity by clicking on the three dots menu

    ![Manage activity options](/images/1225976435.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>
