## Approval Timeframes

Meta uses a combination of automated AI and human review processes. While official guidelines state approvals can take up to **24 hours**, most templates are processed much faster.

### Typical Approval Times

- **Simple templates**: Usually approved within minutes
- **Marketing templates**: May take 1-4 hours
- **Complex templates**: Can take up to 24 hours
- **First-time submissions**: May require additional review

## Guidelines for Faster Approval

### Content Best Practices

- Use clear, professional language
- Avoid spam-like content or excessive capitalization
- Include a clear call-to-action
- Make sure the template purpose is obvious

### Technical Requirements

- Follow Meta's template formatting guidelines
- Use proper placeholder syntax for variables
- Keep message length within recommended limits
- Include required opt-out language for marketing templates

## Common Rejection Reasons

Templates may be rejected for:

- Misleading or deceptive content
- Violating Meta's commerce policies
- Missing required disclosures
- Poor formatting or unclear purpose
- Spam-like characteristics

## Resubmission Tips

If your template is rejected:

1. Review the rejection reason carefully
2. Make the necessary modifications
3. Wait at least 24 hours before resubmitting
4. Consider using a different template category if applicable
