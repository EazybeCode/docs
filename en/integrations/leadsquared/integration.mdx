Connect your LeadSquared account with WhatsApp through the EazyBe extension to sync contacts and backup chats automatically.

## Prerequisites

<Info>
  Before starting, ensure you have:
  - The latest EazyBe WhatsApp extension installed
  - An active LeadSquared account with admin access
</Info>

## Integration Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open WhatsApp Web Integrations">
    Click on the **Integrations** button in your WhatsApp Web interface through the EazyBe extension.

    ![WhatsApp Web Integrations](/images/1187950382.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select LeadSquared">
    Locate and click on the **LeadSquared** connection option from the available integrations list.

    ![Select LeadSquared Integration](/images/1224209473.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access LeadSquared API Settings">
    In your LeadSquared account, navigate to **Settings â†’ API and Webhooks** section to retrieve your credentials.

    <!-- TODO(image-missing): original_url="https://eazybe-9653cc25aa6b.intercom-attachments-1.com/i/o/1138799386/114455385bf661e7666952cb/leadsquared-step-1_36ed56d0.png" -->
  </Step>

  <Step title="Copy Your Host Name">
    From the API Host field, copy your **Host Name** value.

    <!-- TODO(image-missing): original_url="https://eazybe-9653cc25aa6b.intercom-attachments-1.com/i/o/1138799394/a45ff3f642c5bca4fc08dde9/leadsquared-step-2_f70e1193.png" -->
  </Step>

  <Step title="Get Access Key and Secret Key">
    Navigate to the **Secret Key** section to find your **Access ID** and **Secret Key**.

    ![LeadSquared API Credentials](/images/1183716346.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Credentials in EazyBe">
    Return to the EazyBe integration form and enter your credentials:
    - **Host Name**
    - **Access Key**
    - **Secret Key**

    Click **Connect** to complete the integration.

    <!-- TODO(image-missing): original_url="https://eazybe-9653cc25aa6b.intercom-attachments-1.com/i/o/1138799405/47695192282ecaff4c73c0de/leadsquared-step-4_c81d6325.png" -->
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Once connected, your WhatsApp contacts will automatically sync with LeadSquared, and chat backups will be stored in your CRM.
</Tip>
