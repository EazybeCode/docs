The Account section is the last section in the left sidebar of your EazyBe Workspace. It provides access to your personal account settings and billing information.

## Accessing the Account Section

You can access the Account section in two ways:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Via Sidebar">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open the Sidebar">
        Navigate to the left sidebar in your EazyBe Workspace.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Click on Account">
        Click on the **Account** section at the bottom of the sidebar to access your account settings.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Via Profile Icon">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Locate Profile Icon">
        Find the user profile icon in the top-right corner of the workspace.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Click Profile Icon">
        Click on the **profile icon** to access your account settings directly.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Account Section Features

In the Account section, you will find two main options:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Account Details" icon="user">
    View and manage your personal account information, including your profile settings and preferences.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Billing Information" icon="credit-card">
    Access your billing details, subscription information, and payment history.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## What You Can Do

From the Account section, you can:

- **View your account details** - See your profile information and account status
- **Manage billing** - Review subscription plans, payment methods, and invoices
- **Update preferences** - Modify your account settings and notifications

<Tip>
  Keep your account information up to date to ensure smooth operation of all EazyBe features and integrations.
</Tip>
