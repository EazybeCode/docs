- Go to the contact for which you want to create a Deal for.
- Click on the HubSpot icon. The fourth button in the top bar will take you to the Deals section.

  

- Click on the New Deal button.

  

- You will get some default properties and you can also select and customize from a range of properties by clicking on the Edit icon.
Reorder the Selected properties as per your convenience and click on the Apply button. (NOTE: This will only be applicable if you have chosen to Edit the default properties)

  

- Fill all the details and click on Save Deal and your deal will be created.
