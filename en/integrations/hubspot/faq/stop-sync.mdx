## Can I Stop Sync for Particular Contacts?

Yes! You have two options to control syncing for specific contacts:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Option 1: Unlink Contact" icon="link-slash">
    Unlink the contact if you no longer want it to appear on HubSpot at all.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Option 2: Stop Chat Backup" icon="pause">
    Keep the contact in HubSpot but stop backing up their chat conversations using the "Don't Sync" feature.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

---

## How to Stop Chat Backup for Specific Contacts

If you want to keep the contact in HubSpot but stop syncing their chat history, follow these steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a New Label">
    Create a new label specifically for contacts you don't want to sync.

    ![Create New Label](/images/1185303537.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Contacts to the Label">
    Add the contacts you want to exclude from syncing to this label.

    ![Add Contacts to Label](/images/1185303731.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Navigate to Backup and Sync">
    Go to the **Backup and Sync** section in the sidebar.

    ![Backup and Sync Section](/images/1185303881.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Don't Sync">
    Click the **Don't Sync** button for the label you created. This will prevent chat backups for all contacts in that label.

    ![Don't Sync Button](/images/1185304089.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
Contacts in a "Don't Sync" label will remain in HubSpot, but their new WhatsApp conversations will not be backed up.
</Info>
