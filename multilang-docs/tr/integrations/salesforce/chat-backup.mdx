title: "Where can I see backup of my WhatsApp chats in Salesforce?"
description: ""
---

You can view the back up of your WhatsApp chats within Salesforce in several areas such as:

- ### Salesforce Contact's Page:

- Each synced WhatsApp conversation will appear in the** Notes and Attachments section** in the respective Salesforce Contacts' Page.

<Frame>
<img src="/images/1196813393.png" alt="" />
</Frame>
- The conversation will also appear in the **WhatsApp Chats by EazyBe** custom activity object.

<Frame>
<img src="/images/1196813635.png" alt="" />
</Frame>
- ### Salesforce Lead's Page:

- Each synced WhatsApp conversation will appear in the **WhatsApp Chats by EazyBe** custom activity object in the respective Salesforce Leads' Page.

<img src="/images/1196813835.png" alt="" />
</Frame>
