The EazyBe account section can be accessed by clicking on the EazyBe icon on the top right corner.
​

This section will give an overview of your profile and the current plan you’re at and it’s expiry date and also a lot more settings to users as given below.
​


**Adding an account**


- You can add an account by clicking on the Other Profiles button.
​


- Click on the Add Account button and you can add by entering the details.


**Join Another Organization**
​


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1352810290.png" alt="" />
</Frame>

You can join another organization by clicking on the Join another Organization button and entering the organization code of the organization that you want to join.
​

Using this feature, your employees can also join your organization. You just need to copy your Organization code and share it with your employees.
