**Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)**


**How many email accounts can I link to a single phone number on Eazybe?**

*Eazybe allows users to link up to 4 email accounts to a single phone number.*


**Can I switch between accounts easily?**

*Yes, you can switch between accounts by clicking on the "My Account" button. Each added account acts as a standalone profile for all your Eazybe needs.*


**Can I link accounts from different email providers?**

*Yes, you can link accounts from different email providers as long as they are valid and can receive the OTP for verification during the signup process.*


**What information is required during the account creation process?**

*You need to enter the credentials for the new account, including the email address and OTP. Additionally, an Organization code is required, which can be obtained from your admin.*
