Once the integration of Zoho with the EazyBe WhatsApp extension is completed, you can easily verify that everything is working correctly.

## How to Confirm Successful Integration

<Steps>
  <Step title="Complete the Integration Process">
    After connecting your Zoho account with EazyBe, you will be automatically redirected back to WhatsApp.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check for the Zoho Icon">
    Look for the **Zoho icon** in the right sidebar of the EazyBe extension panel.

    ![Zoho Integration Icon](/images/1223657457.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify the Integration">
    If you can see the Zoho icon in the sidebar, it indicates that the integration has been successfully completed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  The Zoho icon appearing in the sidebar confirms that your CRM is now connected and you can start syncing contacts and messages between WhatsApp and Zoho.
</Info>

<Tip>
  If the Zoho icon does not appear after completing the integration, try refreshing the WhatsApp Web page or restarting your browser.
</Tip>
