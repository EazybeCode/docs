## Introduction

Broadcast campaigns allow you to send approved message templates to multiple contacts at once. This is ideal for newsletters, promotional offers, appointment reminders, and other bulk communications.

**This guide covers:**
- Prerequisites before sending your first broadcast
- Two methods: Dashboard and Extension
- Step-by-step broadcast creation process
- Tracking campaign performance

---

## Prerequisites

Before sending your first broadcast, ensure you have:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connected WABA Account">
    Your WhatsApp Business API account must be connected to Eazybe. See [How to Connect WABA](/en/waba/connect-waba) for setup instructions.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Funded Eazybe Wallet">
    Your Eazybe Wallet must have sufficient balance to cover message costs. See [Wallet & Recharge](/en/waba/wallet) for details.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Approved Message Template">
    You need at least one approved message template. See [Creating Message Templates](/en/waba/templates/creating-and-managing-templates) for guidance.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>

</Note>

## Choose Your Broadcasting Method

Eazybe offers two ways to send broadcast campaigns:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Dashboard Method" icon="browser">
    Send broadcasts from the Eazybe web dashboard. Best for uploading CSV contact lists.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Extension Method" icon="puzzle-piece">
    Send broadcasts directly from WhatsApp Web using the Eazybe extension. Best for using labeled contacts.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

---

## Method 1: Dashboard Broadcasting

### Step 1: Navigate to Broadcasts

Access the **Broadcasts** section from the main menu in your Eazybe dashboard.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1589454418.png" alt="Navigate to Broadcasts in Dashboard" />
</Frame>

---

### Step 2: Create New Broadcast

Click the **Create Broadcast** button to start a new campaign.

---

### Step 3: Name Your Campaign

Give your campaign a clear, internal name that helps you identify it later (e.g., "June Newsletter 2025" or "Holiday Sale 2025").

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1589455306.png" alt="Name Your Broadcast Campaign" />
</Frame>

---

### Step 4: Select Template

Choose one of your **Approved Message Templates** from the dropdown list. Only templates with "Approved" status will be available.

---

### Step 5: Upload Contacts

Click **Upload CSV** and select your contact file from your computer.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1589463099.png" alt="Upload CSV Contacts" />
</Frame>

<Warning>
**CSV Requirements:**
- File must contain a column named "Phone"
- Phone numbers must be in international format (e.g., `91xxxxxxxxxx` for India)
- Do not include the "+" symbol
</Warning>

---

### Step 6: Select Contacts

After uploading, select the contacts from the imported CSV to whom you wish to send the broadcast.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1589464921.png" alt="Select Contacts from CSV" />
</Frame>

---

### Step 7: Select Label (Optional)

You can also select contacts by label if you have previously tagged contacts in your system.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1589475395.png" alt="Select Label in Dashboard" />
</Frame>

<Tip>
Ensure only individual contacts (not groups) are labeled when using the label selection method.
</Tip>

---

### Step 8: Launch Broadcast

Review your campaign settings and click **Send Broadcast** to launch.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1589467988.png" alt="Send Broadcast from Dashboard" />
</Frame>

---

## Method 2: Extension Broadcasting

### Step 1: Navigate to Broadcasts

Open WhatsApp Web with the Eazybe extension active. Click on the **Broadcasts** section in the extension panel.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1589492369.png" alt="Navigate to Broadcasts in Extension" />
</Frame>

---

### Step 2: Create New Broadcast

Click the **Create Broadcast** button to start a new campaign.

---

### Step 3: Name Your Campaign

Give your campaign a clear, internal name (e.g., "June Newsletter 2025").

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1589455306.png" alt="Name Your Broadcast Campaign" />
</Frame>

---

### Step 4: Select Template

Choose one of your **Approved Message Templates** from the dropdown list.

---

### Step 5: Select Label

Select a label containing the contacts you want to message. This method uses contacts you've already tagged in WhatsApp.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1589476967.png" alt="Select Label in Extension" />
</Frame>

<Warning>
**Important:** Ensure only individual contacts (not groups) are labeled. Group chats cannot receive broadcast messages.
</Warning>

---

### Step 6: Launch Broadcast

Review your settings and click **Send Broadcast** to launch your campaign.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1589487173.png" alt="Send Broadcast from Extension" />
</Frame>

---

## Tracking Campaign Performance

After launching your broadcast, you can track its performance directly from the Broadcast dashboard:

- **Sent:** Total messages dispatched
- **Delivered:** Messages successfully delivered to recipients
- **Read:** Messages opened and read by recipients
- **Failed:** Messages that could not be delivered

<Note>
Campaign analytics are updated in real-time. Check back periodically to monitor engagement rates.
</Note>
