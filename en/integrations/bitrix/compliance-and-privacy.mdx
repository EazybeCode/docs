## Data Processing Agreement (DPA)

If your organization requires a Data Processing Agreement for compliance or record-keeping purposes, you can easily request one.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Identify Your Need">
    Determine if your organization requires a DPA for compliance with internal policies or regulatory requirements.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send a Request">
    Email us at **hey@eazybe.com** with your DPA request.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive Your DPA">
    Our team will process your request and provide the necessary documentation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  A Data Processing Agreement outlines how EazyBe handles, processes, and protects your data when using the Bitrix24 integration.
</Note>
