## Creating New Contacts

Follow these steps to create a new HubSpot contact directly from WhatsApp:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open WhatsApp Chat">
    Navigate to the WhatsApp conversation for the contact you want to add to HubSpot.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access HubSpot Panel">
    Click the **HubSpot icon** in the right sidebar to open the Mini-CRM panel.

    <img src="/images/1329158695.png" alt="HubSpot Icon in Sidebar" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create Contact">
    Click the **Create Contact** button. The phone number will automatically populate from the WhatsApp conversation.

    <Info>
    The phone number field is auto-filled and non-editable to maintain data integrity between WhatsApp and HubSpot.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fill Contact Details">
    Enter any additional contact information such as:
    - First name and last name
    - Email address
    - Company name
    - Any custom properties configured in your HubSpot account
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save Contact">
    Click **Save** to create the contact in HubSpot. The contact will be instantly available in your CRM.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Check>
Once saved, the contact is immediately synced to your HubSpot account and linked to the WhatsApp conversation.
</Check>

## Customizing Existing Contacts

You can easily update and customize contact properties for existing HubSpot contacts:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Contact Chat">
    Navigate to the WhatsApp conversation of the contact you want to customize.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Edit">
    In the Mini-CRM panel, click the **Edit** button within the contact card to modify the contact properties.

    <img src="/images/1329160376.png" alt="Edit Contact Properties" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Modify Properties">
    Update the contact information as needed:
    - Edit existing property values
    - Rearrange the display order of properties
    - Designate fields as required or optional
  </Step>

  <Step title="Apply and Save">
    Click **Apply** to confirm your changes, then click **Save** to update the contact in HubSpot.
  </Step>
</Steps>
