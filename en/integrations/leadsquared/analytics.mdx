The LeadSquared Analytics section provides valuable insights about your leads, offering statistical measures to capture lead activities and gauge their level of engagement.

![LeadSquared Analytics Dashboard](/images/1136481141.png)

## Key Analytics Metrics

<Steps>
  <Step title="Lead Score">
    A cumulative metric reflecting how engaged a lead is. This score is calculated by assigning points to different lead activities within the application.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Engagement Score">
    These metrics identify purchase-ready leads by analyzing activities leads are performing on your user interface, giving you an idea of their interests and behavior.
  </Step>
