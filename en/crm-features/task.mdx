Do you have a pending task, you want to save it so that you can track it anytime you want. Don’t worry, The Task Section of the EazyBe’s Bitrix’s Integration comes to your rescue.

- Open the WhatsApp chat of your Bitrix lead.
- Click on the Bitrix icon on the right side-bar.
- The last header under the Lead Section will be the tasks one, click on it and you can access the section.
- Click on the Add Task button.
- Enter the details and click on the Save button.
- You can see your created task.
- You have an option to either view and hide note. You can also Edit or Delete the created task by clicking on the three dots.
