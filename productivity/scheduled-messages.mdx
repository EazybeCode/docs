## Overview

Schedule messages to send at the perfect time, even when you're not online.

<Note>
  **Cloud Connection Required**: For messages to send when your computer is off, ensure Cloud Sync is enabled.
</Note>

## Scheduling a Message

<Steps>
  <Step title="Compose Message">
    Type your message in the chat input
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Schedule">
    Click the **clock icon** next to send button
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Date & Time">
    Choose when to send:
    - Specific date and time
    - Quick options (Tomorrow 9am, etc.)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Click **Schedule** to confirm
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Viewing Scheduled Messages

1. Click **Scheduled** in sidebar
2. View all pending messages
3. Edit or cancel as needed

## Recurring Messages

Send messages on a regular schedule:

| Frequency | Use Case |
|-----------|----------|
| **Daily** | Morning check-ins |
| **Weekly** | Weekly updates |
| **Monthly** | Payment reminders |
| **Custom** | Specific days |

### Setting Up Recurring

1. Schedule a message
2. Toggle **Repeat** on
3. Select frequency
4. Set end date (optional)

## Cloud Sync Requirement

For reliable scheduling:

| Sync Status | Messages Send When |
|-------------|-------------------|
| **Cloud ON** | Any time (recommended) |
| **Cloud OFF** | Only when browser open |

## Time Zones

- Messages scheduled in YOUR time zone
- Recipients receive at their local equivalent
- Dashboard shows your local time

## Best Practices

<Tip>
  Schedule messages during business hours to avoid late-night or early-morning sends.
</Tip>

### Good Times to Schedule

| Audience | Best Times |
|----------|------------|
| B2B | 10am-4pm weekdays |
| B2C | 11am-1pm, 7pm-9pm |
| Global | Consider recipient timezone |
