EazyBe's Zoho Integration Tasks Section helps you save and track pending assignments easily. Keep all your contact-related tasks organized and accessible within the platform.

## How to Create Contact Tasks

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the WhatsApp Chat">
    Open the WhatsApp conversation with your Zoho Contact that you want to create a task for.

    ![Open WhatsApp Chat](/images/1217632058.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click on the Zoho Icon">
    In the right-side panel of the EazyBe extension, locate and click on the **Zoho icon** to access your CRM features.

    ![Click Zoho Icon](/images/1113035273.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access the Tasks Section">
    Navigate to the **Tasks Section**, positioned as the second header under the Contact Section.

    ![Tasks Section](/images/1113035517.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create a New Task">
    Click on the **New Task** button to initiate task creation.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Task Details and Save">
    Complete the task information fields and click **Save Task** to create your task.

    ![Save Task](/images/1192101471.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing Your Tasks

Once created, you can perform the following actions on your tasks:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="View" icon="eye">
    Access and review all your saved tasks for the contact
  </Card>
  <Card title="Edit" icon="pen">
    Modify existing tasks to update information or deadlines
  </Card>
  <Card title="Delete" icon="trash">
    Remove tasks that are completed or no longer needed
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
