This guide will walk you through the required steps to connect the EazyBe WhatsApp Extension with Salesforce and granting the necessary permissions for integration.

- ### Open the EazyBe WhatsApp ExtensionAs a first step, click on the **Integrations **button on your EazyBe WhatsApp web extension.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1112649892.png" alt="" />
</Frame>

- ### Connect to SalesforceClick on the **Connect button** below Salesforce integration. This button will redirect you to your Salesforce account.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1195253698.png" alt="" />
</Frame>

- ### Select your Salesforce AccountChoose the account that you wish to connect with the EazyBe extension.

- ### Grant Necessary PermissionsWhen prompted, click on the Accept button to grant the necessary permissions required by EazyBe for a seamless integration of your Salesforce account with WhatsApp. These permissions will allow you to view and sync your WhatsApp chats with Salesforce.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1196689623.png" alt="" />
</Frame>

- ### Successful IntegrationCongratulations! You have successfully connected your EazyBe WhatsApp Extension with Salesforce.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1195256371.png" alt="" />
</Frame>
