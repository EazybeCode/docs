The necessary permissions will be prompted during the integration process. Be sure to accept these permissions.

## Why Are Permissions Required?

These permissions are vital for enabling smooth synchronization and backup functionality for your WhatsApp conversations when using the Eazybe WhatsApp Chrome Extension.

![Required Permissions](/images/1196804845.png)

<Warning>
  Make sure to accept all requested permissions during the integration process. Declining permissions may result in limited functionality.
</Warning>
