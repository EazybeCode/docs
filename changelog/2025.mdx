## January 2025

### January 11, 2025

**New Feature: AI Unreplied Chats Agent**

Introducing intelligent chat prioritization:
- Automatic Critical/Important/Normal classification

- Smart keyword and sentiment detection
- CRM-based customer value recognition

**Improvement: Enhanced Privacy Controls**

- New Privacy & Compliance documentation
- GDPR compliance tools
- Employee monitoring transparency features
- Label-based chat exclusion from backup
