Integrating LeadSquared with your EazyBe WhatsApp extension is simple and quick! Below mentioned are pre-requisites needed to integrate them:

- **Ensure you have an active LeadSquared Account:** You need to ensure that your LeadSquared account is setup and functioning.
​

- **Install EazyBe WhatsApp Extension**: Ensure that your EazyBe WhatsApp extension is properly connected to WhatsApp web.
​

  <img src="/images/1346590090.png" alt="" />
</Frame>

- **Permissions**: Ensure you have the necessary permissions from your Administrator to connect LeadSquared to WhatsApp.

To integrate LeadSquared with EazyBe, ensure that the extension is linked to your LeadSquared account. For a step-by-step guide on how to do this, visit [How to Integrate LeadSquared with the EazyBe WhatsApp Extension](https://help.eazybe.com/en/articles/9707595-how-to-integrate-leadsquared-with-the-eazybe-extension).
