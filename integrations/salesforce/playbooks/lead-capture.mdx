</Info>

## The Problem

WhatsApp leads don't make it into Salesforce pipeline.

## The Solution

Eazybe automatically creates Salesforce leads when new WhatsApp conversations start.

  <img src="/images/salesforce/lead-capture-solution.gif" alt="Auto-create Salesforce lead" />
</Frame>

## Step-by-Step Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Eazybe Settings">
    Click **Eazybe icon** → **Settings** → **CRM Integration**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enable Auto-Create">
    Toggle **Auto-create records in Salesforce** to ON
    
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/salesforce/auto-create-toggle.png" alt="Auto-create toggle" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure Field Mapping">
    | WhatsApp | Salesforce Field |
    |----------|------------------|
    | Phone Number | Mobile Phone |
    | Profile Name | First Name |
    | - | Lead Source = "WhatsApp" |
    | - | Lead Owner = Rep who received message |
    
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/salesforce/field-mapping.png" alt="Field mapping" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>
