This guide will walk you through the required steps to connect the EazyBe WhatsApp Extension with Salesforce and granting the necessary permissions for integration.

- 
#### Open the EazyBe WhatsApp Extension
As a first step, click on the **Integrations **button on your EazyBe WhatsApp web extension.

  

- 
#### Connect to Salesforce
Click on the **Connect button** below Salesforce integration. This button will redirect you to your Salesforce account.

  

- 
#### Select your Salesforce Account
Choose the account that you wish to connect with the EazyBe extension.

- 
#### Grant Necessary Permissions
When prompted, click on the Accept button to grant the necessary permissions required by EazyBe for a seamless integration of your Salesforce account with WhatsApp. These permissions will allow you to view and sync your WhatsApp chats with Salesforce.

  

- 
#### Successful Integration
Congratulations! You have successfully connected your EazyBe WhatsApp Extension with Salesforce.
