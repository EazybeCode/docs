EazyBe includes a powerful AI suggestion feature that helps you rephrase and improve your messages when communicating with clients through WhatsApp.

## How to Use AI Suggestions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open WhatsApp Chat">
    Open the WhatsApp chat of any contact you want to message.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable AI Suggestions">
    Ensure that the **AI suggestions** button is switched on in the chat interface.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Type Your Message">
    Type in your text. You need to type at least **10 characters** for the AI to provide suggestions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get AI Suggestions">
    Click on the **Rephrase** button and you'll see text suggestions generated by AI.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Request More Suggestions">
    If you want additional options, click the **Rephrase** button again to get more AI-generated suggestions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

</Tip>
