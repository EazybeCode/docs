With Eazybe's integration, you can automate WhatsApp messages directly through HubSpot workflows. Here's how to set it up step-by-step:
â€‹


### **Step 1: Set Up a Workflow in HubSpot**

- Go to HubSpot and navigate to the **Automation** tab in the top menu.


- Select **Workflows** from the dropdown.


- Click **Create Workflow** in the top-right corner and choose **From scratch**.


- Select **Contact-based**, **Deal-based**, or **Company-based** depending on your needs.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1255812257.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


### **Step 2: Add a Trigger**

- Name your workflow.


- Click **Set up triggers** and choose the conditions to initiate the workflow. For example:


**Contact properties**: Trigger when a contact reaches a specific lifecycle stage.


- **Deal properties**: Trigger when a deal moves to a specific stage.


- **Custom triggers**: Define your own rules.


- Click **Apply filter** and **Save** to finalize the trigger.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1267586286.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


- Turn on the **Re-enrollment** to make sure it work properly


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1267587957.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


### **Step 3: Add the EazyBe WhatsApp Action**

- Click the **+** icon below your trigger to add an action.


- Scroll down and select **Send WhatsApp message** under the EazyBe integration section.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1255804613.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


### **Step 4: Configure the WhatsApp Message**

- **Recipient Phone**: Select the contact's phone field (e.g., Mobile Phone Number, Phone Number).


- **Message**: Customize your message. Use contact tokens to personalize it.


- **Media URL** (Optional): Add links to share documents or files.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1255806368.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


### **Step 5: Review and Activate the Workflow**

- Preview your workflow to ensure all triggers and actions are set correctly.


- Click **Review and publish** to make the workflow live.
