Eazybe custom webhooks allow you to quickly connect WhatsApp to any destination. By using Eazybe custom webhooks, you can automatically trigger actions when you receive or send WhatsApp messages.

Examples of using Eazybe custom webhooks include:


Triggering a custom webhook when team members receive a WhatsApp message.

Triggering a custom webhook when team members send a WhatsApp message.
To know how to integrate EazyBe WhatsApp extension with Webhooks do the following steps.


- Ensure that you have the latest version of our WhatsApp extension installed and running on your device.


- When you login into your WhatsApp web account you will see the Integrations button on the right-side bar.


- Click on the Button and you will see a popup.


- Click on the Connect button under the WebHooks section.


- Choose your account.


- Turn on the WebHooks by clicking on the Turn On button on the top-right corner.
