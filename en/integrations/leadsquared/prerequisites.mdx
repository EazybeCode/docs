Before connecting LeadSquared with WhatsApp, ensure you have the following prerequisites in place.

## Requirements

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Active LeadSquared Account" icon="user-check">
    Ensure your LeadSquared account is set up and functioning properly.
  </Card>
  <Card title="EazyBe Extension" icon="puzzle-piece">
    The EazyBe WhatsApp extension must be installed and connected to WhatsApp Web.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Administrator Permissions" icon="shield-check">
    You need the necessary permissions from your administrator to connect LeadSquared to WhatsApp.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Verification Checklist

<Steps>
  <Step title="Verify LeadSquared Account">
    Log into your LeadSquared account and confirm it is active and functioning correctly.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Install EazyBe Extension">
    Ensure the EazyBe WhatsApp extension is properly installed in your browser and connected to WhatsApp Web.

    ![EazyBe Extension Setup](/images/1346590090.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm Admin Access">
    Verify that you have administrator permissions or have received approval from your administrator to proceed with the integration.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Once all prerequisites are met, you can proceed with the LeadSquared and WhatsApp integration setup.
</Info>
