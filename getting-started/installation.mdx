## Overview

Eazybe works as a web application that enhances WhatsApp Web with CRM integration, team collaboration, and AI features.

## System Requirements

| Requirement | Minimum |
|-------------|---------|
| Browser | Chrome, Edge, or Brave (latest version) |
| Internet | Stable connection |
| WhatsApp | Business or Personal account |
| Google Account | Required for chat backup |

## Installation Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Account">
    Visit [eazybe.com](https://eazybe.com) and sign up
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Up Workspace">
    Create your company workspace
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install Browser App">
    Add Eazybe to your browser from [eazybe.com/install](https://eazybe.com/install)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect WhatsApp">
    Open WhatsApp Web and scan QR code
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Google Drive">
    Authorize Google Drive for chat backup
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Verify Installation

After setup, you should see:

- ✅ Eazybe sidebar in WhatsApp Web
- ✅ "Connected" status indicator
- ✅ Your conversations loading

## Inviting Team Members

1. Go to **Settings** → **Team**
2. Click **+ Invite**
3. Enter team member's email
4. They'll receive setup instructions

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Eazybe not appearing?">
    1. Refresh WhatsApp Web
    2. Clear browser cache
    3. Re-install from eazybe.com/install
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Connection issues?">
    1. Check internet connection
    2. Make sure WhatsApp phone is online
    3. Re-scan QR code if needed
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
