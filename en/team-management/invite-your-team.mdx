**What is the purpose of inviting employees to my organization on Eazybe?**

*Inviting employees enables task assignment, access to shared Quick Replies and Labels, and monitoring of analytics and chats through the Team Inbox.*

**How are my chats backed up when using Eazybe?**

*Chats are backed up from the user's WhatsApp number in the Admin's Google Drive, and you can view them from the Team Inbox.*

**Is my data secure when using Eazybe?**

*Eazybe does not store any data on its servers. All data is backed up with the user's WhatsApp number and stored securely in the Admin's Google Drive.*

**Are there any specific details required when adding employees to Eazybe?**

*Yes, when adding employees, you need to provide mandatory details such as Name, Email, and WhatsApp Number. Additional details may also be included as needed.*

**Can invited employees access shared resources like Quick Replies and Labels?**

*Yes, inviting employees allows them access to shared Quick Replies and Labels, promoting consistent messaging and collaboration.*

**What features are available for organization members in the Team Inbox?**

*Organization members can monitor analytics, view chats, and engage with tasks and follow-ups in the Team Inbox, enhancing collaboration and transparency.*

**How can I check if the invites to employees were successfully sent?**

*After entering employee details and clicking "Send Invites," you can verify the successful sending of invites by checking the confirmation message or status in the Eazybe workspace.*
