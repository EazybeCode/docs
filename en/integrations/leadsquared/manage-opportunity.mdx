---
title: "Manage Opportunity"
description: "View, edit, delete, and create tasks for LeadSquared opportunities from the EazyBe WhatsApp extension"
---

The EazyBe WhatsApp Extension allows you to efficiently manage your LeadSquared Opportunities directly from within WhatsApp.

## View Opportunity on LeadSquared

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select the Opportunity">
    Click on the respective opportunity you want to view from the Mini-CRM panel.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open in LeadSquared">
    Click the **View Opportunity on LeadSquared** button to access the LeadSquared Opportunities page directly.

    ![View Opportunity on LeadSquared](/images/1188095094.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  You can also view chat backup in the Opportunities section within LeadSquared.
</Info>

## Edit Opportunity

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select the Opportunity">
    Choose the opportunity you want to modify from the list.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open Edit Mode">
    Click the **Edit** button to open the properties window.

    ![Edit Opportunity](/images/1224421190.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Modify Properties">
    Adjust the user properties as needed in the edit form.

    ![Edit Opportunity Form](/images/1224421425.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click **Apply** to confirm your changes, then click **Save** to finalize the updates.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Delete Opportunity

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Options Menu">
    Click the **three dots** beside the target opportunity.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Delete">
    Select the **Delete** button to remove the opportunity.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Deleting an opportunity is permanent. Make sure you want to remove it before confirming.
</Warning>

## Create Opportunity Tasks

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Opportunity">
    Click on the target opportunity to open its details.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Navigate to Tasks Section">
    Go to the **Tasks** section within the opportunity.

    ![Tasks Section](/images/1224435299.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add New Task">
    Click **Add Task** and fill in the required details.

    ![Add Task Form](/images/1224435471.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save Task">
    Click **Save** to create the task.
  </Step>
</Steps>
