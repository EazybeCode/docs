The profile section of the Zoho Lead will show the basic contact details. To access and edit the profile section, you should ensure that the Lead is created.


- Open the WhatsApp chat of your Zoho Lead.


- Click on the Zoho icon on the right-side bar.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1112961374.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


- The first header that opens up under the Lead section will be the profile one.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1112962128.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


- You can view and edit user properties for your lead and can save the changes by clicking on the Save button below.
