## Feature: Team Inbox

Transform your customer communication with a high-velocity, centralized Team Inbox, now engineered for 40% faster performance to handle massive scale with instant response times. This shared workspace allows multiple team members to access and manage all your business's WhatsApp conversations from a single, unified dashboard with zero-latency, real-time collaboration.

You can utilize Smart Chat Assignment to route conversations to specialized agents—ensuring team members only see their focused workload—while maintaining full Communication Transparency through "Sent By" tracking to know exactly which agent communicated with which lead. Empower your support, sales, and marketing teams to work together seamlessly, handling high volumes of inquiries without ever missing a beat.

## Why Team Inbox Matters

For businesses that are scaling, a Team Inbox is the key to delivering fast, personal, and organized customer service through WhatsApp. It moves you beyond the limitations of a single-device app and provides a robust, lightweight platform for professional team-based communication.

### Boosts Team Productivity and Collaboration

Enable your team to work together with zero-latency synchronization. By routing chats to specific agents and limiting views to their focused workload, you eliminate confusion and allow for faster resolution times, significantly increasing overall team efficiency.

### Enhances Customer Support

With a unified view and instant interface response times, your team can respond faster than ever. The ability to see conversation history, internal notes, and context means customers don't have to repeat themselves, leading to higher satisfaction and loyalty.

### Streamlines Sales and Lead Management

Your sales team can effectively qualify leads and guide prospects through the purchasing process within the shared inbox. "Sent By" tracking ensures you always know which sales agent is nurturing a lead, creating a more efficient and accountable sales cycle.

### Provides Actionable Insights & Transparency

Beyond built-in analytics, gain granular visibility into your team's operations. Monitor response times and use "Sent By" tracking to audit individual agent contributions, allowing you to optimize your support strategy and make informed decisions to improve customer engagement.

## Chat Assignment

<Steps>
    <Step>
        <h3>Access Assignment Options</h3>
        <p>Click on the "Assign to" dropdown to assign the chat.</p>
        <img src="/images/team-inbox-17.png" alt="Assign to dropdown" />
    </Step>
    <Step>
        <h3>View Team Structure</h3>
        <p>You get a Team-wise employees list and also an All Employees section to assign this chat.</p>
        <img src="/images/team-inbox-18.png" alt="Team employees list" />
    </Step>
    <Step>
        <h3>Select Team Member</h3>
        <p>Click on any Team and select whom to assign.</p>
        <img src="/images/team-inbox-19.png" alt="Select team member" />
    </Step>
    <Step>
        <h3>Assignment Complete</h3>
        <p>Now the chat has been assigned to the selected Employee and they can access this under their login.</p>
        <img src="/images/team-inbox-20.png" alt="Assigned chat confirmation" />
    </Step>
    <Step>
        <h3>Unassign Chats</h3>
        <p>You can also mark the chat as unassigned if needed.</p>
        <img src="/images/team-inbox-21.png" alt="Unassign chat option" />
    </Step>
</Steps>
