---
title: "Create and Customize Lead"
description: "How to create new leads in LeadSquared and customize lead properties through EazyBe"
---

Create new leads directly from WhatsApp conversations and customize their properties within the LeadSquared Mini-CRM interface.

## Create a New Lead in LeadSquared

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the WhatsApp Conversation">
    Open the WhatsApp conversation with the contact you wish to create a lead for.

    ![WhatsApp Conversation](/images/1343068459.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access LeadSquared Mini-CRM">
    Click the **LeadSquared icon** in the right sidebar to access the Mini-CRM interface.

    ![LeadSquared Mini-CRM](/images/1343068864.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Link or Create Contact">
    - To link an existing lead: Select **"Link Contact"**, choose the lead from available options, and confirm
    - For new contacts: Click **"Create Contact"**, then populate the required fields
  </Step>

  <Step title="View Lead in LeadSquared">
    Once created, use the **"View Lead on LeadSquared"** button to view the lead directly in LeadSquared.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Customize Lead Properties

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the Lead's Contact Card">
    Open the WhatsApp chat of the LeadSquared lead you want to customize.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open Edit Mode">
    Click the **"Edit"** button in the Contact Card to modify properties.

    ![Edit Contact Card](/images/1343043977.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Modify Properties">
    A dialog appears where you can:
    - Edit property values
    - Reorder properties
    - Designate fields as required or optional

    ![Property Editor](/images/1343044285.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click **"Apply"** to confirm your changes, then **"Save"** to finalize.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Customizing lead properties allows you to capture the information most relevant to your sales process directly from WhatsApp conversations.
</Tip>
