The Dashboard is the first section in the left sidebar of your EazyBe Workspace. It provides a comprehensive overview of your employees' performance across various parameters.

## Accessing the Dashboard

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Sidebar">
    Navigate to the left sidebar in your EazyBe Workspace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click on Dashboard">
    Click on the **Dashboard** section to access the performance overview.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Dashboard Features

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Employee Performance" icon="chart-line">
    View detailed metrics about your employees' performance across multiple parameters.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Export Contacts" icon="download">
    Download contact data using the Export button with customizable filters.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Team Filtering" icon="users">
    Select all employees or filter by specific teams to view targeted data.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Date Range Selection" icon="calendar">
    Use the calendar to specify the date range for your performance reports.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Exporting Data

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Export Button">
    Locate and click the **Export** button in the Dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Employees">
    Choose to export data for all employees or select employees from a specific team.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Date Range">
    Open the calendar and select the date range for which you want to view the details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download Report">
    Complete the export to download your contact data.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Analytics Section

The Dashboard includes a dedicated **Analytics** section where you can view detailed data and message analytics for your employees.

<Info>
  The Analytics section provides insights into messaging patterns, response times, and overall team communication metrics.
</Info>
