## How to Add Multiple Accounts

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Other Profiles">
    Navigate to the **My Account** section and select the **Other Profiles** button.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Add New Account">
    Click the **Add Account** button to begin adding a new profile.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Credentials and Verify">
    Enter your email credentials and sign up. Verify your account via OTP or Google sign-in.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Provide Organization Code">
    Enter the Organization code provided by your administrator to link your account.

  </Step>
</Steps>

## Switching Between Accounts

Once setup is complete, you can switch between accounts using the **My Account** button. Each account functions as an independent profile with its own settings and data.


## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How many accounts can I link?">
    Eazybe allows users to link up to **4 email accounts** to a single phone number.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is it easy to switch between accounts?">
    Yes, switching is accessible through the **My Account** button. Each profile operates independently with its own settings.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I use different email providers?">
    Yes, linking works across different email providers as long as you complete valid OTP verification.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What information is required to add an account?">
    You'll need your email credentials, complete OTP verification, and have the Organization code from your administrator.
  </Accordion>
