Have you created an opportunity on LeadSquared and now want to create and track tasks for that opportunity? If yes, here's how you can do it:


#### 1. Open the Lead's WhatsApp Chat
Start by opening the WhatsApp chat of the LeadSquared lead you wish to manage.


#### 2. Access the Opportunity Section
In the LeadSquared Mini-CRM view, click on the **Opportunity** section associated with the lead.


#### 3. Access the Tasks Section
Click on the opportunity for which you want to create a task, then click on the **Tasks** section.


#### 4. Add a New Opportunity Task
Click on the **Add Task** button to add a new task related to the respective opportunity.

#### 5. Fill in the Required Details
Fill in the task details in the provided fields. You can also edit additional fields by clicking on the **Edit** button.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1224446975.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


#### 6. Save the Task
Once you've entered the task details, click the **Save** button, and the task will be saved.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1224447153.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


#### 7. Mark Task as Complete
Once a task is complete, you can mark it as finished by clicking on the **Complete** button.


#### 8. Add More Tasks
To create more tasks, simply click on the **Add Task** button and repeat the steps above.
