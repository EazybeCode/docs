This guide explains how to view and edit a Salesforce Lead profile directly within WhatsApp using EazyBe's Mini-CRM integration.

## Viewing and Editing Lead Profiles

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the WhatsApp Chat">
    Launch the WhatsApp conversation with the Salesforce lead you want to view.

    ![Open WhatsApp Chat](/images/1217639388.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access the Salesforce Integration">
    Click the **Salesforce** icon in the EazyBe extension's right sidebar to open the Mini-CRM panel.

    ![Salesforce Integration](/images/1116537053.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access the Profile Section">
    The first header under the Lead section displays the **Profile Section** with the lead's information.

    ![Profile Section](/images/1116538000.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="View and Edit User Properties">
    Review the user information displayed. To modify details, click the **Edit** button.

    ![Edit Lead Properties](/images/1116538258.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save the Changes">
    After making your modifications, click **Save** at the bottom to apply the changes to Salesforce.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Changes made to the lead profile are synced directly to Salesforce in real-time.
</Info>
