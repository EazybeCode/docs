This guide explains how to set up triggers, actions, and integrations to send personalized WhatsApp messages, improve engagement, and streamline customer communication efficiently through HubSpot workflows.

## Prerequisites

Before creating a HubSpot workflow for WhatsApp campaigns, ensure you have:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="HubSpot Account" icon="hubspot">
    An active HubSpot account with workflow automation access
  </Card>
  <Card title="Eazybe Integration" icon="plug">
    WhatsApp Integration by Eazybe installed in HubSpot
  </Card>
  <Card title="Approved Templates" icon="file-check">
    At least one approved WhatsApp message template
  </Card>
  <Card title="Contact Properties" icon="address-book">
    Contact properties configured for targeting
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Creating Your HubSpot Workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access HubSpot Account">
    Log into your HubSpot account and navigate to the main dashboard.

    ![Access HubSpot Account](/images/1933597299.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Navigate to Automation">
    Click on the **Automation** button in your HubSpot navigation menu.

    ![Navigate to Automation](/images/1933597300.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Workflows">
    Click on **Workflows** to access the workflow management area.

    ![Select Workflows](/images/1933597301.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create New Workflow">
    Click the **Create workflow** button to start building a new automation.

    ![Create New Workflow](/images/1933597302.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose From Scratch">
    Select **From scratch** to build a custom workflow tailored to your needs.

    ![Choose From Scratch](/images/1933597303.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Trigger Type">
    Click on the **Met filter criteria** option as your trigger type to target specific contacts.

    ![Set Trigger Type](/images/1933597304.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Object Type">
    Click on **Contact** as the object type for your workflow enrollment.

    ![Select Object Type](/images/1933597306.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Contact Properties">
    Click on **Contact properties** to define your targeting criteria.

    ![Choose Contact Properties](/images/1933597307.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Search for Property">
    Search for the contact property on which you want your workflow to be triggered.

    ![Search for Property](/images/1933597308.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Property Name">
    Click on the required property name (e.g., Company name) that you want to filter by.

    ![Select Property Name](/images/1933597309.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure Filter Conditions">
    Add your condition (e.g., **is equal to any of**) and then add the value under **Add values** to match the entered value with the contact property.

    ![Configure Filter Conditions](/images/1933597310.png)

    <Tip>
    You can add multiple values to target contacts from different companies or segments.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Continue Setup">
    Click **Next** to proceed to the enrollment settings.

    ![Continue Setup](/images/1933597311.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enable Re-enrollment">
    Turn on the **Re-enroll** option if you want to re-enroll all your old contacts under this workflow.

    ![Enable Re-enrollment](/images/1933597312.png)

    <Info>
    Re-enrollment allows contacts to go through the workflow multiple times when they meet the trigger criteria again.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save and Continue">
    Click the **Save and continue** button to finalize your trigger settings.

    ![Save and Continue](/images/1933597313.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add WhatsApp Action">
    Scroll down and click on the **WhatsApp Integration by Eazybe** button from the available actions.

    ![Add WhatsApp Action](/images/1933597314.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Template Action">
    Click **Send Ready-to-Use WhatsApp Template** to configure the message.

    ![Choose Template Action](/images/1933597315.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Contact Field">
    Select your contact field for sending the template.

    ![Select Contact Field](/images/1933597316.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Template">
    Select your approved WhatsApp template from the dropdown list.

    ![Choose Template](/images/1933597317.png)

    <Warning>
    Only templates with **Approved** status will appear in the list. Ensure your template is approved before creating the workflow.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save Action">
    Click **Save** to confirm your WhatsApp action configuration.

    ![Save Action](/images/1933597318.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review Workflow">
    Click the **Review and turn on** button to review your complete workflow setup.

    ![Review Workflow](/images/1933597319.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Enrollment Option">
    Choose your preferred enrollment option for the workflow.

    ![Choose Enrollment Option](/images/1933597320.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Final Checks">
    Click the **Skip to turn on** button or complete all the checks by clicking **Next**.

    ![Final Checks](/images/1933597322.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Activate Workflow">
    Click the **Turn on workflow** button to activate your WhatsApp campaign automation.

    ![Activate Workflow](/images/1933597323.png)

    <Info>
    Your HubSpot workflow is now ready for WhatsApp campaigns! Contacts matching your criteria will automatically receive WhatsApp messages.
    </Info>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Test Before Launch" icon="flask">
    Always test your workflow with a small group of contacts before launching to a larger audience.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Monitor Performance" icon="chart-line">
    Regularly check your workflow analytics to optimize message delivery and engagement rates.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Respect Opt-outs" icon="user-shield">
    Ensure your contact list respects customer preferences and opt-out requests.

    Personalize your templates with HubSpot contact properties for better engagement.
  </Accordion>
