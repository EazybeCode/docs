A **super-admin** on HubSpot has the authority to invite new users to the organisation and

manage their permissions. Here's how to grant permissions for seamless integration of HubSpot with EazyBe:

## Giving Permissions Through HubSpot

### Access the Settings Page

Log in to HubSpot and navigate to the **Settings **page.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1182407641.png" alt="" />
</Frame>
### Manage Users & Teams

In the settings, click on **Users & Teams**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1182409870.png" alt="" />
</Frame>
### Edit User Permissions

Locate the user whose permissions you want to manage and click on **Edit permissions** button beside their name

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1182411479.png" alt="" />
</Frame>
- ### Grant Required Permissions

Ensure that necessary permissions are granted, especially:

**File permissions and App Marketplace permissions**

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1182439947.png" alt="" />
</Frame>

These permissions are essential for ensuring smooth integration of EazyBe with HubSpot and to enable key functionalities like chat backup and CRM sync.
