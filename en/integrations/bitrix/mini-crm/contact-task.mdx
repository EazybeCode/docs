Do you have a pending task you want to save so you can track it anytime? Don't worry! The Task Section of EazyBe's Bitrix Integration comes to your rescue.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the WhatsApp Chat">
    Launch the WhatsApp conversation with your Bitrix contact where you want to create a task.

    ![Open WhatsApp Chat](/images/1224494345.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click on the Bitrix Icon">
    Select the Bitrix icon located in the EazyBe extension's right sidebar to access Bitrix features.

    ![Click Bitrix Icon](/images/1224494696.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access the Task Section">
    Navigate to the Tasks section. This is the last header under the Contact Section.

    ![Access Task Section](/images/1224497216.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add a Task">
    Click the **Add Task** button to create a new task.

    ![Add Task](/images/1224498450.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter the Task Details">
    Fill in all the required task information, then click **Save** to create the task.

    ![Enter Task Details](/images/1224501321.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="View Your Created Task">
    Once saved, your task will appear in the task list. You can confirm it was created successfully.

    ![View Created Task](/images/1224503000.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Manage Your Task">
    Use the available options to manage your tasks:
    - Click to view or hide task notes
    - Use the three-dot menu to edit or delete tasks

    ![Manage Task](/images/1224503222.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Keep your tasks organized by adding clear titles and detailed notes. This makes it easier to track pending work with your Bitrix contacts.
</Tip>
