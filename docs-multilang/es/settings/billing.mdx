This help article explains Eazybe’s flexible pricing structure, detailing the subscription plans, key features, and advanced integrations that empower WhatsApp business communication and CRM automation. It lets businesses choose a plan tailored to their needs with clear billing and support options.

## Eazybe Precios Instructions

Eazybe's pricing consists of three main components:

- **Subscription Fee** – The recurring cost for the selected plan, billed per WhatsApp number.
- **Features & Integrations** – Included in each plan from basic productivity to advanced CRM automation.
- **Support Levels & AI Automation** – Vary by plan to suit different service needs.

## 1. Subscription Fee (Monthly/Yearly)

The subscription fee depends on the plan selected:

- **Free Plan:** INR 0 /month
- **Starter Plan:** INR 760 /number/month (yearly billing) or INR 1160 (monthly billing)
- **Scaler Plan:** INR 1160 /number/month (yearly billing) or INR 1560 (monthly billing)

## 2. Plan Features Breakdown

## Free Plan

- Etiquetas: 1
- WhatsApp Business labels
- Quick reply with attachment: 1
- Scheduler (3 events while laptop is open)
- Support: None

## Starter Plan

- WhatsApp CRM:

Unlimited labels, scheduling, and quick replies
- Team inbox for collaborative chat management

WhatsApp Automation:

- Scheduled and bulk messaging
- Conectar WhatsApp Business App and API

CRM Integrations:

- HubSpot, Zoho, Bitrix, Google Sheets
- WhatsApp chat backup
- Send messages directly from CRM

Support: Email

## Scaler Plan

- Everything in Starter Plan, plus:
- WhatsApp Automation:

Workflow integration
- Dedicated API solution

CRM Integrations:

- Salesforce, Webhook
- Custom CRM modules within WhatsApp Mini CRM
- CRM Property-to-WhatsApp labeling providing visual context

AI Features:

- Unreplied Chat Agent categorizing chats and sending summary messages every 3 hours (customizable timing during business hours)

Support: Call and WhatsApp Group

## 3. About WhatsApp API and Business App Integration

- Convert your existing WhatsApp Business number to a WhatsApp API number to enable broadcasting without blocking risk.
- Continue managing daily chats through the familiar WhatsApp Business app environment.

## 4. About Advanced CRM Features

- View any custom CRM modules or objects directly within WhatsApp via the Mini CRM feature.
- Create labels based on any CRM contact, deal, or ticket property (currently HubSpot) that show in the extension and team inbox for quick access.
- Click on labels to view CRM contact details instantly within WhatsApp.

## 5. About Chats Sin Responder Automation

- The AI-powered agent categorizes unreplied chats as critical or non-critical.
- Admins receive WhatsApp message summaries every three hours to ensure timely responses.
- The timing of notifications is customizable and aligned to organizational working hours.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q1. What are the main components of Eazybe’s pricing?**

The pricing includes subscription fees, plan-based features, integrations, and support levels.

**Q2. Can I switch between monthly and yearly billing?**

Yes, you can choose to pay monthly or save with yearly billing.

**Q3. What CRM integrations are included?**

Starter plan includes HubSpot, Zoho, Bitrix, Google Sheets; Scaler plan adds Salesforce, Webhook, and custom WhatsApp modules.

**Q4. What support options are available?**

Free plan has no support; Starter offers email support; Scaler provides phone and WhatsApp group support.

**Q5. How does AI automation work?**

The AI-powered unreplied chat agent helps prioritize responses by categorizing chats and sending admin summaries.

For detailed pricing, features, and assistance, please visit your Eazybe HelpDesk or contact support.
