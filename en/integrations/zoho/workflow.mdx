Automate your WhatsApp messaging through Zoho workflows to send personalized customer communications triggered by specific business events. This guide walks you through setting up a workflow that sends WhatsApp messages automatically.

## Setting Up Zoho Workflows for WhatsApp

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Workflow Settings">
    Navigate to **Settings** → **Automation** → **Workflow Rules** in your Zoho CRM.

    ![Workflow Settings](/images/1291001886.jpg)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create a New Workflow Rule">
    Click on **Create Rule** and select the module you want to trigger the workflow from (Contacts, Deals, Leads, etc.). Give your workflow a descriptive name and define when it should execute (on record creation, edit, or on a scheduled basis).

    ![Create Workflow Rule](/images/1291002018.jpg)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure Trigger Criteria">
    Set up the conditions that will trigger the workflow. For example, you might want to send a WhatsApp message when a Contact Stage changes to "New Lead" or when a Deal reaches a specific stage.

    ![Trigger Configuration](/images/1290779158.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add the EazyBe WhatsApp Action">
    In the workflow actions section, select **messageonWhatsApp** from the available actions. This is the EazyBe integration action that enables WhatsApp messaging.

    ![Add WhatsApp Action](/images/1291002676.jpg)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure Sender Phone Number">
    Select the phone number that will send the WhatsApp messages. This must be a phone number that is already connected to your EazyBe account.

    ![Configure Sender](/images/1291003153.jpg)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Compose Your Message">
    Write your message content using dynamic Zoho fields to personalize the communication. You can use merge fields like `$First_Name`, `$Company`, or any other Zoho field to create personalized messages.

    ![Compose Message](/images/1291003379.jpg)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review and Activate">
    Review all your workflow configurations to ensure everything is set up correctly. Once verified, save and activate the workflow to start sending automated WhatsApp messages.

    ![Review and Activate](/images/1291003649.jpg)
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  The workflow will automatically send WhatsApp messages whenever the trigger conditions are met. Make sure your EazyBe account is properly connected and the sender phone number is active.
</Info>

<Tip>
  Use personalization tokens like `$First_Name` to make your automated messages feel more personal and increase engagement rates.
</Tip>
