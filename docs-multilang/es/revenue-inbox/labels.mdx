## Overview

Labels help you categorize and organize WhatsApp conversations for better workflow management. Eazybe supports both:

- **WhatsApp Business Labels** - Native labels from WA Business app
- **Eazybe Custom Labels** - Additional labels created in the extension

## Creating Labels

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    In WhatsApp Web with Eazybe extension, click **Settings** in the sidebar
    
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/1113048889.png" alt="Step 1" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enable Business Labels">
    Find **Show/Create Business Labels** and enable it
    
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/1113049106.png" alt="Step 2" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create New Label">
    Click the **+ Add Label** button
    
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/1113049302.png" alt="Step 3" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Name Your Label">
    Enter a label name and choose a color
    
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/1113049518.png" alt="Step 4" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save** to create the label
    
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/1113049922.png" alt="Step 5" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Applying Labels to Chats

1. Open any WhatsApp chat
2. Click the **Label Icon** in the Eazybe toolbar
3. Select one or more labels to apply
4. Labels appear next to the chat in your list

## Using Labels with Backup

Labels control what gets synced to Team Inbox:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Sync Label" icon="sync">
    Chats with this label backup to Team Inbox and CRM
  </Card>
  <Card title="Don't Sync Label" icon="ban">
    Chats excluded from backup (personal conversations)
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Label Visibility

| Visibility | Who Can See |
|------------|-------------|
| **Personal** | Only you |
| **Shared** | Everyone in your organization |
| **Team** | Only specific teams |

## Filtering by Label

In the Team Inbox, use the label filter to quickly find conversations:

1. Open **Team Inbox**
2. Click the **Filter** dropdown
3. Select one or more labels
4. View only matching conversations

<Tip>
Combine label filters with the **Unreplied** filter to find, for example, all "Hot Leads" that haven't been responded to.
</Tip>
