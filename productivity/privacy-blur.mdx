## Overview

Privacy Blur hides sensitive customer data when you're sharing your screen or working in public spaces.

## Enabling Privacy Blur

1. Click **Settings** (gear icon) in sidebar
2. Find **Privacy Blur** toggle
3. Turn ON

## What Gets Blurred

| Element | Blurred |
|---------|---------|
| Phone numbers | ✅ |
| Names | ✅ |
| Profile pictures | ✅ |
| Message content | ✅ |
| Attachments | ✅ |

## Quick Toggle

Press `Ctrl + Shift + B` (Windows) or `Cmd + Shift + B` (Mac) to quickly toggle blur on/off.

## Use Cases

- **Screen sharing**: Hide customer data during demos
- **Public spaces**: Work from cafes safely
- **Training**: Show workflows without exposing real data
- **Screenshots**: Capture screens without sensitive info

## Privacy Mode vs. Full Blur

| Mode | What's Hidden |
|------|---------------|
| **Privacy Mode** | Contact names and numbers only |
| **Full Blur** | All content including messages |

Select mode in Settings → Privacy → Blur Level.
