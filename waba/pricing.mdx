## Overview

This page outlines the current per-message pricing for WhatsApp broadcast communications through Eazybe across all supported regions. Use this reference to accurately forecast messaging costs.

<Info>
**Effective Date**: January 1, 2026. Rates are subject to change based on Meta's pricing updates.
</Info>

## Message Categories

WhatsApp charges based on message category:

| Category | Description | Cost |
|----------|-------------|------|
| **Marketing** | Promotional offers, product announcements, newsletters | Higher rate |
| **Utility** | Order updates, shipping notifications, appointment reminders | Lower rate |
| **Service** | Customer-initiated support conversations | Free (within 24-hour window) |

## Free Messages

The following messages are **free of charge**:

- **Service messages**: Customer-initiated conversations within the 24-hour window
- **Utility templates**: When sent within an active 24-hour customer service window
- **Free entry point conversations**: Messages from Click-to-WhatsApp ads (72-hour window)

## Managing Your Costs

### Eazybe Wallet

Pre-pay for messages and track spending in real-time through your Eazybe wallet. See [Wallet & Recharge](/waba/wallet) for details.

### Tips to Reduce Costs

1. **Encourage customer-initiated conversations** - Service messages are free
2. **Send utility messages within the 24-hour window** - They're free during active sessions
3. **Use Click-to-WhatsApp ads** - Get 72 hours of free messaging
4. **Target lower-cost regions** when possible
5. **Monitor your broadcast analytics** to optimize message effectiveness
