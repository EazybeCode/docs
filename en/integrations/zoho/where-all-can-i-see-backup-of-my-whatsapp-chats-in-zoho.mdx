---
title: "Where all can I see backup of my WhatsApp chats in ZOHO?"
description: ""
---

You can view the back up of your WhatsApp chats within ZOHO in several areas such as:

- 
#### ZOHO Contact's Page:

Each synced WhatsApp conversation will appear in the Notes Section in the respective ZOHO Contacts' Page.

  

- 
#### ZOHO Lead's Page:

Each synced WhatsApp conversation will appear in the Notes section in the respective ZOHO Leads' Page.

  

- 
#### ZOHO Deal's Page:

The chats will also be synced in the 5 latest deals of the respective ZOHO Contact.
