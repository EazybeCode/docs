The account section provides an overview of your profile, current plan, and expiry date. Access it by clicking the Eazybe icon in the top right corner.

![Account Overview](/images/1352831740.png)

## Adding an Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Other Profiles">
    Click the **Other Profiles** button in your account section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add New Account">
    Select **Add Account** and enter your details to link an additional profile.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Joining Another Organization

![Join Organization](/images/1352810290.png)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Initiate Join Process">
    Click **Join another Organization** from your account settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Organization Code">
    Enter the organization code provided by the target organization's administrator.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
**Share Your Organization Code**

Your employees can join your organization by using your unique Organization code. Simply copy the code from your account settings and share it with your team members.
</Info>
