## Introduction

This FAQ addresses the most common questions about using the WhatsApp Business Account (WABA) integration with Eazybe. Find answers to billing, verification, templates, and troubleshooting queries below.

---

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How does billing work? What is the Eazybe wallet?">
    All charges for WhatsApp template messages are managed through your **Eazybe wallet**.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Initial Credits">
        When you start, your organization receives **â‚¹100 or $1 free credits** from Eazybe to get started.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Usage Deductions">
        As you send template messages, funds are automatically deducted from your wallet balance.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Recharging">
        You can easily recharge your wallet from the **Billing** section in your dashboard when your balance runs low.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Tip>
    Monitor your wallet balance regularly to ensure uninterrupted messaging capabilities.
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I get the Green Tick on my WhatsApp profile?">
    **Yes.** Using an official WABA through Eazybe makes you eligible to apply for Meta's Green Tick verification.

    <Note>
    The Green Tick verification process is handled through **Meta Business Manager** once your account is active and meets Meta's notability criteria.
    </Note>

    **Requirements for Green Tick:**
    - Active WABA account
    - Verified business on Meta Business Manager
    - Meeting Meta's notability standards
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Why was my Message Template rejected?">
    Meta enforces strict policies for message templates. Common rejection reasons include:

    | Reason | Description |
    |--------|-------------|
    | **Promotional Content** | Using promotional content in non-marketing template categories |
    | **Sensitive Data Requests** | Asking for sensitive personal or financial data |
    | **Incorrect Formatting** | Template formatting does not meet Meta's guidelines |

    <Warning>
    You can create a new template by updating the content to comply with Meta's policies and resubmit it for approval.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="My CSV upload failed. Why?">
    CSV upload failures typically occur due to formatting issues. Ensure your contact list meets these requirements:

    <Steps>
      <Step title="File Format">
        The file must be in `.csv` format.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Column Name">
        The file must contain a column specifically named **"Phone"**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Phone Number Format">
        Phone numbers must include the country code but **without** the `+` sign.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    **Example Format:**
    ```
    Phone
    91xxxxxxxxxx
    44xxxxxxxxxx
    1xxxxxxxxxx
    ```

    <Warning>
    Do not include the `+` symbol before the country code. Use `91xxxxxxxxxx` instead of `+91xxxxxxxxxx`.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Why did my WABA Coexistence account disconnect?">
    Your account may disconnect for several reasons related to security and compliance with Meta's guidelines:

    | Reason | Description |
    |--------|-------------|
    | **Switching Mobile Devices** | Your connection may be lost when moving your WhatsApp Business account to a new phone |
    | **Parallel App and Web Use** | Using the mobile app and WhatsApp Web simultaneously can cause connection issues |
    | **Inactivity** | The connection can expire if the WhatsApp Business App is not opened for over 14 days |
    | **Policy Violations** | Violating WhatsApp's Business Policy through spam or prohibited actions leads to disconnection |
    | **Switching App Types** | Moving your number between the consumer and Business apps can sever the connection |

    <Tip>
    To maintain a stable connection, regularly open your WhatsApp Business App and avoid switching between consumer and business versions.
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
