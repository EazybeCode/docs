This guide explains how to view and recharge your Eazybe Wallet balance to ensure uninterrupted WhatsApp broadcast campaigns.

## How the Eazybe Wallet Works

All charges for sending WhatsApp broadcasts are managed through the Eazybe Wallet. Your wallet balance is deducted each time you send broadcast messages.

Once your free credits are exhausted, you'll need to add funds to continue sending broadcasts.

## Viewing Your Wallet Balance

Navigate to the **Billing** section in your Eazybe dashboard to view your current balance and transaction history.

## How to Recharge Your Wallet

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Billing">
    Access the **Billing** section in the Eazybe dashboard. Here you can view your current balance and complete transaction history.

    ![Billing Section](/images/1589170996.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Add Credits">
    Select the **+ Add Credits** button to initiate a recharge.

    ![Add Credits Button](/images/1589172811.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Recharge Amount">
    Input your desired recharge amount and click **Purchase Credits**.

    ![Enter Amount](/images/1589173303.png)

    <Tip>
    Consider adding a larger amount to avoid frequent recharges and ensure your campaigns run smoothly.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete Payment">
    You will be redirected to Stripe's secure payment gateway to complete your transaction.

    ![Stripe Payment](/images/1589175484.png)

    <Info>
    Stripe provides enterprise-grade security for all payment transactions.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirmation">
    After successful payment, your new balance will be reflected in your wallet immediately.

    ![Payment Confirmation](/images/1589177170.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Ensure your wallet has sufficient balance before scheduling broadcast campaigns to avoid interruptions.
</Warning>
