### Overview
Eazybe’s Broadcast feature lets you quickly send a WhatsApp message or template to a group of contacts directly from the extension. This is ideal for marketing announcements, alerts, or operational updates.

---

### Instructions

### Step 1: Open Broadcast in the Eazybe Extension

- On WhatsApp Web with Eazybe extension, locate the top bar.
- Click the **Broadcast** icon or menu.

  

- Select **Broadcast messages** from the Menu. 

  

### Step 2: Enter Broadcast Details

- Specify a unique broadcast name for your message (this helps track and manage previous broadcasts).
- From the **Template** dropdown, choose the message template or quick reply you want to send.
- From the **Send to** dropdown, select the group or label of contacts you wish to reach (e.g., “Leads”, “VIP Customers”, etc.).
- Review all details to ensure accuracy.

  

### Step 3: Send Your Broadcast

- Click the **Broadcast** button at the bottom of the popup.
- Your message will be queued with a progress bar and delivered via WhatsApp to all selected contacts under the chosen label.

  

### Step 4: Check Your Broadcast Analytics

- Click the **Broadcast** icon or menu.

  

- Select **Analytics** from the Menu. 

  

- Check the Required Broadcast in matrix format which provide the **total message sent, delivered, failed, read** and the broadcast campagain status as** Finished, In Progress or Failed.**

  

---

### Steps to consider

Make sure the broadcast name is descriptive and unique for tracking campaign analytics.
- Templates should be pre-approved for WhatsApp to avoid delivery issues.
- Use labels to segment your audience for targeted messaging.
