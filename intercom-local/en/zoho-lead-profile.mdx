The profile section of the Zoho Lead will show the basic contact details of your lead. To access and edit the profile section, you should ensure that the Lead is created on ZOHO and the ZOHO CRM is integrated with EazyBe.

- Open the WhatsApp conversation of your Zoho Lead.
- Click on the Zoho icon on the right-side bar.

  

- The first header that opens up under the Lead section will be the profile one.
- You can view and edit user properties for your lead and can save the changes by clicking on the Save button below.

  

You also get an option to directly view the Lead on ZOHO by clicking on the View Lead on ZOHO button and it will open up a new tab  and will redirect you to your ZOHO lead's page.
