As an organization administrator, you can monitor which team members have connected their Zoho accounts to the EazyBe WhatsApp Extension.

## Viewing Connected Team Members

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log into EazyBe Workspace">
    Access the EazyBe Workspace using your administrator credentials.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Navigate to Dashboard">
    Go to the **Dashboard** section where complete information on your team's Zoho usage is displayed.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check CRM Integrated Column">
    Look for the **CRM integrated** column in the dashboard table. Team members who have connected their Zoho accounts will display a Zoho icon in this column.

    ![Dashboard with CRM Integration Status](/images/1192633018.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  The dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of all team members and their Zoho connection status, making it easy to track who has completed the integration setup.
</Info>

<Tip>
  If a team member's Zoho icon is not visible, they may need to complete the integration process. Share the setup guide with them to help them connect their account.
</Tip>
