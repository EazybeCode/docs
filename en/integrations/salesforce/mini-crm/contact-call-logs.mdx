This guide explains how to add Call Logs for your Salesforce Contacts using the EazyBe WhatsApp Extension. Track all your call activities directly from WhatsApp.

## Adding a Call Log

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the WhatsApp Chat">
    Access the WhatsApp conversation with your Salesforce Contact.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click on the Salesforce Icon">
    Select the Salesforce icon in the right sidebar of the extension.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access the Call Logs Section">
    In the Contact Section, locate the **Call Logs** header at the bottom and click it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a New Call Log">
    Click the **Add Call Log** button to open the call log form.

    ![Add Call Log Button](/images/1116542441.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Call Log">
    Enter the required information and click the **Save Call Log** button.

    ![Save Call Log Form](/images/1116542775.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Your Created Call Log">
    The newly created call log becomes visible in the Call Logs section.

    ![Created Call Log](/images/1116543261.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing Call Logs

Once a call log is created, you can manage it using the three-dot menu next to each entry:
- **Edit** - Modify call log details
- **Delete** - Remove the call log

<Info>
  Call logs are synced directly to Salesforce when you save. The call log information will be reflected in your Salesforce Contact record.
</Info>
