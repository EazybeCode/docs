## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sync Conversations">
    Team members sync their WhatsApp chats via the Chrome extension or WABA connection
  </Step>
  <Step title="AI Analysis">
    Eazybe's AI scans every message for buying signals, complaints, urgency keywords, and more
  </Step>
  <Step title="Auto-Labeling">
    Conversations are automatically tagged as **Critical**, **Hot Lead**, **Follow-up Needed**, etc.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manager Visibility">
    All tagged conversations appear in the Revenue Inbox with priority filters
  </Step>
  <Step title="Action & Alerts">
    Managers get WhatsApp notifications every 3 hours with unreplied chat summaries
  </Step>
</Steps>

## AI Detection Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Hot Lead Detection" icon="fire">
    The AI identifies buying signals like:
    - "I want to buy"
    - "What's the price?"
    - "Can I pay today?"
    - "Send me the invoice"
    - High-value customer from CRM data
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Escalation Detection" icon="triangle-exclamation">
    Flags conversations containing:
    - "Cancel my order"
    - "I want a refund"
    - "This is unacceptable"
    - "Let me speak to a manager"
    - Negative sentiment indicators
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Unreplied Detection" icon="clock">
    Monitors conversations where:
    - Customer sent last message
    - No team reply within SLA threshold
    - Priority increases over time
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Follow-up Detection" icon="bell">
    Tracks conversations needing action:
    - Scheduled follow-up reminders
    - Promised callbacks
    - Pending quotes or proposals
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
