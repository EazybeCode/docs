## Overview

Privacy Blur is a security feature that obscures contact names and profile pictures on your screen. Perfect for:
- Working in public spaces
- Screen sharing during demos
- Recording tutorials
- Protecting customer privacy

## How to Enable Privacy Blur

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Click the **Settings** button (gear icon) in the Eazybe sidebar
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Find Security Settings">
    Scroll to the **Security Settings** section
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enable Blur Options">
    - **Blur Contact Name** - Blurs contact names in chats and lists
    - **Blur Contact Image** - Blurs profile pictures
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose Scope">
    Select who the settings apply to:
    - Everyone in the organization
    - Everyone except admin
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What Gets Blurred

| Element | When Blurred |
|---------|--------------|
| Contact names | In chat list, headers, and messages |
| Phone numbers | In contact details and chat headers |
| Profile pictures | In chat list and contact cards |
| Group member names | In group info and participant lists |
| About section | Contact's status/bio text |

## Additional Settings

Beyond blur, the Settings panel offers:

### Customizable Features
- **AI Button** - Toggle AI suggestions on/off
- **Make Full Screen** - Expand WhatsApp to fill the browser
- **Add Signature** - Automatically append your signature to messages
- **Hide All Label** - Remove the "All" label from the top bar

### Signature Setup
1. Enable **Add Signature** in settings
2. Enter your desired signature text
3. Click the checkmark to save
4. Your signature will appear above every message you send

<Info>
The signature feature doesn't support emojis currently. Keep your signature professional and concise.
</Info>

## Admin Controls

Organization admins can enforce privacy settings across the team:
- Apply blur settings to all employees
- Exempt admins from blur requirements
- Set organization-wide defaults

This ensures consistent privacy practices across your entire team.
