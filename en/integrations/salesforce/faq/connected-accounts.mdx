---

The organization's administrator can view which employees have connected their Salesforce accounts to the Eazybe WhatsApp Extension by accessing the Eazybe Workspace.

## Prerequisites

<Note>
  The admin must log in to their account on the [Eazybe Workspace](https://workspace.eazybe.com/).
</Note>

## Go to the Dashboard Section

The dashboard provides complete team Salesforce usage information. The Salesforce icon appears in the table under the **"CRM Integrated"** column.

![Dashboard - Connected Accounts](/images/1196802617.png)

<Tip>
  From the dashboard, you can quickly identify which team members have successfully integrated their Salesforce accounts and monitor the overall adoption across your organization.
</Tip>
