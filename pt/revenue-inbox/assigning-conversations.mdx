## Assigning Conversations

Assign conversations to specific team members to ensure accountability and workload distribution.

### How to Assign

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Conversation">
    Click on the conversation in Team Inbox
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Assign">
    Click the **Assign** button or dropdown
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Team Member">
    Choose the team member to assign
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    The conversation is now assigned
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Assignment Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Auto-Assignment** | Route new chats based on rules |
| **Round Robin** | Distribute evenly across team |
| **Skills-Based** | Assign based on expertise |
| **Manual** | Admin assigns directly |

### Filtering by Assignment

In Team Inbox, filter conversations by:
- **Assigned to me** - See your assignments
- **Unassigned** - Chats needing assignment
- **Assigned to [Name]** - Specific team member

### Assignment Notifications

When assigned a conversation:
- Email notification (if enabled)
- In-app notification
- Appears in "My Conversations" filter

<Warning>
Only admins and managers can assign conversations to others. Agents can only see their own assigned chats.
</Warning>
