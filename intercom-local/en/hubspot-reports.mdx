You can easily track your sales team activity on WhatsApp. Wondering how, EazyBe's HubSpot Integration allows you to access HubSpot Reports and you can generate reports on HubSpot. 
How to do so?
1. Ensure your HubSpot is connected with EazyBe.

2. Go to HubSpot -> Reports.

3. Click on the Create Report button on the top.

4. You can create the report for the specific activity as per your use case.

Note: You can create report for multiple activities based on your requirements and can track the data for the same.

5. Select the activity template for which you want to create a report for.
​
The different templates for which you can create a report on HubSpot are:

i) Activity of recently created contacts
This will give you a brief description of the contacts that have submitted a form and all the details, along with create date, lifecycle stage, etc., for the contact can be seen in this section.

  

ii) Contacts created totals by first conversion

This will give an analytics about the contacts that are submitting the user form. You can view the Count of contacts in the graphical representation as given below.

  

iii) Contacts total with time comparison

This will give you a demonstration of the number of leads and customers created recently. There are multiple filters that you can use to track the metrics.

  

iv) Contacts most recently created by source
This will give you a demonstration of the sources that have been most beneficial for you when creating contacts. This will give a pie chart comparing various sources of contact creation and will represent the data accordingly.
​

  

There are a lot more activities for which you can create your HubSpot reports.

6. You can click on the activity and it will open up the activity's window. Customize the same as per your requirements.

7. Click on Save and select the necessary settings for your organisation.

8. Once created, your reports will be visible in the Reports page.
