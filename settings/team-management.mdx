## Team Management

Add team members to your Eazybe organization for collaborative WhatsApp management.

### Inviting Team Members

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings">
    Open Eazybe Workspace → Settings → Team
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Invite">
    Click **Invite Team Member**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Details">
    Provide:
    - Name
    - Email
    - WhatsApp Number
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign Role">
    Select Admin, Manager, or Agent
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send Invite">
    Team member receives email invitation
  </Step>
</Steps>

### What Team Members Get

When you invite employees, they get access to:

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Shared Labels** | Access team labels and funnels |
| **Quick Replies** | Use shared quick reply templates |
| **Team Inbox** | View assigned conversations |
| **Analytics** | See their own performance metrics |

### Chat Backup & Monitoring

<Info>
Chats from team members' WhatsApp numbers are backed up to the Admin's Google Drive and visible in Team Inbox.
</Info>

This enables:
- **Conversation monitoring** - See all team chats
- **Quality assurance** - Review customer interactions
- **Training** - Identify coaching opportunities
- **Compliance** - Maintain records of all communications

### Data Privacy

Eazybe does not store data on its servers. All data is:
- Backed up with the user's WhatsApp number
- Stored securely in the Admin's Google Drive

<Warning>
Only Admins can view all team conversations. Agents can only see their own chats.
</Warning>
