---

If you need to disconnect your Zoho CRM integration from EazyBe, follow these simple steps.

## Disconnecting Zoho Integration

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Integration Panel">
    Click on the **integration button** in the right sidebar of the EazyBe extension panel.

    ![Integration Button](/images/1192609871.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Disconnect">
    Click on the **Disconnect button** under the Zoho section.

    ![Disconnect Button](/images/1223657903.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm Disconnection">
    A prompt will appear asking you to confirm the disconnection. Click **Confirm** to complete the process.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Disconnecting the integration will stop syncing contacts and messages between WhatsApp and Zoho CRM. Your existing data in Zoho will not be deleted.
</Warning>

<Info>
  You can reconnect the integration at any time by following the setup process again. All your settings will need to be reconfigured after reconnecting.
</Info>
