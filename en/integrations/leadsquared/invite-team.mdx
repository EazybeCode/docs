## How to Invite Team Members

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Organization Settings">
    Navigate to the organization panel at [workspace.eazybe.com/organization](https://workspace.eazybe.com/organization).

    ![Organization Settings](/images/1346582786.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Employee Management">
    Locate and click on **Employees â†’ Add Employees** in the interface.

    ![Add Employees Option](/images/1346582785.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Details and Send Invitation">
    Fill in the required information for each team member:
    - **Name** (required)
    - **Email** (required)
    - **WhatsApp Number** (required)

    Click **Add** to add the employee, then send the invitation.

    ![Employee Details Form](/images/1346582787.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What Happens After Inviting

<Info>
  Once team members accept the invitation and connect their WhatsApp:
  - They can access shared Quick Replies and Labels
  - Their chats are backed up from their WhatsApp number to the Admin's Google Drive
  - You can assign tasks to them through LeadSquared
</Info>

<Tip>
  EazyBe does not store any data on its servers. All chat backups are stored directly in your Google Drive for maximum privacy and security.
</Tip>
