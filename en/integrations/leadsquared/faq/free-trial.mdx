Yes! EazyBe offers a free trial period so you can test the LeadSquared integration before committing to a subscription.

## Trial Period

<Info>
  EazyBe provides a **4-day free trial** for all new users to test the LeadSquared integration.
</Info>

## Extend Your Trial

Want more time to evaluate the integration? You can extend your trial period by an additional 4 days.

<Tip>
  **How to extend your trial:** Simply rate us on the Chrome Web Store, and your trial will be extended by another 4 days, giving you a total of 8 days to fully test the integration.
</Tip>

## What's Included in the Trial

During your free trial, you'll have access to:

- Full WhatsApp to LeadSquared sync functionality
- Mini-CRM features for lead management
- Message backup capabilities
- All core integration features
