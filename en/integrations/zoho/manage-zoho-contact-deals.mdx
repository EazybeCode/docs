---

You can easily manage the deals associated with a ZOHO Contact directly from your WhatsApp Chat.

Steps to Manage Deals:

- 
#### Open the WhatsApp Chat
Open the WhatsApp Chat of the ZOHO Contact whose deals you want to manage.

- 
#### Click on the ZOHO Icon
In the right sidebar of the extension, click on the ZOHO icon.

  

- 
#### Access the Deals Section
Under the **Contact** header, select the **Deals Section** to see the existing deals.

  

- 
#### Edit a Deal
To modify a deal, 

- Click on the three dots next to the specific deal.

  

- Click on the **pencil** icon to open the deal's details.
**Edit** the properties and add new ones if needed.

  

- 
#### Save Changes
After making the necessary changes, click on the **Save** button to update the deal.

  

**Note**: You can also view the chat backup in the **5** most recent deals of your ZOHO Contact.
