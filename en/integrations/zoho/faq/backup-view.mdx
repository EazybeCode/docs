Synced WhatsApp conversations are stored in multiple locations within Zoho CRM, making it easy to access chat history from different record types.

## Where to Find Your WhatsApp Chat Backups

<Steps>
  <Step title="Zoho Contact's Page">
    Each synced WhatsApp conversation appears in the **Notes Section** of the respective Zoho Contact's page.

    ![Zoho Contact Notes Section](/images/1192638821.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Zoho Lead's Page">
    Synced conversations also display in the **Notes Section** of respective lead records in Zoho CRM.

    ![Zoho Lead Notes Section](/images/1192642992.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Zoho Deal's Page">
    Chats are automatically synced to the **5 latest deals** associated with the respective Zoho Contact.

    ![Zoho Deal Notes Section](/images/1192645730.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  All WhatsApp conversations are stored as notes, allowing you to maintain a complete communication history within your CRM records.
</Info>
