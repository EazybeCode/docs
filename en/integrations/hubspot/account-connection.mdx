## Overview

Connecting Eazybe to your HubSpot account enables powerful WhatsApp integration, allowing you to manage conversations, sync contacts, and streamline your customer communication workflow directly from HubSpot.

<Note>
Before proceeding, ensure you have completed all the [prerequisites](/en/integrations/hubspot/prerequisites) for the HubSpot integration.
</Note>

## Step-by-Step Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Eazybe Extension">
    Open WhatsApp Web and click on the Eazybe extension icon to access the extension panel.

    <img src="/images/1112616161.png" alt="Eazybe Extension Panel" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Navigate to Integrations">
    In the Eazybe panel, navigate to the **Integrations** section and select **HubSpot** from the available CRM options.

    <img src="/images/1112616321.png" alt="HubSpot Integration Selection" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Sign In to HubSpot">
    Click the **Connect HubSpot** button. You will be redirected to the HubSpot login page.

    <img src="/images/1175564015.png" alt="HubSpot Sign In" />

    Enter your HubSpot credentials and sign in to your account.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Authorize Eazybe">
    After signing in, HubSpot will ask you to authorize Eazybe to access your account. Review the permissions and click **Connect App** to proceed.

    <img src="/images/1175569765.png" alt="Authorize Eazybe App" />

    <Info>
    Eazybe requires access to your contacts and files to enable WhatsApp-HubSpot synchronization.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select HubSpot Account">
    If you have multiple HubSpot accounts, select the account you want to connect with Eazybe.

    <img src="/images/1175570125.png" alt="Select HubSpot Account" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm Connection">
    Once authorized, you'll be redirected back to WhatsApp Web. The Eazybe panel will show a confirmation that your HubSpot account is now connected.

    <img src="/images/1182331572.png" alt="Connection Confirmed" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify Integration">
    Verify the integration by checking that your HubSpot contacts are accessible from the Eazybe panel.

    <img src="/images/1182332364.png" alt="Integration Verified" />

    <Check>
    Your HubSpot account is now successfully connected to Eazybe!
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Connection Failed">
    If the connection fails, ensure you have the required permissions in your HubSpot account. Check the [prerequisites](/en/integrations/hubspot/prerequisites) page for details on required permissions.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Authorization Denied">
    If you accidentally denied authorization, you can retry by going to Integrations → HubSpot and clicking Connect HubSpot again.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Multiple Accounts">
    If you have multiple HubSpot accounts, make sure you select the correct account during the connection process.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
