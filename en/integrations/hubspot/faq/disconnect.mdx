## How Do I Disconnect the Integration?

If you need to disconnect your HubSpot integration from the Eazybe WhatsApp extension, follow these simple steps.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Integration Panel">
    Click on the **integration button** in the right sidebar of your WhatsApp Web interface.

    ![Integration Icon](/images/1185312671.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Disconnect HubSpot">
    Click on the **Disconnect button** under the HubSpot section. A prompt will appear asking you to confirm the disconnection.

    ![Disconnect Process](/images/1185312864.gif)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm Disconnection">
    Confirm the disconnection when prompted. Your HubSpot integration will be removed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Disconnecting the integration will stop syncing your WhatsApp chats with HubSpot. You can reconnect at any time by following the integration setup process again.
</Warning>
