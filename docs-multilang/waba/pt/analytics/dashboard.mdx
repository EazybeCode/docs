## Overview

## Accessing the Dashboard

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Workspace">
    Click the **Organization** button in the Eazybe sidebar to open the workspace
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Login">
    Sign in with your admin credentials
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Navigate to Analytics">
    Click **Analytics** in the left sidebar
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Key Metrics

### Message Analytics

| Metric | Description | Why It Matters |
|--------|-------------|----------------|
| **Sent Messages** | Total outbound messages | Volume of outreach |
| **Received Messages** | Total inbound messages | Customer engagement |
| **Follow-up Messages** | Scheduled/repeated messages | Persistence & nurturing |
| **Response Rate** | Replies / Sent messages | Message effectiveness |

### Conversation Analytics

| Metric | Description | Why It Matters |
|--------|-------------|----------------|
| **1:1 Chats** | Individual conversations | Direct customer engagement |
| **Group Chats** | Group message activity | Team/broadcast reach |
| **Active Conversations** | Chats with recent activity | Current workload |
| **Average Response Time** | Time to first reply | Service quality |

### Call Analytics

| Metric | Description | Why It Matters |
|--------|-------------|----------------|
| **Total Calls** | Voice/video calls made | Communication mix |
| **Call Duration** | Average call length | Conversation depth |
| **Missed Calls** | Unanswered incoming calls | Availability issues |

## Filtering Options

Filter your analytics by:
- **Date Range** - Custom period, last 7/30/90 days
- **Team Member** - Individual or whole team
- **Conversation Type** - 1:1, Groups, or All
- **Labels** - Specific contact segments
