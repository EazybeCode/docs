<Info>
**Setup Time**: 20 minutes | **Difficulty**: Medium | **Requires**: WABA
</Info>

## Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Message Template">
    Create approved template in Eazybe WABA section
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create Automation Rule">
    In LeadSquared: **Automation** → **Rules** → **Create Rule**
    
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/leadsquared/automation-rule.png" alt="Automation rule" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Webhook Action">
    Configure webhook to Eazybe endpoint
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Common Examples

- New lead → Welcome WhatsApp
- Stage change → Status update
- No activity 7 days → Re-engagement
