The Lead Info section of the Bitrix lead will show the basic contact details. To access and edit the lead info section, you should ensure that the lead is created.


- Open the WhatsApp chat of your Bitrix lead.


- Click on the Bitrix icon on the right-side bar.


- The first header that opens up under the Lead section will be the lead info section.


- You can view and edit user properties for your lead and can save the changes by clicking on the Save button below.


- You can also view your Bitrix lead in the Bitrix by clicking on the button as mentioned below.
