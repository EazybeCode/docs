Access and manage your Zoho Contact profiles directly from WhatsApp using the EazyBe extension. View basic contact details and update information without leaving your conversation.

## How to Access the Contact Profile

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the WhatsApp Chat">
    Open the WhatsApp conversation with your Zoho Contact whose profile you want to view or edit.

    ![Open WhatsApp Chat](/images/1217629398.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click on the Zoho Icon">
    In the right-side panel of the EazyBe extension, locate and click on the **Zoho icon** to access your CRM features.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access the Profile Section">
    Click on the **Contact** header. The first section that opens will be the **Profile Section**, displaying the contact's basic details.

    ![Contact Profile Section](/images/1113027329.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Viewing and Editing Contact Details

Once in the Profile Section, you can:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="View Properties" icon="eye">
    See all user properties and contact information at a glance
  </Card>
  <Card title="Edit Properties" icon="pen-to-square">
    Modify contact details directly from the extension
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Edit User Properties">
    Make changes to any editable contact fields as needed.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save Changes">
    After making your changes, click the **Save** button to update the contact profile in Zoho.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Changes made to the contact profile are automatically synced with your Zoho CRM, keeping your records up to date across platforms.
</Tip>
