## Freshdesk Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Integrations">
    Click **Integrations** in Eazybe panel
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Freshdesk">
    Click **Connect to Freshdesk**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize">
    Login and grant permissions
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure">
    Set sync preferences
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify">
    Open a chat to verify connection
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
Issues? See [Troubleshooting](/integrations/freshdesk/troubleshooting).
</Info>
