# Setup and Details

This guide walks you through the complete process of creating, training, and deploying your AI Knowledge Base Agent on WhatsApp.

## Overview

AI Agents by Eazybe enable businesses to build intelligent, automated agents that operate directly on WhatsApp, trained using your own business data.

These agents are designed to:

- Handle customer conversations automatically
- Assist in sales and support workflows
- Learn from your existing knowledge and chat history
- Deliver human-like responses based on real interactions

<Note>
  This feature represents the foundation of advanced AI agents. Businesses can later unlock deeper personalization, CRM integrations, and fully customized automation systems.
</Note>

## Step-by-Step: Create Your AI Agent

Follow these steps to set up and deploy your AI Knowledge Base Agent:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access AI Agents">
    Navigate to the <strong>AI Agents</strong> section in your dashboard and click on <strong>Create another agent</strong> or <strong>Add Agent</strong>.

    ![AI Agents dashboard link](/images/ai-agents/image1.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Agent">
    Click on <strong>Add Agent</strong> in your dashboard to begin creating your AI agent.

    ![Add Agent button](/images/ai-agents/image2.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Knowledge Source">
    Choose one of the following methods to train your agent:
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Website URL</strong> – Enter your website URL to scrape content</li>
      <li><strong>Upload Knowledge Base</strong> – Upload documents (PDF, DOCX, TXT, CSV)</li>
      <li><strong>Connect Existing Knowledge Base</strong> – Link to your current resources</li>
    </ul>

    ![Add knowledge source options](/images/ai-agents/image3.png)
    ![Upload or connect knowledge base](/images/ai-agents/image4.png)

    <Tip>
      This helps the agent understand your business and provide accurate responses.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Use Case">
    Choose the primary purpose of your agent:
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Customer Support (CS)</strong> – Handles queries, complaints, FAQs</li>
      <li><strong>Sales Agent</strong> – Converts leads, shares product info, follows up</li>
    </ul>

    ![Select use case options](/images/ai-agents/image5.png)

    Click <strong>Next Step</strong> to continue.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Sync WhatsApp Chats (Most Important)">
    Enable <strong>Sync WhatsApp Chats</strong> to allow the agent to learn from your historical conversations. This is a critical step that enables the agent to:
    <ul>
      <li>Learn real customer tone</li>
      <li>Understand frequently asked questions</li>
      <li>Replicate your team's communication style</li>
    </ul>

    ![Sync WhatsApp chats option](/images/ai-agents/image6.png)
    ![Select conversations to learn from](/images/ai-agents/image7.png)

    Select the conversations you want the agent to learn from and click <strong>Continue</strong>.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure AI Model">
    Click on <strong>AI Brain</strong> and select your AI agent model. Choose the appropriate model based on your requirements and expected conversation volume.

    ![AI Brain model selection](/images/ai-agents/image8.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Agent Type">
    Confirm the role of your agent:
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Customer Support (CS)</strong> – Handles queries, complaints, FAQs</li>
      <li><strong>Sales Agent</strong> – Converts leads, shares product info, follows up</li>
    </ul>

    ![Agent type selection](/images/ai-agents/image9.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Deployment Number">
    Choose the WhatsApp number you want to deploy the AI agent on. This should be a connected WhatsApp Business API (WABA) number.

    ![Select deployment number](/images/ai-agents/image10.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Customize System Instructions">
    Edit the <strong>System Instructions</strong> based on your specific use cases. This defines how the agent behaves and responds to customer queries.

    ![System instructions editor](/images/ai-agents/image11.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Finalize Training">
    Confirm your selected data sources and click <strong>Launch Agent</strong>. The system will automatically train your agent.

    ![Launch agent button](/images/ai-agents/image12.png)

    <Warning>
      Training typically takes just a few minutes. Do not close the browser during this process.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Test in Playground">
    Click on <strong>Playground</strong> to interact with your agent before going live. Use this opportunity to:
    <ul>
      <li>Validate response quality</li>
      <li>Test edge cases</li>
      <li>Make adjustments to system instructions if needed</li>
    </ul>

    ![Playground testing interface](/images/ai-agents/image13.png)
    ![Sources knowledge review](/images/ai-agents/image14.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review WhatsApp Number">
    Click on <strong>WhatsApp</strong> to verify the connected number.

    ![Connected WhatsApp number](/images/ai-agents/image15.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review Billing">
    Click on <strong>Billing</strong> to review your plan.

    ![Billing information](/images/ai-agents/image16.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Publish Your Agent">
    Click <strong>Publish</strong> to make your AI agent live.

    ![Publish agent button](/images/ai-agents/image17.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## After Deployment

Once deployed:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Live Operation">
    Your AI agent is now active and will respond to customer messages on the selected WhatsApp number.

    ![Agent live dashboard](/images/ai-agents/image18.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Internal Testing">
    Your team can also interact with the agent internally to monitor performance.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Continuous Learning">
    The agent responds instantly using its trained knowledge base. Monitor conversations and re-sync data sources as needed to keep the agent up-to-date.
  </Step>
</Steps>
