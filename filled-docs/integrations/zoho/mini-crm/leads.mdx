To create a new lead that is not already present on ZOHO using EazyBe WhatsApp extension:

- Open the WhatsApp chat of your respective contact.

- Click on the Zoho icon in the right side-bar.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1112651537.png" alt="" />
</Frame>

- The Lead Section will open up.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1112651863.png" alt="" />
</Frame>

- Fill in the necessary details and click on the Create Lead button. Your new ZOHO lead will be created.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1112656078.png" alt="" />
</Frame>

- You also have an option to view the Lead directly in the Zoho by clicking on the View Lead on Zoho button as given below.
