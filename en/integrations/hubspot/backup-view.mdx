## Where Can I See My WhatsApp Chat Backups in HubSpot?

<Check>
Your WhatsApp chat backups are accessible in **three primary locations** within HubSpot's interface.
</Check>

---

## Backup Locations

<Steps>
  <Step title="HubSpot Contact Page">
    Synced WhatsApp conversations appear in the **WhatsApp Activity section** of the HubSpot contact's timeline. This allows you to access all message backups and interaction records for each contact.

    ![WhatsApp Activity section on HubSpot Contact Page](/images/1178973615.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="HubSpot Deals Section">
    When a contact has associated deals, their chat records display in the **latest deal on the Deals Page**. This helps you track conversations related to specific business opportunities.

    ![Chat backup in HubSpot Deals section](/images/1178986333.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="HubSpot Company Page">
    Chat data also appears on the **Company profile page** within HubSpot, giving you a comprehensive view of all communications at the company level.

    ![Chat backup on HubSpot Company Page](/images/1178987523.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>
