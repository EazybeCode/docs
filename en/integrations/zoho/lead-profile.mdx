## Overview

The Profile Section displays basic contact details for a Zoho Lead. This feature allows you to view and modify lead properties without leaving your WhatsApp conversation.

<Note>
  The lead must be created in Zoho first before you can access their profile through the Mini-CRM view.
</Note>

## Accessing the Lead Profile

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open WhatsApp Chat">
    Access the WhatsApp conversation for the contact whose lead profile you want to view.

    ![Open WhatsApp Chat](/images/1112961374.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click on the Zoho Icon">
    Click on the **Zoho icon** located in the right sidebar of the Eazybe extension to open the Mini-CRM panel.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Visit the Profile Section">
    The Profile section appears as the first header within the Zoho Mini-CRM View's Lead Section. Here you can see all the basic contact details.

    ![Lead Profile Section](/images/1112962128.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Edit User Properties">
    View and modify user properties for your Zoho Lead:
    - Update contact information
    - Modify lead details
    - Add or change custom fields

    Click the **Save** button to apply any changes made.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Profile Information

The lead profile typically includes:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Name** | Lead's full name |
| **Email** | Primary email address |
| **Phone** | Contact phone number |
| **Company** | Associated company name |
| **Lead Status** | Current status in your pipeline |
| **Lead Source** | How the lead was acquired |

<Tip>
  Keep your lead profiles updated to ensure your team has the most accurate information during conversations.
</Tip>
