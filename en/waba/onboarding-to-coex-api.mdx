## Prerequisites

Before starting the onboarding process, ensure you meet all these requirements:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="WhatsApp Business Account" icon="whatsapp">
    You must have an existing WhatsApp Business account/number. **Personal WhatsApp numbers are not supported.**
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="App Version" icon="mobile">
    WhatsApp Business App **Version 2.24.17 or newer** is required. Older versions will fail during QR-linking.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Phone Number Health" icon="heart-pulse">
    Your number must have:
    - Regular WhatsApp activity
    - No Meta restrictions
    - No policy violations
    - Active genuine usage for **3-7 days minimum**
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Facebook Business Page" icon="facebook">
    A Facebook Business Page with **Admin Access** is required.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Regional Restrictions" icon="globe">
    Numbers from **Nigeria or South Africa** are not currently supported due to Meta regulations.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Number Eligibility" icon="check-circle">
    Your number must not be:
    - Linked to another WhatsApp API provider
    - Blocked by Meta
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

### Integration Failed

<Warning>
Common reasons for integration failure:
- Old WhatsApp version
- Number inactivity
- Meta restrictions
- No Facebook Page admin access
</Warning>

**Solution:** Use another number that has active WhatsApp Business usage and no past violations.

### Chat History Did Not Import

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open WhatsApp Business App">
    Navigate to **Settings > Business Tools > WhatsApp Business Platform**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Disconnect">
    Tap **Disconnect** to remove the current connection
  </Step>
  <Step title="Restart Onboarding">
    Restart the Coex onboarding process from the beginning
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
If you manually skipped history import initially, you must fully offboard and onboard again to import chat history.
</Info>

### Number Has Meta Restrictions

**Symptoms:**
- Instant failure
- QR code doesn't complete scanning
- No "Connect to Business Platform" message appears

**Solution:** Use a number with zero policy issues and frequent legitimate WhatsApp Business activity.

## FAQs

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I use a personal WhatsApp number?" icon="question">
