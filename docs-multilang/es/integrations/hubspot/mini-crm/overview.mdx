## 

The **HubSpot Vista Mini-CRM** inside WhatsApp is a game-changing feature that allows you to manage your HubSpot CRM directly from your WhatsApp interface. It simplifies your workflow by providing key CRM functionalities without switching tabs or apps.

Here’s what you can do with the Vista Mini-CRM:

**Create & Edit Contactos**: Add or update HubSpot contacts instantly from your WhatsApp conversations.
