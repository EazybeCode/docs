To create a new contact on Salesforce that is not yet in the system.

- ### Open the WhatsApp Chat

Open the WhatsApp chat of the respective contact you wish to add.
- ### Access the Salesforce Mini-CRM View

Click on the Salesforce icon in the right sidebar of the extension.

<Frame>
<img src="/images/1116545133.png" alt="" />
</Frame>
- ### Access the Contacts Section

Click on the **Contact** header at the top of the **Salesforce Mini-CRM View**.
- ### Link an Existing Contact

If there is an **existing Salesforce contact**, click on **Link to existing Salesforce contact** and select the contact from the dropdown menu.
- ### Create a New Contact

If the contact is new and hasnâ€™t been created in Salesforce yet, click on the **Create Contact** button.

<img src="/images/1116545783.png" alt="" />
</Frame>
- ### Fill in Contact Details

Fill in the details in the provided fields. You can also **customize** the fields to your preference by clicking on the **Edit** button and selecting the desired user properties.

<Frame>
<img src="/images/1116546388.png" alt="" />
</Frame>
- ### Create the Contact

Click on the **Create Contact** button. Congratulations! You have successfully added your first Salesforce contact.
- ### View the Contact in Salesforce

You can directly view the contact in Salesforce by clicking on the **View Contact on Salesforce** button.
