In this guide, let us learn how to integrate Bitrix with the EazyBe extension.


- **Ensure you have an active Bitrix account**: You need to ensure that your Bitrix account is setup and functioning.


- **Install EazyBe WhatsApp Extension**: Ensure that your EazyBe WhatsApp Extension is properly connected to WhatsApp web.


- **Permissions**: Ensure you have the necessary permissions to connect Bitrix with WhatsApp. The necessary conditions will be prompted during the integration process.


To integrate Bitrix with EazyBe, ensure that the extension is linked to your Bitrix account. For a step-by-step guide on how to do this, visit [how to install and connect EazyBe with your Bitrix account](https://help.eazybe.com/en/articles/9955209-how-to-install-and-connect-eazybe-with-bitrix-account).
