## Overview

Move back and forth between WhatsApp and HubSpot seamlessly. Eazybe provides convenient navigation buttons that allow you to switch contexts instantly, saving time and improving your workflow efficiency.

---

## Go to HubSpot Button

The **Go to HubSpot** button provides quick access to your HubSpot account directly from WhatsApp Web.

<img src="/images/1185329761.png" alt="Go to HubSpot Button" />

### Where to Find It

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Contact Name Area" icon="user">
    Located beside the contact name at the top of the chat, directing you to the respective contact's HubSpot page.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Profile Section" icon="address-card">
    Available within the Profile section of the mini-CRM view for quick navigation.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<img src="/images/1167041200.png" alt="HubSpot Navigation from WhatsApp" />

<Tip>
Redirect buttons in the Deals and Call Logs sections work similarly, taking you directly to the corresponding HubSpot pages.
</Tip>

---

## Go Back to WhatsApp Button

Eazybe offers a convenient button to return instantly to your WhatsApp conversations from HubSpot.

<img src="/images/1182664571.png" alt="Go Back to WhatsApp Button" />

### Available Locations

The **Go Back to WhatsApp** button appears in multiple locations throughout HubSpot:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Contacts Page">
    Find the button in the **Phone Number** column on the Contacts page to jump directly to the WhatsApp conversation with that contact.

    <img src="/images/1182667834.png" alt="WhatsApp Button on Contacts Page" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Deals Page">
    Located in the **Contact details** section of any deal, allowing quick access to related WhatsApp conversations.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Companies Page">
    Available in the **Phone Number** column on the Companies page for instant WhatsApp access.

    <img src="/images/1182444071.png" alt="WhatsApp Button on Companies Page" />
  </Step>
</Steps>
