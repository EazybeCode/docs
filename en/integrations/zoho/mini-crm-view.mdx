## Overview

The Zoho Mini-CRM View brings your Zoho CRM functionalities directly into your WhatsApp interface. This powerful integration allows you to manage leads, contacts, and deals without ever leaving your messaging conversations.

![Mini-CRM View Interface](/images/1332169251.png)

## What You Can Do

With the Mini-CRM View, you can perform the following actions directly within WhatsApp:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Manage Leads">
    Create and view Zoho leads from WhatsApp chats. Edit lead profiles and add notes to keep your information current.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Handle Contacts">
    Create, view, and edit Zoho contacts. Access full contact profiles and update information in real-time.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Assign Tasks">
    Assign tasks to contacts directly from the WhatsApp interface, ensuring follow-ups are never missed.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Manage Deals">
    Create and manage contact deals. Track deal progress without switching between applications.
  </Step>
</Steps>
