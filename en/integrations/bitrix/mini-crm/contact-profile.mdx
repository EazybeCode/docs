Access and manage your Bitrix contact profiles directly from WhatsApp conversations using the Eazybe extension's Mini CRM view.

## Accessing the Contact Profile

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the WhatsApp Chat">
    Open the WhatsApp chat of your **Bitrix Contact** whose profile you want to access.

    ![Open WhatsApp chat](/images/1224444094.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click on the Bitrix Icon">
    Click on the **Bitrix** icon in the right sidebar of the extension.

    ![Bitrix icon in sidebar](/images/1224444391.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access the Profile Section">
    The first header that appears under the **Contact** section will be the **Profile** section.

    ![Contact profile section](/images/1224457681.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="View and Edit User Properties">
    You can view and edit the user properties of the contact. After making changes, click on the **Save** button at the bottom to update the contact details.

    ![Edit contact properties](/images/1224463190.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Contact in Bitrix">
    You can also view the contact directly in Bitrix by clicking on the **View on Bitrix** button.

    ![View on Bitrix button](/images/1224464736.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>
