In this guide, let us learn how to integrate FreshDesk with the EazyBe extension.


- Ensure that you have the latest version of our WhatsApp extension installed and running on your device.


- When you login into your WhatsApp web account you will see the Integrations button on the right-side bar.


- Click on the Button and you will see a popup.


- Click on the Connect button under the FreshDesk section.


- Choose your account.


- Enter the required details for your account.


- Congratulations, you have successfully integrated FreshDesk with EazyBe.
