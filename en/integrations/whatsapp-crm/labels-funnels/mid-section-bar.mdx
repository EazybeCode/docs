The mid-bar section appears on the right side of the contact details and provides quick access to label and funnel management.

## Overview

The mid-bar section allows you to:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Add/Remove Contacts" icon="user-plus">
    Quickly add or remove a contact directly to or from your labels and funnels.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Switch Stages" icon="arrows-left-right">
    Easily switch between different stages or sub-labels for your contacts.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Using the Mid Section Bar

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the Mid Section Bar">
    Open any WhatsApp chat to see the mid-bar section on the right side of the contact details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Contact to Label/Funnel">
    Click on the desired label or funnel to add the current contact to it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Remove Contact from Label/Funnel">
    Click on an active label or funnel to remove the contact from it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Switch Stages or Sub-Labels">
    For funnels, click on different stages to move the contact through your pipeline. For labels, switch between sub-labels as needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  The mid-bar section provides the fastest way to manage contact organization without leaving the chat view.
</Tip>
