## How to Manage Your Subscription

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Your Account">
    Click on the **My Account** button as shown below.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Subscription Management">
    Click on the **Manage Subscriptions** button. This will redirect you to the Stripe Billing page where you can:

    - Download invoices
    - View and update billing details
    - Update seats and manage your subscription

  </Step>
</Steps>

## How to Delete Your Eazybe Data

<Warning>
Data deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Please ensure you have backed up any important information before submitting a deletion request.
</Warning>

To delete your Eazybe data, submit your request to Eazybe Support via email, including your account email address. The procedure will be completed within **14 business days**.

<Card title="Contact Support" icon="envelope">
  Email: [hey@eazybe.com](mailto:hey@eazybe.com)
</Card>
