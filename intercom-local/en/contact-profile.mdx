Profile Section

The profile section of the Zoho contact will show the basic contact details. To access and edit the profile section, you should ensure that the Contact is created.

- Open the WhatsApp chat of your Zoho Contact.
- Click on the Zoho icon on the right-side bar.
- Click on the contact header. The first header that opens up under the Contact section will be the profile section.

  

- You can view and edit user properties for your contact and can save the changes by clicking on the Save button below.
