## Topic Detection

Conversations are automatically tagged with relevant topics:

- **Sales**: pricing, demo, purchase, buy
- **Support**: help, issue, problem, not working
- **Billing**: invoice, payment, charge, refund
- **General**: info, question, details

## Conversation Summaries

For long conversation threads, AI generates:
- **Key points** discussed
- **Action items** identified
- **Customer sentiment** overview
- **Next steps** suggested
