Edit and update your Zoho Contact profiles directly from WhatsApp using the EazyBe extension. Customize contact properties and keep your CRM records accurate without leaving your conversation.

## How to Edit a Contact Profile

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the WhatsApp Chat">
    Open the WhatsApp conversation with the Zoho Contact whose profile you want to edit.

    ![Open WhatsApp Chat](/images/1223651695.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click on the Edit Button">
    In the Contact Card within the profile section, locate and click the **Edit** button to modify the contact details.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Edit Contact Details">
    A dialog box will appear where you can:
    - Modify or select user properties as needed
    - Mark each property as **required** or **optional**

    ![Edit Contact Details](/images/1223651935.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save the Changes">
    Click **Apply** to refresh the Contact card with your chosen properties. Make any necessary edits to the property fields, then click **Save** to confirm your modifications.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Property Customization

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Required Properties" icon="asterisk">
    Mark essential fields that must be filled for the contact record
  </Card>
  <Card title="Optional Properties" icon="circle-minus">
    Set fields that can be left empty if information is not available
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
  Customizing your contact properties allows you to capture the most relevant information for your business needs while keeping your CRM organized.
</Tip>
