#### Move back and forth between WhatsApp and Bitrix seamlessly


#### Go to Bitrix Button
This Go to Bitrix button provides a quick access to your Bitrix account.


- This button can be found beside the contact or lead name at the top or within the **Profile Section**. Clicking on it will redirect you to the respective contact's page on Bitrix.


#### GoBacktoWhatsApp Button
EazyBe offers a **Go Back to WhatsApp** button which is available in Bitrix. Clicking on this button will instantly return you to the WhatsApp conversation of the corresponding Bitrix Contact.
