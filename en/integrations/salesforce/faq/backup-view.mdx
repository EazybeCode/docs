You can access your WhatsApp chat backups in Salesforce through several areas.

## Salesforce Contact's Page

Synced WhatsApp conversations appear in two locations within respective Salesforce Contacts' Pages:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Notes and Attachments">
    Find your synced conversations in the Notes and Attachments section.
  </Card>
  <Card title="WhatsApp Chats by Eazybe">
    View conversations in the custom activity object.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

![Contact Page - Notes and Attachments](/images/1196813393.png)

![Contact Page - WhatsApp Chats Activity](/images/1196813635.png)

## Salesforce Lead's Page

Synced WhatsApp conversations display in the **"WhatsApp Chats by Eazybe"** custom activity object within respective Salesforce Leads' Pages.

![Lead Page - WhatsApp Chats Activity](/images/1196813835.png)

<Tip>
  Both Contacts and Leads pages provide easy access to your synced WhatsApp conversations, helping you maintain a complete communication history within Salesforce.
</Tip>
