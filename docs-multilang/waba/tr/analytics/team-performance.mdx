## Overview

Team Performance analytics help managers understand how each team member is performing on WhatsApp, identify coaching opportunities, and ensure consistent customer service.

  <img src="/images/Screenshot+2024-09-17+110725.png" alt="Team Performance Dashboard" />
</Frame>

## Individual Metrics

For each team member, track:

| Metric | Description | Benchmark |
|--------|-------------|-----------|
| **Messages Sent** | Daily/weekly outbound volume | Varies by role |
| **Response Time** | Average time to reply | < 5 min (business hours) |
| **Conversation Count** | Active chats managed | Based on capacity |
| **Follow-up Rate** | Scheduled vs completed | > 90% |
| **Last Active** | Most recent activity | Within 1 hour |

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Regular 1:1 Reviews">
    Review individual analytics weekly with each team member to provide feedback and support.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Celebrate Wins">
    Recognize top performers and share best practices with the team.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Focus on Trends">
    Look at week-over-week trends rather than single data points.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Context Matters">
    Consider workload, customer type, and role when comparing metrics.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
