Before you can use EazyBe Cloud WhatsApp, ensure you meet the following requirements:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="EazyBe Extension" icon="puzzle-piece">
    The EazyBe extension must be installed and connected to your WhatsApp Web
  </Card>
  <Card title="Teams Plan" icon="users">
    You need a Teams Plan subscription to access Cloud features
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Requirements Checklist

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install EazyBe Extension">
    Ensure that the EazyBe extension is installed in your browser and connected to your WhatsApp Web account.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Subscribe to Teams Plan">
    Maintain a **Teams Plan** subscription to enjoy the full benefits of EazyBe Cloud WhatsApp.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
Once both requirements are met, you can start using Cloud WhatsApp to sync conversations and schedule messages even when your laptop is closed.
</Info>
