This guide will walk you through the required steps to connect the EazyBe WhatsApp Extension with Bitrix and granting the necessary permissions for integration.

### Open the EazyBe WhatsApp Extension

As a first step, click on the **Integrations **button on your EazyBe WhatsApp web extension.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1112649892.png" alt="" />
</Frame>
### Connect to Bitrix

Click on the **Connect button** below Bitrix integration. This button will redirect you to your Bitrix account.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1195253698.png" alt="" />
</Frame>
- ### Select your Bitrix Account

You will be redirected to a new tab. Click on the link and then, Choose the account that you wish to connect with the EazyBe extension.
### Install Eazybe on Bitrix

Install the EazyBe Application in your Bitrix CRM from the **Bitrix App Marketplace**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1204587764.png" alt="" />
</Frame>
### Grant Necessary Permissions

When prompted, click on the Accept button to grant the necessary permissions required by EazyBe for a seamless integration of your Bitrix account with WhatsApp. These permissions will allow you to view and sync your WhatsApp chats with Bitrix.
Once, all these steps are done, you'll be redirected to WhatsApp.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1204585520.png" alt="" />
</Frame>
- ### Successful Integration

Congratulations! You have successfully connected your EazyBe WhatsApp Extension with Bitrix.
