## Overview

The Pipedrive Mini CRM allows you to manage your sales activities directly from WhatsApp without switching applications. Create contacts, add notes, manage tasks, and create deals based on your WhatsApp conversations.

<Note>
Make sure you have [connected your Pipedrive account](/en/integrations/pipedrive/account-connection) before using these features.
</Note>

---

## Opening the Mini CRM

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click the Pipedrive Logo">
    In the Eazybe panel, click on the **Pipedrive logo** to open the Mini CRM sidebar.

    <Info>
    The Mini CRM appears as a sidebar panel within WhatsApp Web, giving you quick access to Pipedrive features.
    </Info>
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Creating Contacts

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open a WhatsApp Chat">
    Select the chat for which you want to create a contact in Pipedrive.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open Mini CRM">
    Click on the Pipedrive logo to open the Mini CRM.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create Contact">
    Under Mini CRM, click on **Create Contact**.

    The contact will be created based on the information from the open WhatsApp chat.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Adding Notes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open a WhatsApp Chat">
    Select the chat for which you want to add a note.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open Mini CRM">
    Click on the Pipedrive logo to open the Mini CRM.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create Note">
    Under Mini CRM, click on **Notes** and create a new note.

    <Tip>
    Add important details from your WhatsApp conversation to keep your Pipedrive records updated.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Creating Tasks

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open a WhatsApp Chat">
    Select the chat for which you want to create a task.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open Mini CRM">
    Click on the Pipedrive logo to open the Mini CRM.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create Task">
    Under Mini CRM, click on **Tasks** and create a new task.

    Set due dates, priorities, and assign tasks to team members.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Creating Deals

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open a WhatsApp Chat">
    Select the chat for which you want to create a deal.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open Mini CRM">
    Click on the Pipedrive logo to open the Mini CRM.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create Deal">
    Under Mini CRM, click on **Deals** and create a new deal.

    <Info>
    The deal will be associated with the contact from your open WhatsApp chat.
    </Info>
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## One-Click Contact Redirect

<Steps>
  <Step title="Use the Redirect Button">
    Click the **One Click Contact Redirect** button to directly open the contact's Pipedrive page.

    <Tip>
    This is useful when you need to view complete contact details or history in Pipedrive.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Chat Backup View

<Steps>
  <Step title="View Chat Backup">
    Under Mini CRM, access the **Chat Backup** view to see all WhatsApp conversations associated with contacts.

    <Info>
    Chat backup allows you to review conversation history directly within the contact view.
    </Info>
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Quick Reference

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Create Contact** | Add WhatsApp contacts to Pipedrive |
| **Notes** | Add notes to contacts from conversations |
| **Tasks** | Create and manage tasks for follow-ups |
| **Deals** | Create deals based on sales conversations |
| **Contact Redirect** | Open contact's full Pipedrive page |
| **Chat Backup** | View WhatsApp chat history in contacts |
