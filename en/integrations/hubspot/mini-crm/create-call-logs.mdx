## Overview

The Eazybe WhatsApp Extension allows you to log calls for your HubSpot contacts without leaving your WhatsApp conversations. This feature helps you maintain a complete communication history and track all customer interactions in your CRM.

<Note>
Ensure the WhatsApp contact is already created in HubSpot before creating call logs for them.
</Note>

---

## Creating HubSpot Call Logs

Follow these steps to create call logs for your HubSpot contacts directly from WhatsApp:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select the Contact">
    Open the WhatsApp chat of the contact for whom you want to log a call. Make sure the contact already exists in HubSpot.

    <img src="/images/1112632101.png" alt="Select WhatsApp Contact" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access Call Logs Section">
    Click on the **HubSpot icon** in the right sidebar. The fifth button in the top bar will take you to the **Call Logs** section.

    <img src="/images/1112632305.png" alt="Access Call Logs Section" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create New Call Log">
    Click on the **New Call Log** button to start creating a new call entry.

    <img src="/images/1112632477.png" alt="New Call Log Button" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete Call Details">
    Fill in the required fields for your call log:

    <img src="/images/1112632645.png" alt="Call Log Details Form" />

    | Field | Description |
    |-------|-------------|
    | **Call Outcome** | Select the result from the dropdown (e.g., Connected, No Answer, Left Voicemail) |
    | **Direction** | Choose whether the call was Inbound or Outbound |
    | **Date & Time** | Specify when the call occurred |
    | **Call Description** | Enter relevant details about the conversation |
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save Call Log">
    Click on the **Save Call Log** button to save the entry to HubSpot.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Check>
The call log is now saved and will appear in the contact's activity timeline in HubSpot.
</Check>

## Call Log Fields Explained

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Call Outcome" icon="phone-volume">
    Track the result of your call:
    - Connected
    - No Answer
    - Busy
    - Left Voicemail
    - Wrong Number
  </Card>
  <Card title="Call Direction" icon="arrow-right-arrow-left">
    Specify if you made or received the call:
    - **Inbound** — Customer called you
    - **Outbound** — You called the customer
  </Card>
  <Card title="Date & Time" icon="calendar-clock">
    Record the exact timestamp of the call for accurate activity tracking.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Description" icon="file-lines">
    Add notes about the conversation, next steps, or important details discussed.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Log Calls Promptly">
    Create call logs immediately after each call while details are fresh in your mind.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Be Descriptive">
    Include key points discussed, action items, and any follow-up needed in the description field.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Use Consistent Outcomes">
    Apply consistent call outcomes across your team for accurate reporting and analytics.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Track All Communications">
    Log both inbound and outbound calls to maintain a complete communication history.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Why Log Calls?

<Info>
Logging calls in HubSpot provides valuable benefits for your sales and support teams:
- **Complete History** — View all interactions with a contact in one place
- **Team Visibility** — Share call information across your organization
- **Better Follow-ups** — Know exactly where conversations left off
- **Accurate Reporting** — Track call volumes and outcomes for analysis
</Info>
