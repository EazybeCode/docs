## Overview

Broadcast Reports provide detailed analytics on your bulk messaging campaigns, helping you measure effectiveness and optimize future campaigns.

## Campaign Metrics

### Delivery Metrics

| Metric | Description | Good | Excellent |
|--------|-------------|------|-----------|
| **Sent** | Messages submitted | - | - |
| **Delivered** | Reached recipient | > 90% | > 98% |
| **Failed** | Couldn't deliver | < 10% | < 2% |
| **Read** | Opened by recipient | > 50% | > 70% |

### Engagement Metrics

| Metric | Description | Good | Excellent |
|--------|-------------|------|-----------|
| **Response Rate** | Recipients who replied | > 5% | > 15% |
| **Click Rate** | Clicked links (if any) | > 3% | > 10% |
| **Opt-out Rate** | Requested to stop | < 3% | < 1% |

## Failure Analysis

Common delivery failures and solutions:

| Failure Reason | Cause | Solution |
|----------------|-------|----------|
| **Invalid Number** | Wrong format | Validate phone formats |
| **Not on WhatsApp** | No WA account | Remove from list |
| **Blocked** | User blocked you | Respect opt-outs |
| **Rate Limited** | Too fast | Slow down sending |
