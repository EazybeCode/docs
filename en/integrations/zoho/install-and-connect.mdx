## How to Install and Connect Eazybe with Your Zoho Account

<Info>
This guide walks you through connecting the Eazybe WhatsApp Extension with Zoho CRM and obtaining the necessary permissions.
</Info>

---

## Step-by-Step Integration Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Eazybe WhatsApp Extension">
    Click the **"Integrations"** button on your Eazybe WhatsApp Web extension to access the available CRM integrations.

    ![Eazybe Extension Integrations Button](/images/1112649892.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect to Zoho">
    Click the **"Connect"** button below the Zoho integration option. You will be redirected to your Zoho account for authentication.

    ![Connect to Zoho button](/images/1112650071.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Your Zoho Account">
    Choose the Zoho account you wish to connect with the Eazybe extension. If you have multiple accounts, select the appropriate one.

    ![Select Zoho Account](/images/1190703200.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Grant Necessary Permissions">
    When prompted, click **"Accept"** to grant the required permissions. These permissions allow Eazybe to view and sync your WhatsApp chats with Zoho CRM.

    ![Grant Permissions](/images/1190704611.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Integration Complete">
    <Check>
    **Congratulations!** Your Eazybe extension is now successfully connected to your Zoho account. You can start syncing your WhatsApp conversations with Zoho CRM.
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>
