Do you have a pending task you want to save so you can track it anytime? Don't worry! The Task section of Eazybe's Bitrix integration comes to your rescue.

## Creating a Lead Task

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the WhatsApp Chat">
    Open the WhatsApp chat of your Bitrix lead.

    ![Open WhatsApp chat](/images/1224494345.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click on the Bitrix Icon">
    Click on the **Bitrix** icon in the right sidebar of the Eazybe extension.

    ![Bitrix icon in sidebar](/images/1224494696.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access the Task Section">
    The last header under the Lead section will be the **Tasks** section. Click on it to access the section.

    ![Task section](/images/1224497216.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a Task">
    Click on the **Add Task** button.

    ![Add task button](/images/1224498450.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the Details">
    Fill in the required information and click on the **Save** button.

    ![Task details form](/images/1224501321.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Your Created Task">
    Your task will be created and displayed in the UI.

    ![View created task](/images/1224503000.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage Your Task">
    You have the option to either view or hide notes. You can also **edit** or **delete** the created task by clicking on the three dots menu.

    ![Manage task options](/images/1224503222.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>
