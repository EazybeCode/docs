Integrating ZOHO with your EazyBe extension is simple and quick! Below mentioned are pre-requisites needed to integrate them:

<iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/F-Y2FW72LH0?rel=0" frameborder="0" webkitallowfullscreen mozallowfullscreen allowfullscreen></iframe>- **Ensure you have and active ZOHO account:** You need to ensure that your ZOHO account is setup and functioning.

- **Install EazyBe WhatsApp Extension:** Ensure that your EazyBe WhatsApp extension is properly connected to WhatsApp web.

- **Permissions:** Ensure you grant the necessary permissions to connect ZOHO with WhatsApp.

To integrate ZOHO with EazyBe, ensure that the extension is linked to your ZOHO account. For a step-by-step guide on how to do this, visit [how to install and connect EazyBe with your ZOHO account](https://help.eazybe.com/en/articles/9809038-how-to-install-and-connect-eazybe-with-your-zoho-account).
