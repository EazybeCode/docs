This guide provides step-by-step instructions for creating automated WhatsApp marketing campaigns through HubSpot workflows, enabling personalized messaging and improved customer engagement.

## Prerequisites

Before creating a HubSpot workflow for WhatsApp campaigns, ensure you have:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="HubSpot Account" icon="hubspot">
    An active HubSpot account with workflow automation access
  </Card>
  <Card title="Eazybe Integration" icon="plug">
    WhatsApp Integration by Eazybe installed in HubSpot
  </Card>
  <Card title="Approved Templates" icon="file-check">
    At least one approved WhatsApp message template
  </Card>
  <Card title="Contact Properties" icon="address-book">
    Contact properties configured for targeting
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Creating Your HubSpot Workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access HubSpot">
    Log into your HubSpot account and navigate to the main dashboard.

    <!-- TODO(image-missing): original_url="step1-hubspot-login" -->
  </Step>

  <Step title="Navigate to Automation">
    Go to the **Automation** section in your HubSpot navigation menu.

    <!-- TODO(image-missing): original_url="step2-automation-section" -->
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Workflows">
    Click on **Workflows** to access the workflow management area.

    <!-- TODO(image-missing): original_url="step3-workflows" -->
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create New Workflow">
    Click the **Create workflow** button to start building a new automation.

    <!-- TODO(image-missing): original_url="step4-create-workflow" -->
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose From Scratch">
    Select **From scratch** to build a custom workflow tailored to your needs.

    <!-- TODO(image-missing): original_url="step5-from-scratch" -->
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Trigger Type">
    Choose **Met filter criteria** as your trigger type to target specific contacts.

    <!-- TODO(image-missing): original_url="step6-trigger-type" -->
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Object Type">
    Pick **Contact** as the object type for your workflow enrollment.

    <!-- TODO(image-missing): original_url="step7-object-type" -->
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Contact Properties">
    Select **Contact properties** to define your targeting criteria.

    <!-- TODO(image-missing): original_url="step8-contact-properties" -->
  </Step>

  <Step title="Search Property">
    Search for your desired contact property in the search field.

    <!-- TODO(image-missing): original_url="step9-search-property" -->
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Property Name">
    Choose the specific property name you want to filter by (e.g., Company name).

    <!-- TODO(image-missing): original_url="step10-property-name" -->
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure Conditions">
    Set your filter conditions (e.g., **is equal to any of**) and add matching values.

    <!-- TODO(image-missing): original_url="step11-configure-conditions" -->

    <Tip>
    You can add multiple values to target contacts from different companies or segments.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Continue Setup">
    Click **Next** to proceed to the enrollment settings.

    <!-- TODO(image-missing): original_url="step12-next" -->
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enable Re-enrollment">
    If you want to re-enroll existing contacts who meet the criteria again, enable the **Re-enroll** option.

    <!-- TODO(image-missing): original_url="step13-re-enroll" -->

    <Info>
    Re-enrollment allows contacts to go through the workflow multiple times when they meet the trigger criteria again.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save and Continue">
    Click **Save and continue** to finalize your trigger settings.

    <!-- TODO(image-missing): original_url="step14-save-continue" -->
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add WhatsApp Action">
    Scroll down and select **WhatsApp Integration by Eazybe** from the available actions.

    <!-- TODO(image-missing): original_url="step15-whatsapp-integration" -->
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Template Action">
    Click **Send Ready-to-Use WhatsApp Template** to configure the message.

    <!-- TODO(image-missing): original_url="step16-send-template" -->
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Contact Field">
    Choose the contact field that contains the phone number for template delivery.

    <!-- TODO(image-missing): original_url="step17-contact-field" -->
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Template">
    Select your approved WhatsApp template from the dropdown list.

    <!-- TODO(image-missing): original_url="step18-choose-template" -->

    <Warning>
    Only templates with **Approved** status will appear in the list. Ensure your template is approved before creating the workflow.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save Action">
    Click **Save** to confirm your WhatsApp action configuration.

    <!-- TODO(image-missing): original_url="step19-save-action" -->
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review Workflow">
    Click **Review and turn on** to review your complete workflow setup.

    <!-- TODO(image-missing): original_url="step20-review" -->
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enrollment Settings">
    Choose your preferred enrollment settings for the workflow.

    <!-- TODO(image-missing): original_url="step21-enrollment-settings" -->
  </Step>

  <Step title="Final Checks">
    Either click **Skip to turn on** or complete any additional checks as needed.

    <!-- TODO(image-missing): original_url="step22-final-checks" -->
  </Step>

  <Step title="Activate Workflow">
    Click **Turn on workflow** to activate your WhatsApp campaign automation.

    <!-- TODO(image-missing): original_url="step23-turn-on" -->

    <Info>
    Your workflow is now active! Contacts matching your criteria will automatically receive WhatsApp messages.
    </Info>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Test Before Launch" icon="flask">
    Always test your workflow with a small group of contacts before launching to a larger audience.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Monitor Performance" icon="chart-line">
    Regularly check your workflow analytics to optimize message delivery and engagement rates.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Respect Opt-outs" icon="user-shield">
    Ensure your contact list respects customer preferences and opt-out requests.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Use Variables" icon="code">
    Personalize your templates with HubSpot contact properties for better engagement.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
