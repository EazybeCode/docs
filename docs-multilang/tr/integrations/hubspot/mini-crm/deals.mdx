## Create HubSpot Anlaşmalar

- ### Select the Contact

Open the WhatsApp chat of the contact for whom you want to create a **deal**. Make sure the contact is created on HubSpot.
### Access the Anlaşmalar section

Click on the **HubSpot icon **in the right sidebar. The fourth button in the top bar will take you to the **Anlaşmalar section**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1112636242.png" alt="" />
</Frame>
### Create a New Deal

Click on the **New Deal** button to start creating a deal.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1112635692.png" alt="" />
</Frame>
### Customize Deal Properties

You will see some default properties, but you can also click on the **Edit** icon to select and customize additional properties. Reorder the selected properties as per your convenience, and then click **Apply**. (Note: This step is only applicable if you choose to edit the default properties)

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1112635878.png" alt="" />
</Frame>
### Fill in the Deal details

Complete the necessary fields with relevant information, then click **Save Deal**. If you need to create more deals, you can do so by clicking on the **Add** button.

  <img src="/images/1112636418.png" alt="" />
</Frame>

## 
Manage HubSpot Anlaşmalar

**View and Manage Anlaşmalar**

- Open the WhatsApp chat of the HubSpot contact.
- Click on the HubSpot icon and go to the Anlaşmalar section.
To view or manage a deal, click the redirect button next to the deal name to access it in HubSpot.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1330527736.png" alt="" />
</Frame>
- **Edit Anlaşmalar**

- Click the **Edit icon** next to the deal.
- Update properties like pipeline or deal stage as needed.
- To add more properties, click **Edit** at the top, select additional options, and click **Save** to update.
