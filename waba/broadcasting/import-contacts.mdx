## CSV Format Requirements

Your CSV file must include:

| Column | Required | Format | Example |
|--------|----------|--------|---------|
| **Phone** | ✅ Yes | International format, no + | 919876543210 |
| **Name** | Recommended | Text | John Smith |
| **Custom fields** | Optional | Text | ORD12345 |

### Example CSV

```csv
Phone,Name,OrderID,City
919876543210,John Smith,ORD001,Mumbai
919876543211,Jane Doe,ORD002,Delhi
447700900123,Bob Wilson,ORD003,London
```

## Phone Number Formats

<Warning>
Invalid formats cause delivery failures!
</Warning>

| Format | Valid? | Example |
|--------|--------|---------|
| International (no +) | ✅ | 919876543210 |
| International (with +) | ❌ | +919876543210 |
| Local format | ❌ | 9876543210 |
| With spaces | ❌ | 91 9876 543210 |
| With dashes | ❌ | 91-9876-543210 |

## Upload Methods

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="CSV Upload">
    1. Prepare your CSV file
    2. Go to **Broadcasts** → **Create Broadcast**
    3. Click **Upload CSV**
    4. Select your file
    5. Map columns to variables
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="From Labels">
    If contacts are already in your Eazybe inbox with labels:
    
    1. Go to **Broadcasts** → **Create Broadcast**
    2. Select **Choose from Labels**
    3. Pick the label(s) containing target contacts
    4. Review and confirm selection
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="From CRM">
    If integrated with HubSpot, Zoho, etc.:
    
    1. Create a contact list/segment in your CRM
    2. Export as CSV with phone numbers
    3. Upload to Eazybe Broadcasts
    
    <Tip>
    Future: Direct CRM list selection coming soon!
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Variable Mapping

When your template has variables like `{{1}}`, `{{2}}`, map them to CSV columns:

| Template Variable | CSV Column |
|-------------------|------------|
| {{1}} | Name |
| {{2}} | OrderID |
| {{3}} | City |

## Cleaning Your List

Before uploading, remove:
- ❌ Duplicate numbers
- ❌ Invalid formats
- ❌ Unsubscribed contacts
- ❌ Numbers not on WhatsApp

<Tip>
Clean lists = better delivery rates + lower costs (no failed messages)
</Tip>

## Opt-In Requirements

<Warning>
Only message contacts who have **explicitly opted in** to receive WhatsApp messages from your business.
</Warning>

Valid opt-in methods:
- Website form with WhatsApp consent checkbox
- SMS opt-in confirmation
- In-app permission request
- Physical form signature

Invalid (can get you blocked):
- Purchased contact lists
- Scraped numbers
- Contacts who haven't consented
