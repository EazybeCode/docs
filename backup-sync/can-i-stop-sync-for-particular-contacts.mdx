title: "Can I stop sync for particular contacts?"
description: ""
---

Yes, you can stop syncing for particular contacts:


- **Unlink the contact** if you no longer want it to appear on HubSpot.


- If you want the contact to remain on HubSpot but wish to stop chat backups for certain contacts, follow these steps:


Create a **new label**.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1185303537.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


- Add the contacts for which you want to stop syncing under this label.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1185303731.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


- Go to **Backup and Sync** in the right sidebar.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1185303881.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


- Click on the **Don't Sync** button next to the label that includes the contacts you want to stop syncing.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1185304089.png" alt="" />
</Frame>
