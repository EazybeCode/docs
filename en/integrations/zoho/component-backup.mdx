## Overview

Eazybe provides two primary features for Zoho CRM integration:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Automatic Chat Backup" icon="cloud-arrow-up">
    Automatically save your WhatsApp conversations within Zoho CRM
  </Card>
  <Card title="Direct Messaging" icon="message">
    Send and receive WhatsApp messages directly from your CRM without switching platforms
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Prerequisites

<Warning>
  **Administrator Action Required:** Before proceeding, administrators must install the custom object using the link below.
</Warning>

Install the Eazybe custom object: [Install Private Plugin](https://crm.zoho.in/market/installPrivatePlugin.do?portalName=akshayvats&nameSpace=zohocrm34&versionIndex=4525443000000189001)

## Step 1: Install from Zoho Marketplace

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log into Zoho CRM">
    Sign in to your Zoho CRM account with administrator credentials.

    ![Zoho CRM Login](/images/1308902777.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Navigate to Marketplace">
    Go to the **Marketplace** in the top-right menu of your Zoho CRM dashboard.

    ![Marketplace Navigation](/images/1308906912.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Search for Eazybe">
    Search for "Eazybe" in the marketplace search bar and select the app from the results.

    ![Search Eazybe](/images/1308908223.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Install the App">
    Click **Install** to begin the installation process.

    ![Install App](/images/1308909155.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify Your Email">
    Add your email ID and enter the OTP verification code sent to your email.

    ![Email Verification](/images/1308910331.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Generate QR Code">
    Click **Show QR** to generate a QR code for WhatsApp authentication.

    ![QR Code Generation](/images/1308912644.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect WhatsApp">
    Scan the QR code with your WhatsApp mobile app to initiate the session and connect Zoho CRM with Eazybe.

    ![Connect WhatsApp](/images/1308915111.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 2: Backup WhatsApp Chats

Once installation is complete, you can start backing up your WhatsApp conversations:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Leads or Contacts">
    Go to the **Leads** or **Contacts** module in your Zoho CRM.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open WhatsApp Eazybe">
    Click on **WhatsApp Eazybe** to access the backup interface.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Automatic Backup">
    All your WhatsApp chats will automatically back up in Zoho CRM.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Chat backups occur automatically every 3 minutes, ensuring your conversations are always up to date in Zoho CRM.
</Tip>
