Manage your existing labels and funnels by editing their settings, reordering their position, or controlling their visibility in the top bar.

## Accessing the Edit Function

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Edit Label Dropdown">
    Click on the **Edit Label** dropdown to access label and funnel management options.

    ![Edit Label Dropdown](/images/1113051203.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing Labels and Funnels

### Reorder Labels

You can reorder the labels in the top bar by using the drag handles.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Drag to Reorder">
    Use the **dots** on the sidebar and drag the required labels to your desired position.

    ![Reorder Labels](/images/1113051383.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Toggle Visibility

Control which labels appear in the top navigation bar.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Toggle Label Visibility">
    Click the **eye button** to toggle the visibility of labels on the top bar.

    ![Toggle Visibility](/images/1113051528.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Create New Entry

Add new labels or funnels directly from the management panel.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add New Label/Funnel">
    Click on the **Add Label/Funnel** button to create a new entry.

    ![Add Label/Funnel](/images/1113051715.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Edit or Delete Entries

Modify or remove existing labels and funnels.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Edit or Delete">
    Click on the respective **Edit** or **Delete** buttons to modify or remove the label/funnel you've created.

    ![Edit or Delete](/images/1113051895.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Deleting a label or funnel is permanent. Make sure you want to remove it before confirming the deletion.
</Warning>

<Tip>
  Use the visibility toggle to hide labels you don't need frequently without deleting them. You can always show them again later.
</Tip>
