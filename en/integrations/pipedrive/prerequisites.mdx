## Overview

Integrating Pipedrive with your Eazybe WhatsApp extension is simple and quick. Before you begin, ensure you meet the following prerequisites for a smooth setup process.

<Note>
Meeting all prerequisites ensures a seamless integration experience and prevents common setup issues.
</Note>

---

## Requirements Checklist

<Steps>
  <Step title="Active Pipedrive Account">
    Ensure your Pipedrive account is set up and functioning properly. You'll need access to your Pipedrive portal to complete the integration.

    <Info>
    Both **free** and **paid** Pipedrive plans support the Eazybe integration.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Install Eazybe WhatsApp Extension">
    Make sure your Eazybe WhatsApp extension is properly installed and connected to WhatsApp Web.

    If you haven't installed the extension yet, visit the [Chrome Web Store](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/eazybe) to download it.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Company Domain">
    You'll need your Pipedrive Company Domain for the integration.

    <img src="/public/images/pipedrive-04-company-domain.png" alt="Pipedrive Company Domain" />

    This is the subdomain in your Pipedrive URL. When you're logged into Pipedrive, look at the address bar — it'll read something like:

    `https://yourcompany.pipedrive.com`

    The part before **.pipedrive.com** is your Company Domain.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Pipedrive API Token">
    You'll need your Pipedrive API Token to authorize the connection.

    To get your API Token:

    <img src="/public/images/pipedrive-05-api-token.png" alt="Pipedrive API Token" />

    1. Go to your Pipedrive account
    2. Click on **Personal Preferences**
    3. Click on the **API** tab
    4. Copy your existing API Token or generate a new one
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Quick Reference

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **Pipedrive Account** | Active account (free or paid) |
| **Eazybe Extension** | Installed and connected to WhatsApp Web |
| **Company Domain** | Your Pipedrive subdomain |
| **API Token** | Generated from Personal Preferences → API tab |
