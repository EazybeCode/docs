To access and edit the profile section of a Bitrix contact, follow these steps:

- 
#### Open the WhatsApp Chat
Open the WhatsApp chat of your **Bitrix contact** whose profile you want to access.
​

  

- 
#### Click on the Bitrix Icon
Click on the Bitrix icon in the right sidebar of the extension.
​

  

- 
#### Access the Profile Section
The first header that appears under the **Contact** section will be the **Profile** section.
​

  

​

- 
#### View and Edit User Properties
You can view and edit the user properties of the contact. After making changes, click on the **Save** button at the bottom to update the contact details.
​

  

- 
#### View Contact in Bitrix
You can also view the contact directly in Bitrix by clicking on the **View on Bitrix** button.
​
