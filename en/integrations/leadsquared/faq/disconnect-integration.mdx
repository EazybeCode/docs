---

If you need to disconnect the LeadSquared integration from EazyBe, follow these simple steps.

## Steps to Disconnect

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Integration Settings">
    Click on the **Integration** button in the EazyBe panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Initiate Disconnect">
    Click the **Disconnect** button to begin the disconnection process.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Action">
    You will be prompted to confirm the action. Click **Confirm** to complete the disconnection.
  </Step>
</Steps>

![Disconnect Integration](/images/1224454597.png)

<Warning>
  Disconnecting the integration will stop all syncing between WhatsApp and LeadSquared. Make sure you have backed up any important data before proceeding.
</Warning>

<Info>
  For more detailed information about the disconnection process, see the [Disconnect LeadSquared Integration](/en/integrations/leadsquared/disconnect) guide.
</Info>
