To use Cloud features in EazyBe, you'll need to subscribe to the Teams Plan, and it will unlock the full benefits of the Cloud functionality.

There are two ways to connect Cloud with the EazyBe WhatsApp Extension:

- ### Connecting Cloud through the EazyBe Workspace

Log into the EazyBe workspace.
- Navigate to the organization's page and select the account for which you want to integrate Cloud.
- Click the **Start Connection** button next to your account name. The status will change from **Not Connected** to **Preparing**.

<Frame>
<img src="/images/1203727195.png" alt="" />
</Frame>
- Once done, the status will update to **Ready To Connect** and the button text will display as **Connect WhatsApp**.

<Frame>
<img src="/images/1203731495.png" alt="" />
</Frame>
- Clicking the **Connect WhatsApp** button will open a new window with a **QR code**.
- Scan this **QR code** using your **WhatsApp mobile app**.
- After scanning, the status will change to **Connected,** allowing you to sync WhatsApp chats even when your laptop is closed.

<Frame>
<img src="/images/1203738168.png" alt="" />
</Frame>

Note: Once connected, the **cloud icon** in the extension will turn green.

### Connecting Cloud through the EazyBe WhatsApp Extension

- Ensure you're logged into **WhatsApp Web** with the **EazyBe extension** running.
- You'll see a **cloud scanner icon** on the left side of your screen.
- Click on this icon, and a popup will appear.
- Click the **Show QR** button to generate a QR code.

<Frame>
<img src="/images/1203759574.png" alt="" />
</Frame>
- Scan this **QR code** using your WhatsApp mobile application.
- After scanning, the **cloud icon** will turn green, and your chats will sync **even when your laptop is closed**.

Note: The connection status will also show as **Connected** in the EazyBe Workspace.

This process demonstrates seamless two-way connectivity between the EazyBe workspace and the EazyBe WhatsApp Extension, ensuring chat backups continue even when your laptop is closed.
