title: "Disconnect Integration"
description: "Learn how to disconnect your Salesforce integration from the Eazybe WhatsApp Chrome Extension."
---

Follow these steps to disconnect your Salesforce integration from Eazybe.

## Steps to Disconnect

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Integration Settings">
    Click on the **Integration** button in the right sidebar.

    ![Integration Button](/images/1196803432.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Disconnect Salesforce">
    Click on the **Disconnect** button under the Salesforce section.

    ![Disconnect Button](/images/1223682567.png)

    <Note>
      A prompt will appear asking you to confirm the disconnection. Click confirm to complete the process.
    </Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Disconnecting the integration will stop syncing your WhatsApp conversations with Salesforce. Make sure to backup any important data before disconnecting.
</Warning>
