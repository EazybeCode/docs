## The Problem

WhatsApp conversations happen outside Zoho:
- Managers have no visibility
- When reps leave, history is lost
- No audit trail

## The Solution

Every WhatsApp message automatically appears in Zoho timeline.

## Step-by-Step Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Integration Settings">
    Go to **Settings** → **Integrations** → **Zoho CRM**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enable Chat Backup">
    Toggle **Backup chats to Zoho** to ON
    
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/zoho/enable-chat-backup.png" alt="Enable chat backup" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose Activity Type">
    Select how messages appear:
    - **Notes** - As notes on record
    - **Activity** - As WhatsApp activity
    
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/zoho/activity-type.png" alt="Activity type" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure Sync Options">
    Choose what to backup:
    - ✅ Text messages
    - ✅ Images
    - ✅ Documents
    - ✅ Voice notes
  </Step>
</Steps>
