## Revenue Inbox

The Revenue Inbox (Team Inbox) is your centralized hub for managing all WhatsApp conversations across your sales and support team.

### What is Revenue Inbox?

Revenue Inbox lets you:
- **Monitor all team conversations** in one place
- **Identify unreplied and critical chats** using AI-powered detection
- **Track response times** and team performance
- **Assign conversations** to team members
- **Filter by labels, funnels, and priority**

### Accessing Revenue Inbox

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Eazybe Workspace">
    Go to [workspace.eazybe.com](https://workspace.eazybe.com) or click the Organization button in the extension
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Team Inbox">
    Select **Team Inbox** from the left sidebar
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Conversations">
    See all synced WhatsApp conversations from your team
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Who Can Access?

| Role | Access Level |
|------|--------------|
| Admin | Full access to all team conversations |
| Manager | Access to team members' conversations |
| Agent | Access to own conversations only |

<Info>
Conversations are synced from WhatsApp to Revenue Inbox based on your [Backup & Sync](/revenue-inbox/backup-sync) settings.
</Info>
