- **Ensure you have an active Salesforce account**: You need to ensure that your Salesforce account is setup and functioning.


- **Install EazyBe WhatsApp Extension**: Ensure that your EazyBe WhatsApp Extension is properly connected to WhatsApp web.


- **Permissions**: Ensure you grant the necessary permissions to connect Salesforce with WhatsApp.


To integrate Salesforce with EazyBe, ensure that the extension is linked to your Salesforce account. For a step-by-step guide on how to do this, visit [how to install and connect EazyBe with your Salesforce account](https://help.eazybe.com/en/articles/9929131-how-to-install-and-connect-eazybe-with-salesforce-account).
