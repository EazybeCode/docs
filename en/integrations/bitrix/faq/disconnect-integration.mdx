If you need to disconnect your Bitrix integration from EazyBe, follow these simple steps.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Integration Settings">
    Click on the **integration button** in the right sidebar of the EazyBe extension.

    ![Integration Button](/images/1204535227.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Disconnect Bitrix">
    Click on the **Disconnect button** under the Bitrix section.

    ![Disconnect Button](/images/1224558042.png)

    A confirmation prompt will appear asking you to confirm the disconnection.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Disconnecting the integration will stop automatic syncing of WhatsApp conversations with Bitrix. You can always reconnect later if needed.
</Warning>
