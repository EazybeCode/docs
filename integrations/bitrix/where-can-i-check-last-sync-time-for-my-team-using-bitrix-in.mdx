---
title: "Where can I check Last sync time for my team using Bitrix Integration?"
description: ""
---

Admin can check the last sync time for their team on [EazyBe workspace](https://workspace.eazybe.com/).


- Admin should log in to the EazyBe Workspace using their credentials.


- Click on the **Dashboard** button.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1205454436.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


- In the **Last Chat Synced** column, you will see the last sync time of the Bitrix chats for all your employees.
