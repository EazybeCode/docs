Opportunities represent potential deals or clients showing interest in your product. They help you categorize clients based on the status of conversations, allowing you to prioritize the most important ones.

## Creating and Managing Opportunities

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Lead's WhatsApp Chat">
    Access the WhatsApp conversation for the LeadSquared contact you want to create an opportunity for.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access the Opportunity Section">
    Navigate to the **Mini-CRM view** in the right sidebar. The Opportunity section appears only if the lead already exists in LeadSquared.

    ![Opportunity section in Mini-CRM](/images/1224408720.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create Opportunity">
    Click the **Create Opportunity** button to begin adding a new opportunity for this lead.

    ![Create Opportunity button](/images/1224408957.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Edit Opportunity Details">
    Fill in the required opportunity information including deal value, stage, and other relevant details.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save Opportunity Details">
    Click the **Save** button to finalize and create the opportunity.

    ![Save Opportunity form](/images/1224409190.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add More Opportunities">
    Repeat the process to create additional opportunities for the same lead as needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Opportunities created in EazyBe are automatically synchronized with LeadSquared and linked to the specific lead.
</Info>
