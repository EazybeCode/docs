## Before You Begin

To connect a WhatsApp Business Account (WABA) through Eazybe, ensure you have the following ready:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Meta Business Account" icon="meta">
    Your business must be set up on **Meta's Business Manager**. If you don't have one, you can create it during the Eazybe setup process.
    
    [Create Meta Business Account →](https://business.facebook.com/)
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Phone Number" icon="phone">
    You need a phone number that can receive **OTP verification** via SMS or call. This can be:
    - A new number dedicated to WhatsApp API
    - Your existing WhatsApp Business App number (for Coexistence mode)
    
    <Warning>
    Personal WhatsApp numbers are **not** supported. You must use a WhatsApp Business App number.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Admin Access" icon="user-shield">
    You must be an **administrator** of the Facebook Business Account to complete the connection process.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Connection Options

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="New Number" icon="plus">
    **Fresh Start**
    
    Register a new phone number directly with the WhatsApp API. Best for businesses wanting a dedicated API line.
    
    - No cooldown period
    - Clean slate for templates
    - Separate from existing WhatsApp
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Existing Number (Coexistence)" icon="arrows-rotate">
    **Keep Your Number**
    
    Connect your existing WhatsApp Business App number while continuing to use the mobile app.
    
    - Sync up to 6 months of chat history
    - Use app + API simultaneously
    - Keep your verified badge
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Requirements Checklist

<Steps>

  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Eazybe Account">
    ✅ Active Eazybe subscription
    
    ✅ Workspace admin access
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Important Notes

<Warning>
**For Existing WhatsApp Business App Users:**

If your number was previously connected to another WABA provider, you must:
1. Disconnect from the previous provider
2. Wait **1-2 months** (cooldown period)
3. Keep the number active on WhatsApp Business App
4. Then connect via Eazybe
</Warning>

<Info>
**Chat History Import**

When using Coexistence mode, you can import up to **6 months** of chat history from your WhatsApp Business App. Keep the app open during the import process for best results.
</Info>
