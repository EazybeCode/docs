Have you created an opportunity on LeadSquared and now want to create and track tasks for that opportunity? Here's how you can do it.

## Creating Opportunity Tasks

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Lead's WhatsApp Chat">
    Begin by opening the WhatsApp chat for the LeadSquared lead requiring task management.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access the Opportunity Section">
    Within the LeadSquared Mini-CRM view, select the **Opportunity** section tied to that lead.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access the Tasks Section">
    Choose the opportunity that needs a task, then click the **Tasks** section.

    ![Opportunity Tasks Section](/images/1224436524.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add a New Opportunity Task">
    Select the **Add Task** button for the respective opportunity.

    ![Add Task Button](/images/1224446975.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fill in the Required Details">
    Complete the task details in the provided fields. Additional fields can be edited via the **Edit** button.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save the Task">
    Click **Save** to store the task.

    ![Save Task Form](/images/1224447153.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Mark Task as Complete">
    Use the **Complete** button to mark finished tasks as done.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add More Tasks">
    Repeat the process by clicking **Add Task** for additional tasks as needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Tasks created for opportunities in EazyBe are automatically synchronized with LeadSquared.
</Info>
