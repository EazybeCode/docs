## Data Processing Agreement (DPA)

<Info>
If your organization requires a **Data Processing Agreement (DPA)**, you can request one by contacting our team.
</Info>

### How to Request a DPA

<Steps>
  <Step title="Send a Request">
    Email your DPA request to **hey@eazybe.com** with your organization details.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Receive Your DPA">
    Our team will process your request and provide the necessary documentation.
  </Step>
</Steps>
