Connecting your official WABA to Eazybe is a simple process handled through Meta's secure, Embedded Signup flow. This ensures your account is set up correctly and linked to your Eazybe workspace.


### **Step-by-Step Guide to Connect Your WABA** 

- **Navigate to the Get Started page:** In your Eazybe dashboard, go to **Get Started**> **Connect with Facebook**.
​


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1589132316.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


- **Start the Connection:** Click on "**Connect with Facebook" **to open Meta's secure setup window.

**Note:** This popup is managed by Facebook. Eazybe does not see or store your personal Facebook login details.
​


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1589134577.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


- **Log in and Grant Permissions:** Log into the Facebook profile that has admin access to your Facebook Business Account. Grant Eazybe the necessary permissions to manage your WhatsApp assets.
​


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1589138339.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


​


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1589139871.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


- **Select Your Business Assets:** Follow the on-screen prompts within the popup to:


Choose or create the **Meta Business Account** you want to use.


- Choose or create the **WhatsApp Business Account (WABA)**.


- Choose or create your **WhatsApp Business Profile** (this is the name and information your customers will see).
​


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1589141084.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


​


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1589141902.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


- **Verify Your Phone Number:**


Connect to an existing WhatsApp Business number (Coexistence), or add a new phone number you have prepared for your WABA.


- Complete the verification by entering the code you receive.
​


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1589143435.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


​


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1589144849.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


- **Complete the Setup:** Once verified, review your selections and click **Finish**. The popup window will close.
​**Note:** If you have connected an existing WhatsApp Business account, you will have the option to share (or not share) the chat history of upto six months during the initial setup.

Your WABA will now be connected to Eazybe! You'll be redirected back to your dashboard, where you can see the connection status. Your account is now ready for the next steps: creating message templates and sending broadcasts.
