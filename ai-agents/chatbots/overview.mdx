## Overview

Eazybe Chatbots are AI-powered bots that can automatically respond to common customer queries, qualify leads, and route conversations to the right team members.

<Note>
Chatbots require WABA (WhatsApp Business API) connection for automated responses.
</Note>

## Key Capabilities

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Auto-Response">
    Instantly respond to common questions
  </Card>
  <Card title="Lead Qualification">
    Ask qualifying questions before handoff
  </Card>
  <Card title="24/7 Availability">
    Respond outside business hours
  </Card>
  <Card title="Smart Routing">
    Direct conversations to right team members
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Use Cases

| Use Case | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **FAQ Bot** | Answer common questions |

| **Appointment Booking** | Schedule meetings |
| **Support Triage** | Route to right department |

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Ensure WABA Connected">
    Chatbots require WhatsApp Business API connection
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Chatbot">
    Go to **AI Agents** → **Chatbots** → **Create New**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build Flow">
    Use the visual flow builder to design conversation
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test & Deploy">
    Test with your own phone before going live
  </Step>
</Steps>
