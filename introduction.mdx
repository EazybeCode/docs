## Why Eazybe?

### Built for Sales & Marketing Teams

| Challenge | Eazybe Solution |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Scattered conversations | **Revenue Inbox** - All team WhatsApp in one place |
| No CRM visibility | **CRM Integration** - See deals, contacts, history inline |
| Slow response times | **AI Agents** - Smart prioritization & suggestions |
| Manual follow-ups | **Workflows** - Automated WhatsApp from CRM triggers |
| No accountability | **Analytics** - Track response times & performance |
