## Connect WhatsApp

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open WhatsApp Web">
    Go to web.whatsapp.com
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scan QR Code">

    Open WhatsApp on phone → Settings → Linked Devices → Scan
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify">
    Eazybe panel appears when connected

  </Step>
</Steps>
