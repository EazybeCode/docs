The necessary permissions will be prompted during the integration process. Be sure to click on these prompts and ensure that your super-admin has granted **File** and **Apps Marketplace** permissions. These are essential for smooth syncing and backup of your WhatsApp chats using the EazyBe WhatsApp Chrome Extension.
