## Overview

The Eazybe Admin Workspace provides administrators with a centralized dashboard to monitor team performance, track messaging activity, and manage CRM integrations. This guide walks you through accessing and using the admin workspace effectively.

<Note>
Admin workspace access is available only to users with administrator privileges. Contact your organization admin if you need access.
</Note>

## Accessing the Admin Workspace

Follow these steps to access the Eazybe admin workspace:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Organization Settings">
    Click on the **Organisation** button in the right sidebar of the Eazybe extension. This will launch a new browser tab.

    <img src="/images/1185334534.png" alt="Organisation Button" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Authenticate Your Account">
    Enter your credentials to authenticate and access the admin workspace.

    <img src="/images/1182359490.png" alt="Authentication Screen" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Navigate to Dashboard">
    Once logged in, click on the **Dashboard** button in the left sidebar to view your operational metrics and team data.

    <img src="/images/1185349844.png" alt="Dashboard Navigation" />
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Check>
You now have access to the admin workspace and can view team performance data.
</Check>
