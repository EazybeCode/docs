The Integrations section is the fourth section in the left sidebar of your EazyBe Workspace. It provides a centralized hub for managing all your CRM connections.

## Accessing the Integrations Section

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Sidebar">
    Navigate to the left sidebar in your EazyBe Workspace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click on Integrations">
    Click on the **Integrations** section to access the CRM management area.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing Integrations

From the Integrations section, you can:

- **Configure new CRM connections** - Set up integrations with your preferred CRM platform
- **View active integrations** - See all currently connected CRM systems
- **Manage settings** - Adjust sync preferences and connection settings
- **Disconnect integrations** - Remove CRM connections when needed

<Tip>
  Each CRM integration has its own dedicated configuration options. Click on a specific integration to access its settings and customization options.
</Tip>
