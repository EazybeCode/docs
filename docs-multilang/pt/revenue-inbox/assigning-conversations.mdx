## Why Assign Conversations?

Assigning conversations ensures:
- **Clear ownership** - Someone is responsible for each customer
- **No duplicates** - Two people don't work the same chat
- **Accountability** - Track who handled what
- **Better routing** - Match specialists to relevant queries

## How to Assign

### From Team Inbox

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Team Inbox">
    Navigate to **Workspace** → **Team Inbox**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Conversation">
    Click on the conversation you want to assign
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click Assign">
    In the conversation detail panel, click the **Assign** button
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose Team Member">
    Select from dropdown of available team members
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm">
    The conversation now appears in that team member's queue
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Bulk Assignment

1. Select multiple conversations (checkbox)
2. Click **Bulk Actions** → **Assign**
3. Choose the team member
4. All selected chats are assigned

## Viewing Assignments

### For Managers

In Team Inbox, the **Owner** column shows who's assigned to each conversation.

Filter by owner to see a specific team member's queue:
1. Click **Filter** → **Employee**
2. Select the team member
3. See only their assigned conversations

### For Team Members

In the Chrome extension:
- See your assigned conversations highlighted
- Notification when new chats are assigned to you
