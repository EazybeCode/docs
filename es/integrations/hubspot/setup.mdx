## HubSpot Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Integrations">

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/hubspot/integrations-button.png" alt="Integrations button" />
</Frame>

    Click **Integrations** in Eazybe panel
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select HubSpot">

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/hubspot/connect-button.png" alt="Connect to HubSpot" />
</Frame>

    Click **Connect to HubSpot**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize">

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/hubspot/permissions-screen.png" alt="HubSpot permissions" />
</Frame>

    Login and grant permissions
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure">
    Set sync preferences
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify">

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/hubspot/hubspot-icon-chat.png" alt="HubSpot connected" />
</Frame>

    Open a chat to verify connection
  </Step>
</Steps>
