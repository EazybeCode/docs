## How to Invite Team Members

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Organization">
    Go to the Organization section by clicking the **Organization** button in your EazyBe workspace.

    ![Organization Button](/images/1347924117.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Employees">
    Select **Employees → Add Employees** from the menu.

    ![Add Employees](/images/1347924118.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Employee Information">
    Fill in the required employee details including Name, Email, and WhatsApp Number. Click **Add** to save the employee, then click **Send Invites** to distribute invitations.

    ![Send Invites](/images/1347924116.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is the purpose of inviting team members?">
    Inviting team members enables task assignment, provides access to shared Quick Replies and Labels, and allows monitoring of team analytics.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is my data secure?">
    EazyBe does not store any data on its servers. All data is backed up with the user's WhatsApp number.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Where is the backup stored?">
    Backups are stored in the Admin's Google Drive via Team Inbox.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How can I add more seats?">
    You can expand seats through **Accounts → Billing** or through WhatsApp upgrade options.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What access do invited employees receive?">
    Invitees gain access to shared Quick Replies and Labels configured by the organization.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
