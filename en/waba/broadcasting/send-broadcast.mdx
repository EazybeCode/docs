## How to Send a Broadcast Campaign

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Broadcasts">
    Select **Broadcasts** from the main menu in your Eazybe dashboard.

    ![Navigate to Broadcasts](/images/1589454418.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create New Broadcast">
    Click the **Create Broadcast** button to start a new campaign.

    ![Create Broadcast Button](/images/1589492369.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name Your Campaign">
    Provide an internal name for your campaign to help you identify it later.

    ![Name Your Campaign](/images/1589455306.png)

    <Tip>
      Use descriptive names like "June Newsletter 2025" or "Holiday Sale Announcement" to easily find campaigns later.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Template">
    Choose an **Approved Message Template** from the dropdown list.

    ![Select Template](/images/1589463099.png)

    <Warning>
      Only templates with "Approved" status can be used for broadcasts. Pending or rejected templates will not appear in the list.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload Contacts">
    Click **Upload CSV** and select your file containing phone numbers.

    ![Upload CSV](/images/1589464921.png)

    <Info>
      Phone numbers must be in international format without the "+" sign (e.g., 91xxxxxxxxxx for India, 1xxxxxxxxxx for USA).
    </Info>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Recipients">
    Choose the contacts you want to include in your broadcast from the imported CSV.

    ![Select Recipients](/images/1589475395.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Label (Extension Users)">
    If you're using the Eazybe extension, you can select a label containing tagged contacts.

    ![Select Label](/images/1589476967.png)

    <Info>
      Labels work with individual contacts only, not groups.
    </Info>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch Campaign">
    Click **Send Broadcast** to start sending messages to your selected recipients.

    ![Launch Campaign](/images/1589467988.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tracking Campaign Performance

After launching your campaign, you can track its performance directly from the Broadcast dashboard.

![Campaign Performance](/images/1589487173.png)

The dashboard shows you:

- **Messages Sent** - Total number of messages dispatched
- **Messages Delivered** - Successfully delivered to recipients
- **Messages Read** - Opened and read by recipients
