### Introduction
Eazybe's Google Sheets integration allows you to seamlessly sync your WhatsApp chats, manage contacts, and automate data organization. Whether you're using Google Sheets as a CRM or for auto chat backups, this guide will walk you through the setup and key features
​

### Setting Up Google Sheets Integration

### Step 1: Install the Eazybe Chrome Extension
Ensure you have installed the [Eazybe Chrome Extension](https://eazybe.com) in your browser.

### Step 2: Connect Google Sheets

- Open WhatsApp Web and access the **Eazybe Sidebar**.
​
- Click on **Integrations**.
​
- Find **Google Sheets Integration** and click **Connect**.

  

​
- Log in to your Google account and grant the necessary permissions.
​
- After logging in, you will see the option to **Upload an existing sheet** or **Create a new one**.

- View Google sheets contact information in mini-CRM view

- **Now, You can change labels of contact from sheet itself**

### Features & Benefits of Google Sheets Integration

### ✅ WhatsApp Auto Chat Backup in Google Sheets

Automatically back up your WhatsApp conversations to Google Sheets in real time.
- Never lose important customer interactions.

### ✅ Use WhatsApp Business Labels with google sheets

- Any changes you make in your Google Sheet will **automatically reflect in WhatsApp**.
- Keep your customer data updated without manual effort.

### ✅ Use Google Sheets as Your CRM

- Manage contacts efficiently by storing them in a Google Sheet.
- Categorize leads and customers using **WhatsApp Business Labels**.
- Access all your CRM data directly inside WhatsApp Web.

### ✅ Broadcast Messages Easily

- Upload contact lists in bulk using Google Sheets.
- Send targeted WhatsApp messages to specific customer segments.
- Ensure personalized communication at scale.

### ✅ Download Customer Lists Anytime

- Need a backup? Easily export all contacts saved under a specific label.
- Maintain organized records of your customer interactions.
