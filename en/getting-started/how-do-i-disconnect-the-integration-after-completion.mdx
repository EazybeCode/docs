title: "How do I disconnect the integration after completion?"
description: ""
---

Click on the **integration** button, and then the **Disconnect** button.

  

You will be prompted to confirm. To learn more about disconnecting LeadSquared, head over to [How to Disconnect LeadSquared Integration](https://help.eazybe.com/en/articles/9799653-disconnect-leadsquared-integration).
