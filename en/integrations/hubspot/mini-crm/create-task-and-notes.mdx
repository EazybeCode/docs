<Note>
Ensure the WhatsApp contact is already linked to a HubSpot contact before creating tasks or notes.

## Creating HubSpot Tasks

Follow these steps to create tasks for your HubSpot contacts directly from WhatsApp:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Contact Chat">
    Navigate to the WhatsApp chat of the contact for whom you want to create a task. Ensure the contact is linked to a HubSpot contact.

    <img src="/images/1329216536.png" alt="Open WhatsApp Chat" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access the Tasks Section">
    Click the **HubSpot icon** in the sidebar, then navigate to the **Tasks** section (the second button in the Mini-CRM panel).

    <img src="/images/1329218539.png" alt="Tasks Section in Mini-CRM" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add a New Task">
    Select the **Add Task** option to start creating a new task assignment.

    <img src="/images/1329213975.png" alt="Add Task Button" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Task Priority">
    Designate task priority levels to organize your workflow effectively:
    - **High** — Urgent tasks requiring immediate attention
    - **Medium** — Important but not urgent tasks
    - **Low** — Tasks that can be addressed later

    <img src="/images/1329213973.png" alt="Task Priority Settings" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create Multiple Tasks">
    Use the **plus icon** to generate multiple tasks for batch creation when you need to assign several follow-ups at once.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Mark Tasks Complete">
    Once a task is finished, mark it as completed to move it to the finished section and keep your task list organized.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Check>
All tasks created are automatically synced to your HubSpot account and appear in the contact's activity timeline.
</Check>

## Creating HubSpot Notes

Follow these steps to add notes to your HubSpot contacts from WhatsApp:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Contact Chat">
    Navigate to the WhatsApp chat of the contact for whom you want to create notes.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access the Notes Section">
    Click the **Notes** button (the third button in the top navigation of the Mini-CRM panel).

    <img src="/images/1329220020.png" alt="Notes Section" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Write Your Notes">
    Enter your follow-up notes or important information in the provided text box. Include any relevant details about the conversation or next steps.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Attach WhatsApp Messages (Optional)">
    Optionally attach specific WhatsApp messages to your notes by selecting the beginning and ending messages you want to include.

    <img src="/images/1329220374.png" alt="Attach Messages to Notes" />

    <Info>
    Attaching messages provides context and creates a direct link between your notes and the actual conversation.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save the Note">
    Click **Save** to store the note in HubSpot. The note will appear in the contact's activity timeline.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Task vs Notes Comparison

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Tasks" icon="list-check">
    - Action items with due dates

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Use Clear Task Descriptions">
    Write specific, actionable task descriptions that clearly state what needs to be done and by when.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Set Appropriate Priorities">
    Use priority levels consistently to help your team focus on the most important tasks first.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Add Context to Notes">
    Include relevant details and attach WhatsApp messages to provide complete context for future reference.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Review Tasks Regularly">
    Check your task list regularly to ensure nothing falls through the cracks and update statuses promptly.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
