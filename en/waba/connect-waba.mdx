Connecting your official WABA to Eazybe is a simple process handled through Meta's secure, Embedded Signup flow.

## Step-by-Step Connection Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Get Started Page">
    Access your Eazybe dashboard and go to **Get Started** â†’ **Connect with Facebook**.

    ![Get Started Page](/images/1589132316.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start the Connection">
    Click **"Connect with Facebook"** to open Meta's secure setup window.

    ![Connect with Facebook](/images/1589134577.png)

    <Info>
      This popup is managed by Facebook. Eazybe does not see or store your personal Facebook login details.
    </Info>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log in and Grant Permissions">
    Log into the Facebook profile that has **admin access** to your Facebook Business Account.

    ![Facebook Login](/images/1589138339.png)

    Then grant Eazybe the necessary permissions to manage WhatsApp assets.

    ![Grant Permissions](/images/1589139871.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Your Business Assets">
    Follow the prompts to configure your business setup:

    **Choose or create a Meta Business Account:** <img src="/images/1589141084.png" alt="Meta Business Account" />

    **Choose or create a WhatsApp Business Account (WABA):** <img src="/images/1589141902.png" alt="WhatsApp Business Account" />

    **Choose or create your WhatsApp Business Profile:** <img src="/images/1589143435.png" alt="WhatsApp Business Profile" />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Your Phone Number">
    Connect to an existing WhatsApp Business number or add a new one.

    ![Phone Number](/images/1589144849.png)

    Complete verification by entering the code you receive via SMS or voice call.

    ![Verification Code](/images/1591095646.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the Setup">
    Review your selections and click **Finish**.

    ![Complete Setup](/images/1591096785.png)

    <Tip>
      If you're using an existing WhatsApp Business number, you'll have the option to share (or not share) chat history of up to six months during the initial setup.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Connection Complete

Your WABA is now connected to Eazybe! You'll be redirected back to your dashboard, where you can see the connection status.
