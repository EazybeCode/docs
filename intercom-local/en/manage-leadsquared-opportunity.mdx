The EazyBe WhatsApp Extension allows you to efficiently manage your LeadSquared Opportunities. Here are the various tasks you can perform on opportunities:

#### 1. View Opportunity on LeadSquared
You can directly view any created opportunity in LeadSquared.

- Click on the respective opportunity.
- Select the **View Opportunity on LeadSquared** button to be redirected to the **LeadSquared Opportunities page**.
- You can also view chat backup in the Opportunities section.

  

#### 2. Edit Opportunity
To edit an existing opportunity:

- Select the opportunity you want to modify.
- Click on the **Edit** button. It will open a user properties window.

  

- Adjust the user properties as required and click on **Apply**.

  

- After making the necessary changes, click **Save** to confirm the edits.

#### 3. Delete Opportunity
To delete an opportunity:

- Click on the three dots next to the respective opportunity you wish to delete.
- Select the **Delete** button, and the opportunity will be deleted.

#### 4. Create Opportunity Tasks
You can create specific tasks for your opportunities in LeadSquared.

- Click on the opportunity for which you want to create a task.
- Navigate to the **Tasks** section.
Click on the **Add Task** button and fill in the required details.

  

- Once you click on **Save**, a task will be created against the respective opportunity.
