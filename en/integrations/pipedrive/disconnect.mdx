## Overview

If you no longer need the Pipedrive integration or want to connect a different account, you can easily disconnect the integration from the Eazybe panel.

<Note>
  Disconnecting the integration will stop syncing data between WhatsApp and
  Pipedrive. Existing data in Pipedrive will remain unchanged.
</Note>

---

## Disconnecting Pipedrive

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open WhatsApp Web with Eazybe Extension">
    Open WhatsApp Web and ensure the Eazybe extension is active.
  </Step>

<Step title="Navigate to Integrations">
  Click on the **CRM/Integrations** button in the Eazybe panel.
</Step>

<Step title="Select Pipedrive">
  Navigate to **Pipedrive** from the connected integrations.
</Step>

  <Step title="Click Disconnect">
    Click on the **Disconnect** button to remove the integration.
    <img src="/images/pipedrive-07-mini-crm.png" alt="Pipedrive Mini CRM" />

    <Warning>
    After disconnecting, you will need to reconnect to use Pipedrive features again.
    </Warning>

  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm Disconnection">
    Confirm the disconnection when prompted.

    <Check>
    Your Pipedrive account has been successfully disconnected.
    </Check>

  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## What Happens After Disconnecting

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Data Remains" icon="database">
    All contacts, notes, tasks, and deals created in Pipedrive during the
    integration will remain in your Pipedrive account.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Sync Stops" icon="arrows-rotate">
    New WhatsApp conversations will no longer sync to Pipedrive after
    disconnection.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

---

## Reconnecting

If you want to reconnect Pipedrive after disconnecting:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Follow Connection Steps">
    Follow the [Account
    Connection](/en/integrations/pipedrive/account-connection) guide to
    reconnect your Pipedrive account.
  </Step>
</Steps>
