The Team Inbox is the second section in the left sidebar of your EazyBe Workspace. It provides a centralized view of all WhatsApp conversations from employees in your organization.

## Accessing the Team Inbox

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Sidebar">
    Navigate to the left sidebar in your EazyBe Workspace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click on Team Inbox">
    Click on the **Team Inbox** section to access the shared conversation view.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Filtering Conversations

You have flexible options for viewing conversations:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="All Employees">
    Select **All Employees** to view WhatsApp conversations from everyone in your organization.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Specific Team">
    Choose a **specific team** from the dropdown to filter and view only that team's conversations.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Tip>
  Use team filtering to focus on specific departments or groups when reviewing conversation activity.
</Tip>
