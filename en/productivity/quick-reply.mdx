## How to Create a Quick Reply

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click the Quick Reply Button">
    Click the **Quick Reply** button in the Eazybe interface to open the creation dialog.

    ![Quick Reply Button](/images/1112602309.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name Your Quick Reply">
    Assign a descriptive name to your Quick Reply and type your desired message content.

    ![Name Quick Reply](/images/1112602543.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Visibility">
    Select the visibility setting for your Quick Reply:
    - **Personal** - Only you can see and use this reply
    - **Shared** - Available to your entire organization

    ![Choose Visibility](/images/1112602937.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Options">
    Optionally enable **Edit the reply before sending** if you want to customize the message each time you use it.

    ![Configure Options](/images/1112603250.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Enhancements (Optional)">
    You can enhance your Quick Reply with:
    - AI rephrasing for tone adjustment
    - Audio recording for voice messages
    - File attachments

    ![Add Enhancements](/images/1112603466.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Your Quick Reply">
    Click **Create** to finalize and save your Quick Reply.

    ![Create Quick Reply](/images/1112603584.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What visibility options are available?">
    You can choose from:
    - **Personal** - Only visible to you
    - **Shared** - Available to your entire organization
    - **Shared with team** - Available to specific team members
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I add attachments to Quick Replies?">
    Yes, you can attach files to your Quick Replies. The file size limit for attachments is **20MB**, and voice notes are also supported. You can include one file maximum per Quick Reply.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What file types are supported?">
    Various file types are supported for attachments. The maximum file size is 20MB per attachment.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is there a limit on how many Quick Replies I can create?">
    No, you can create **unlimited** Quick Replies to cover all your common messaging needs.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I edit or delete Quick Replies after creating them?">
    Yes, you have full edit and delete capability for all your Quick Replies. You can modify them anytime as your needs change.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Tip>
Create Quick Replies for your most common messages like greetings, follow-ups, and FAQs to dramatically speed up your response time.
</Tip>
