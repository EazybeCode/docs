Your WhatsApp conversations aren't syncing with Salesforce? You're moving your conversations forward, but the backup isn't getting stored on Salesforce. Follow this guide to resolve the issue.

## Check Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Verify Account Type">
    Ensure you have either a **Salesforce Developer Account** or **Salesforce Professional Edition**.

    <Warning>
      The integration does not support trial or Starter accounts.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify Subscription Status">
    Make sure both your Salesforce and Eazybe accounts maintain active standing.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Grant Access

Eazybe asks for specific permissions when integrating with Salesforce. Ensure that these permissions are granted, as they are crucial for storing WhatsApp conversations via the Eazybe WhatsApp Extension.

![Permission Settings](/images/1196810395.png)

<Tip>
  Accept all prompts during the integration process to enable smooth syncing of WhatsApp chats in Salesforce.
</Tip>
