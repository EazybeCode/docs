Admins have full access to the EazyBe workspace, allowing them to view and manage team details and analytics. This guide explains how to access and navigate the admin workspace.

## Viewing Team Details

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the Organisation Page">
    Click the **Organisation button** in the right sidebar of the EazyBe extension. This will open a new Chrome tab where the admin must log in using their credentials.

    ![Organisation Icon](/images/1185334534.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access the Dashboard">
    In the workspace, click the **Dashboard button** in the left sidebar to view your team overview.

    ![Dashboard Access](/images/1192481728.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check Team Details">
    From the dashboard, admins can view important information for all employees including:
    - Last activity timestamp
    - Last chat synced time
    - CRM integration status

    ![Team Details](/images/1192484906.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Analytics Overview

The **Analytics tab** provides comprehensive message analytics that can be filtered for the entire team or for individual employees.

![Analytics Dashboard](/images/1182373085.png)

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Team Analytics" icon="users">
    View aggregated metrics across your entire team's WhatsApp communications
  </Card>
  <Card title="Individual Analytics" icon="user">
    Drill down into specific employee performance and activity
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Info>
  The analytics dashboard helps you monitor team performance, track engagement metrics, and ensure consistent communication standards across your organization.
</Info>
