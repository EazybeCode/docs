## Before You Begin

Make sure you have:

<Check>Google Chrome browser (version 88 or higher)</Check>
<Check>An active WhatsApp account (Personal or Business)</Check>
<Check>A CRM account (HubSpot, Zoho, Salesforce, etc.)</Check>

## Step 1: Install the Chrome Extension

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Chrome Web Store">
    Visit the [Eazybe Chrome Extension](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/eazybe) page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Add to Chrome">
    Click the **Add to Chrome** button and confirm the installation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pin the Extension">
    Click the puzzle icon in Chrome's toolbar and pin Eazybe for easy access.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 2: Connect WhatsApp

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open WhatsApp Web">
    Go to [web.whatsapp.com](https://web.whatsapp.com) in Chrome.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scan QR Code">
    Open WhatsApp on your phone and scan the QR code to log in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Activate Eazybe">
    The Eazybe sidebar will automatically appear on the right side of WhatsApp Web.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  If you don't see the sidebar, click the Eazybe extension icon in your Chrome toolbar.
</Info>

## Step 3: Create Your Workspace

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up">
    Click **Get Started** in the Eazybe sidebar and create your account using email or Google.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Organization">
    Enter your organization name. This becomes your workspace for team collaboration.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Your Plan">
    Select a plan that fits your team size. Start with the free trial to explore all features.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 4: Connect Your CRM

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="HubSpot">
    1. Click the **Settings** icon in the Eazybe sidebar
    2. Select **Integrations** → **HubSpot**
    3. Click **Connect HubSpot**
    4. Authorize Eazybe in the HubSpot popup
    5. Select which HubSpot portal to connect
    
    [Full HubSpot setup guide →](/integrations/hubspot/setup)
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Zoho">
    1. Click the **Settings** icon in the Eazybe sidebar
    2. Select **Integrations** → **Zoho CRM**
    3. Click **Connect Zoho**
    4. Authorize Eazybe in the Zoho popup
    5. Select your Zoho organization
    
    [Full Zoho setup guide →](/integrations/zoho/setup)
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Salesforce">
    1. Click the **Settings** icon in the Eazybe sidebar
    2. Select **Integrations** → **Salesforce**
    3. Click **Connect Salesforce**
    4. Log in to your Salesforce account
    5. Authorize the connection
    
    [Full Salesforce setup guide →](/integrations/salesforce/setup)
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Other CRMs">
    See our [CRM Integrations](/integrations/overview) page for setup guides for:
    - Pipedrive
    - LeadSquared
    - Bitrix24
    - Google Sheets
    - Freshdesk
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Step 5: Start Using Eazybe

You're all set! Here's what you can do now:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Create CRM Contacts" icon="user-plus">
    Click the CRM icon in any WhatsApp chat to create or link contacts.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Auto-Sync Chats" icon="rotate">
    Conversations automatically sync to your CRM every 3-5 minutes.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Invite Your Team" icon="users">
    Go to Workspace → Team to invite colleagues and monitor their conversations.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Use Quick Replies" icon="bolt">
    Create templates for common responses to save time.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
