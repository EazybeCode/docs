## Overview

The **HubSpot Mini-CRM View** brings essential CRM functionality directly into your WhatsApp interface. With Eazybe's Chrome extension, you can access and manage your HubSpot data without switching between applications.

<img src="/images/1329120070.png" alt="HubSpot Mini-CRM View" />

<Note>
The Mini-CRM View appears in the right sidebar of WhatsApp Web when you have the Eazybe extension installed and connected to HubSpot.
</Note>

---

## Key Features

The Mini-CRM View provides quick access to five core HubSpot functions:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Contact Management" icon="address-book">
    Create and manage HubSpot contacts directly from WhatsApp conversations.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Task & Note Logging" icon="list-check">
    Log tasks and notes to keep track of follow-ups and important information.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Deal Management" icon="handshake">
    Create and track deals associated with your WhatsApp contacts.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Call Logging" icon="phone">
    Record call activities and maintain a complete communication history.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Card title="Support Tickets" icon="ticket">
  Create and manage HubSpot support tickets for customer service tracking.
</Card>

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open WhatsApp Web">
    Navigate to WhatsApp Web in your Chrome browser with the Eazybe extension installed.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select a Conversation">
    Click on any chat conversation to view the associated contact details in the Mini-CRM panel.

    <img src="/images/1329121065.png" alt="Select Conversation" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access Mini-CRM Features">
    Use the sidebar icons to access different CRM functions:

    - **Contacts** — View and edit contact properties
    - **Tasks** — Create follow-up tasks
    - **Notes** — Add notes to the contact record
    - **Deals** — Manage sales opportunities
    - **Calls** — Log call activities
    - **Tickets** — Create support tickets

    <img src="/images/1329123990.png" alt="Mini-CRM Features" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Sync with HubSpot">
    All changes made in the Mini-CRM View are automatically synced to your HubSpot account in real-time.

    <img src="/images/1329124748.png" alt="Sync with HubSpot" />
  </Step>
</Steps>
