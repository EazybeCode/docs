## Overview

Manage your team members, their roles, and access permissions.

## Inviting Team Members

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Team Settings">
    Go to **Settings** → **Team** or **Organization** → **Employees**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Add">
    Click **+ Add Employee** or **Invite Team Member**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Details">
    - Name
    - Email address
    - WhatsApp number (required for extension)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign Role">
    Select: Employee, Manager, or Admin
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send Invite">
    Click **Send Invite**
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Roles

| Role | Own Chats | Team Chats | All Chats | Settings |
|------|-----------|------------|-----------|----------|
| **Employee** | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ |
| **Manager** | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ | Limited |
| **Admin** | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |

## Onboarding Process

When you invite someone:

1. They receive email invitation
2. They create Eazybe account
3. They install Chrome extension
4. They connect their WhatsApp
5. Their chats sync to workspace

## Managing Members

### Change Role

1. Find member in team list
2. Click role dropdown
3. Select new role
4. Save

### Remove Member

1. Find member in team list
2. Click **Remove** or menu → Remove
3. Confirm removal

<Warning>
  Removing a member stops their chat sync. Historical data remains in Google Drive.
</Warning>

## Best Practices

<Tip>
  Start team members as Employees and promote to Manager once they're comfortable with the system.
</Tip>
