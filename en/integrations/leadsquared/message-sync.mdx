WhatsApp conversations automatically sync between EazyBe and LeadSquared, ensuring all your communications are backed up in your CRM.

## Automatic Sync

<Info>
  WhatsApp chats with LeadSquared leads synchronize automatically **every 3 minutes**. The EazyBe extension's LeadSquared Mini-CRM view displays the last synced date and time.
</Info>

![Last Sync Time Display](/images/1188126053.png)

## Initial Integration Backup

When you first integrate LeadSquared with EazyBe, the system automatically provides a chat backup for the **past 3 days**.

![Initial Backup](/images/1188126513.png)

## Where Chats Are Logged

### Activity History

Conversations are backed up under the **"WhatsApp Chats by EazyBe"** activity object within LeadSquared's leads section.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Leads">
    Go to the **Leads** dropdown and select **Manage Leads**.

    ![Manage Leads](/images/1188126933.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select a Lead">
    Choose the specific lead whose chat history you want to view.

    ![Select Lead](/images/1188127246.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="View Activity History">
    Navigate to the **Activity History** section.

    ![Activity History](/images/1188129860.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Find WhatsApp Chats">
    Check the **"WhatsApp Chats by EazyBe"** activity to view all synced conversations.

    ![WhatsApp Chats Activity](/images/1188130902.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Backup in Opportunity Section

Chats also appear in the LeadSquared **Opportunity** section for linked opportunities, ensuring all communications stay accessible for reference.

![Opportunity Chat Backup](/images/1188131665.png)

![Chat Details View](/images/1188128363.png)

<Tip>
  All synced conversations are preserved in LeadSquared, providing a complete communication history for each lead and opportunity.
</Tip>
