## What is Broadcasting?

Broadcasting lets you send personalized WhatsApp messages to thousands of contacts simultaneously. Unlike personal WhatsApp's 256-contact limit, WABA broadcasting has no upper limit.

## Broadcast Types

| Type | Description | Use Case |
|------|-------------|----------|
| Marketing | Promotional content | Sales, offers, newsletters |
| Utility | Transactional updates | Order confirmations, reminders |
| Authentication | Security codes | OTPs, verification |

## Limits & Pricing

| Tier | Daily Limit | How to Unlock |
|------|-------------|---------------|
| Tier 1 | 1,000 | New accounts |
| Tier 2 | 10,000 | Good quality rating |
| Tier 3 | 100,000 | Sustained quality |
| Tier 4 | Unlimited | Enterprise |

<Info>
  Meta charges per conversation, not per message. A conversation lasts 24 hours from first message.
</Info>
