## Where Can I Check the Last Sync Time for My Team?

<Check>
Admins can verify synchronization timestamps for their entire team through the **Eazybe Workspace Dashboard**.
</Check>

---

## How to Check Last Sync Time

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log In to Eazybe Workspace">
    Log in to your Eazybe Workspace account using your admin credentials.

    ![Eazybe Workspace login](/images/1185349004.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access the Dashboard">
    Click on the **Dashboard** button to view comprehensive team data and synchronization information.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Find the Last Chat Synced Column">
    Locate the **"Last Chat Synced"** column in the team overview table. This column displays the HubSpot chat sync timestamps for all team members.

    ![Dashboard showing Last Chat Synced column](/images/1185353530.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>
