To edit the properties of a LeadSquared lead created via EazyBe WhatsApp extension:


- Open the WhatsApp chat of the respective LeadSquared lead.


- In the **Contact Card** that appears, click on the **Edit** button.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1224270213.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


- A pop-up window will appear, allowing you to edit or select the lead's properties as per your requirements. You can also rearrange the order of property display and choose whether a property is required or optional.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1224270404.png" alt="" />
</Frame>


- After making your changes, click **Apply**. You will then see the updated properties in the Contact Card in the LeadSquared Mini-CRM view.


- Edit the properties as needed, and click **Save** to finalize your changes.
