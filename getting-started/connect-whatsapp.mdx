## Overview

Eazybe works with WhatsApp Web to provide CRM integration and team monitoring. This guide covers how to connect your WhatsApp account.

## Method 1: Standard QR Code Connection

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open WhatsApp Web">
    Navigate to [web.whatsapp.com](https://web.whatsapp.com) in Chrome.
    
    You'll see a QR code on the screen.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Open WhatsApp on Your Phone">
    **For Android:**
    - Open WhatsApp
    - Tap the three dots menu (â‹®)
    - Select **Linked Devices**
    
    **For iPhone:**
    - Open WhatsApp
    - Go to **Settings**
    - Tap **Linked Devices**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Scan the QR Code">
    - Tap **Link a Device**
    - Point your phone camera at the QR code on your computer screen
    - Wait for the connection to establish
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Verify Eazybe Activation">
    Once WhatsApp Web loads:
    - The Eazybe sidebar should appear on the right
    - You'll see your chats with the CRM integration icons
    
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/eazybe-sidebar.png" alt="Eazybe sidebar" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Method 2: Cloud Connection

Cloud connection enables chat backup even when your laptop is offline.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Cloud Settings">
    In the Eazybe workspace, go to **Settings** â†’ **Cloud Integration**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enable Cloud Backup">
    Toggle on **Cloud WhatsApp Connection**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Scan Cloud QR Code">
    A new QR code will appear. Scan this with your WhatsApp app just like the standard method.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm Connection">
    Your WhatsApp is now connected to Eazybe Cloud. Chats will sync even when your browser is closed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Cloud connection is available on paid plans. It allows CRM sync to continue even when you're not actively using WhatsApp Web.
</Info>

## Connecting Multiple Numbers

If you manage multiple WhatsApp numbers:

1. Each WhatsApp number requires its own Chrome profile or browser session
2. You can connect multiple numbers to the same Eazybe organization
3. Each number appears separately in the Revenue Inbox

<Warning>
  WhatsApp only allows one web session per number. Opening WhatsApp Web on another device will disconnect the previous session.
</Warning>

## Keeping Your Connection Active

WhatsApp Web requires your phone to maintain an internet connection. To keep your connection stable:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Keep Phone Connected">
    Ensure your phone has a stable WiFi or mobile data connection at all times.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Disable Battery Optimization">
    On Android, disable battery optimization for WhatsApp to prevent it from sleeping.
    
    Settings â†’ Apps â†’ WhatsApp â†’ Battery â†’ Don't optimize
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Enable Background App Refresh">
    On iPhone, enable background refresh for WhatsApp.
    
    Settings â†’ General â†’ Background App Refresh â†’ WhatsApp â†’ On
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Avoid Logging Out">
    Don't log out of WhatsApp Web unless necessary. Simply closing the browser tab doesn't log you out.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting Connection Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="QR Code Not Loading">
    - Refresh the WhatsApp Web page
    - Clear browser cache
    - Disable ad blockers temporarily
    - Try incognito mode
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Phone Not Scanning QR Code">
    - Clean your phone's camera lens
    - Increase screen brightness
    - Move closer to the screen
    - Ensure QR code is fully visible
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Connection Keeps Dropping">
    - Check your phone's internet connection
    - Restart WhatsApp on your phone
    - Log out and log back in to WhatsApp Web
    - Update WhatsApp to the latest version
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="'Phone Not Connected' Message">
    - Ensure your phone is online
    - Open WhatsApp on your phone to reestablish connection
    - Check if WhatsApp is updated
    - Restart your phone
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Security Notes

<Warning>
  **Security Best Practices:**
  - Only use WhatsApp Web on trusted devices
  - Log out when using shared computers
  - Regularly check Linked Devices in WhatsApp settings
  - Remove any unrecognized devices
</Warning>

To view and manage linked devices:
1. Open WhatsApp on your phone
2. Go to **Linked Devices**
3. Tap any device to log it out
