At Eazybe, we prioritize the privacy and security of our customers' data. This page outlines our commitment to protecting your information.

## Our Privacy Commitments

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="No Data Storage" icon="database">
    We do not store any contact chat data on our servers. The only data we collect is the essential contact information required to provide our services.
  </Card>
  <Card title="User Control" icon="sliders">
    Users have full control over their data and can manage, update, or delete their information as they see fit.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Encrypted Transfers" icon="lock">
    When transferring data to third-party CRMs, we use an encrypted channel process, ensuring that Eazybe does not retain any data during this transfer.
  </Card>
  <Card title="GDPR Compliance" icon="shield-check">
    We comply with all General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) policies and are in the process of applying for certification.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Key Privacy Principles

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Data Storage Policy">
    We prioritize the privacy of our customers and do not store any contact chat data on our servers. The only data we collect is the essential contact information required to provide our services.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="User Data Control">
    Users have full control over their data and can manage, update, or delete their information at any time through their account settings.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Third-Party Data Transfer">
    When transferring data to third-party CRMs, we use an encrypted channel process. Eazybe does not retain any data during this transfer, ensuring your information goes directly to your chosen CRM.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Regulatory Compliance">
    We comply with all General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) policies and are actively working toward certification to demonstrate our commitment to data protection.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
