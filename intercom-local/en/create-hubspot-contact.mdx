To create a new contact on HubSpot that is not yet in the system:

- Open the WhatsApp chat of the respective contact you wish to add.
- Click on the **HubSpot icon** in the right sidebar of the extension.

  

- Click on the **Create Contact** button.

  

- Congratulations, you have successfully added your first contact to HubSpot.

  

Note: The phone number field will be automatically filled based on the WhatsApp chats and will be non-editable.
