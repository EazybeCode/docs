This guide explains how to create a new lead in Salesforce directly from a WhatsApp conversation when the contact doesn't already exist in your CRM.

## Creating a New Lead

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the WhatsApp Chat">
    Access the conversation with the contact you want to add as a lead in Salesforce.

    ![Open WhatsApp Chat](/images/1116534683.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access the Salesforce Integration">
    Tap the **Salesforce** icon in the EazyBe extension's right sidebar to open the Mini-CRM panel.

    ![Salesforce Integration](/images/1196665966.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check for Existing Lead">
    Search for the contact in Salesforce. If found, you can link to the existing record instead of creating a duplicate.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a New Lead">
    If the contact isn't in Salesforce, select **Create Lead** to add them as a new lead.

    ![Create Lead Button](/images/1116535196.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Lead Information">
    Complete the required fields for the new lead. You can customize the form by clicking the **Edit** button to add additional properties.

    ![Lead Information Form](/images/1223671551.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Lead on Salesforce">
    After creation, click **View Lead on Salesforce** to open the record directly in your Salesforce account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  The lead will automatically be linked to this WhatsApp conversation, and future chats will sync to Salesforce every 3 minutes.
</Tip>
