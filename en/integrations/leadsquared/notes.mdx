---

Create and manage follow-up notes within the EazyBe LeadSquared Mini-CRM view to preserve important conversations and key messages for future reference.

## Creating and Managing Notes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select the Contact">
    Open the WhatsApp conversation with the contact that exists on LeadSquared.

    ![Select LeadSquared Contact](/images/1224277350.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access the Notes Section">
    Click the **LeadSquared icon** in the right sidebar and navigate to the **second section** in the top bar to access the Notes area.

    ![Notes Section](/images/1224277590.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add a New Note">
    Click the **"Add Note"** button for the respective lead to create a new note.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Note Details">
    Input your note content and click **"Save"**. The timestamp is automatically recorded for your reference.

    ![Save Note](/images/1224278242.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Edit or Delete Notes">
    Use the **three-dot menu** next to any note to modify or remove it as needed.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Multiple Notes">
    Add additional notes by repeating the **"Add Note"** action. All notes are preserved with their timestamps.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  You can also create notes for **LeadSquared Opportunities** using the same process, helping you track important details across all your CRM records.
</Info>

<Tip>
  Use notes to capture key conversation points, follow-up reminders, and important customer details that you want to reference later in your sales process.
</Tip>
