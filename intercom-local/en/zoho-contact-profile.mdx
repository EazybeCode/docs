The **Profile Section** of the Zoho contact displays basic contact details. Follow these steps to access and edit this section:

- 
#### Open the WhatsApp Chat
Open the WhatsApp chat of your Zoho Contact.

- 
#### Click on the ZOHO icon
In the right-side bar of the EazyBe extension, click on the Zoho icon.

  

- 
#### Access the Profile Section
Click on the **Contact** header. The first section that opens up will be the **Profile Section**.

  

- 
#### View and Edit Details
You can view and edit user properties for your contact. After making changes, click on the **Save** button to update the profile.
