No, this isn't your usual social media comment section. This area is exclusively designed for you to store notes from your WhatsApp conversations with your contacts and to track the progress or stage where your conversation left off.

## Adding a Contact Comment

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the WhatsApp Chat">
    Open the WhatsApp chat of your Bitrix contact.

    ![Open WhatsApp chat](/images/1224483840.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click on the Bitrix Icon">
    Click on the **Bitrix** icon located in the right sidebar of the Eazybe extension.

    ![Bitrix icon in sidebar](/images/1224484997.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access the Comment Section">
    The third header will be the **Comment** section. Click on it to access the section.

    ![Comment section](/images/1224486372.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a Comment">
    Click on the **Add Comment** button.

    ![Add comment button](/images/1224487514.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in the Required Details">
    Enter the necessary details and click on the **Save** button.

    ![Fill comment details](/images/1224489306.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Your Created Comment">
    Your comment will be created. You will see options to view the **note**, **edit**, or **delete** the created comment by clicking on the three dots menu.

    ![View and manage comment](/images/1224492088.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>
