### Create a New Lead in LeadSquared

### Step 1: Open the Lead's WhatsApp Chat
Open the WhatsApp chat of the contact you want to create a lead for.

### Step 2: Access the LeadSquared Mini-CRM View
Click on the LeadSquared icon in the right sidebar. The Leads section will appear.

### Step 3: Link an Existing Lead

  

If the contact already exists as a lead in LeadSquared:

- Click on **Link Contact**.
- Select the lead from the dropdown.
- Click the **Link** button.

### Step 4: Create a New Lead

  

If the contact is new and not yet a lead in LeadSquared:

- Click on the **Create Contact** button.
- Fill in the required details in the provided fields. You can also customize the fields according to your preferences.

### Step 5: View the Lead Directly in LeadSquared
To view the lead directly in LeadSquared, click on the **View Lead on LeadSquared** button.

---

### Customize Lead Properties in LeadSquared

### Step 1: Open the WhatsApp Chat of the Lead
Access the WhatsApp chat of the respective LeadSquared lead.

### Step 2: Edit Lead Properties

- In the Contact Card that appears, click on the **Edit** button.

  

- A pop-up window will appear, allowing you to edit or select the lead's properties as per your requirements.

  

- Rearrange the order of property display and choose whether a property is required or optional.

### Step 3: Save the Changes

- After making your changes, click **Apply**. You will then see the updated properties in the Contact Card in the LeadSquared Mini-CRM view.
Edit the properties as needed and click **Save** to finalize your changes.
