Admins can check the last sync time for their team on the Eazybe Workspace.

## How to Check Last Sync Time

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to Eazybe Workspace">
    Admin should log in to the [Eazybe Workspace](https://workspace.eazybe.com/) using their credentials.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Navigate to Dashboard">
    Click on the **Dashboard** button.

    ![Dashboard Button](/images/1196795192.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="View Last Sync Time">
    In the **Last Chat Synced** column, you will see the last sync time of the Salesforce chats for all your employees.

    ![Last Chat Synced Column](/images/1196800803.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  The Last Chat Synced column helps you monitor team activity and ensure all conversations are being backed up properly.
</Tip>
