## Overview

The Eazybe Odoo integration allows you to connect your WhatsApp conversations directly with your Odoo CRM. Sync contacts, create leads, add notes, and manage your customer relationships without leaving WhatsApp.

## Integration Features

### Mini CRM View
Access Odoo contact information directly within WhatsApp:
- View contact details
- Create and edit contacts
- Add leads and opportunities
- Attach notes to conversations

### Label-Based Sync
Control which contacts get synced based on WhatsApp labels for organized backup.

### Direct Redirect
Click "View on Odoo" to jump directly to a contact's profile in your Odoo instance.

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Prerequisites">
    Ensure you have:
    <ul>
      <li>Active Odoo account with admin access</li>
      <li>Odoo Instance URL</li>
      <li>API Key generated from Odoo</li>
    </ul>
    <Tip>
    Check the [Prerequisites](/en/integrations/odoo/prerequisites) guide for detailed steps on getting your API key.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect Your Account">
    Follow the [Account Connection](/en/integrations/odoo/account-connection) guide to link your Odoo instance.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Use Mini CRM">
    See the [Mini CRM](/en/integrations/odoo/mini-crm) guide to learn about creating contacts and managing data.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Manage Chat Backup">
    Configure label-based sync to control which conversations are backed up to Odoo.
  </Step>
</Steps>
