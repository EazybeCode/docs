## Prerequisites Checklist

<Steps>
  <Step title="Active Salesforce Account">
    You need to ensure that your Salesforce account is set up and functioning properly. Verify that you can log in and access your Salesforce dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="EazyBe WhatsApp Extension Installed">
    Ensure that your EazyBe WhatsApp Extension is properly installed and connected to WhatsApp Web. The extension should be active in your Chrome browser.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Required Permissions">
    Ensure you grant the necessary permissions to connect Salesforce with WhatsApp. These permissions will be requested during the integration process.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Once you have verified all prerequisites, you can proceed with the installation. Check out our guide on [how to install and connect EazyBe with your Salesforce account](/en/integrations/salesforce/setup) for step-by-step instructions.
</Info>
