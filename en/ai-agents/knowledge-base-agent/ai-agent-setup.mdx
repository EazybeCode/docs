## Overview

This guide provides step-by-step instructions on how to create a new AI agent, define its purpose, and configure various aspects like tools, communication channels, and billing preferences. Learn to customize your agent to best suit your specific operational needs.

![AI Agents Overview](/images/ai-agent-1.png)

<Note>
Before you begin, ensure you have **Admin permissions** to create and manage AI agents in your workspace.
</Note>

---

## Step-by-Step Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the Agent Gallery">
    Navigate to the **AI Agents** section in your Eazybe dashboard to access the Agent Gallery. This is your starting point for creating intelligent automated responses.

    ![Agent Gallery](/images/ai-agent-3.png)

    <Info>
    The Agent Gallery displays all your existing agents and provides options to create new ones or use pre-built templates.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start Custom Agent Creation">
    Click on **Create Agent** to begin creating your custom AI agent. This opens the agent creation interface where you'll define your agent's capabilities.

    ![Create Agent](/images/ai-agent-5.png)

    <Tip>
    You can choose from templates or create a completely custom agent from scratch based on your specific requirements.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Describe Your Agent">
    Enter a comprehensive description for your agent. This is the most critical step — the quality of your description directly impacts your agent's performance.

    ![Describe Agent](/images/ai-agent-7.png)

    **Describe your agent however you prefer:**

    - **Single line**: *"Reply to my WhatsApp customer inquiries about pricing"*
    - **Bullet points**:
      * Greet the customer
      * Ask what they need
      * Share pricing
      * Collect their email
    - **Detailed instructions**: *"When a customer asks about pricing, share our rate card. If they ask for a discount, tell them to talk to the sales team."*
    - **Any language**: Hindi, English, Hinglish, Spanish — use whatever language you're comfortable with

    <Warning>
    The better and more specific your description, the smarter your agent will be. Include details about:
    - Your business context
    - How you want the agent to respond
    - What actions it should take
    - Any specific behaviors to avoid
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create Your Agent">
    Click **Create Agent** when ready. The system will automatically generate a complete agent configuration based on your description.

    ![Click Create Agent](/images/ai-agent-9.png)

    The system intelligently generates:
    - **Role** — who the agent is (e.g., "You are a sales assistant for XYZ company")
    - **Goal** — what it should achieve (e.g., "Convert inquiries into booked demos")
    - **Flow** — step-by-step conversation flow (e.g., "First greet, then ask about their need, then share pricing")
    - **Tools** — what actions the agent can take (e.g., "Search knowledge base, schedule follow-up")

    <Info>
    You don't need to write any of this yourself. If the system needs more clarity on something, it will ask you before finalizing.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Engage with Ace (Optional)">
    On the left side of the screen, you'll find **Ace** — your AI-powered agent builder assistant. Think of Ace as your co-pilot for refining your agent.

    ![Ace Assistant](/images/ai-agent-11.png)

    Use plain language to tell Ace what you want to change:
    - *"Add a step where the agent asks for the customer's email"*
    - *"Make the tone more professional"*
    - *"Add the follow-up tool so the agent can send reminders"*
    - *"If the customer is angry, transfer to a human"*
    - *"Change the greeting message"*

    <Tip>
    Ace understands natural language — no coding or technical knowledge required. Whatever you want to change about your agent's behavior, just tell Ace and it will update the prompt, tools, and flow for you.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review and Refine">
    Review the agent that the system has built for you. Provide additional details if the system asks for clarification on any aspect of the agent's behavior or capabilities.

    ![Review Progress](/images/ai-agent-13.png)

    <Info>
    This is your opportunity to fine-tune the agent before deployment. Review the generated role, goals, conversation flow, and available tools.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit Agent Details">
    Click on **"Add"** to confirm your agent details and proceed to the next configuration stage.

    ![Add Details](/images/ai-agent-15.png)

    <Warning>
    Ensure you've reviewed all settings before proceeding. You can always modify your agent later, but getting it right initially saves time.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure Agent Access">
    Submit agent access details for workflows and integrations. This determines what systems and data your agent can interact with.

    ![Agent Access](/images/ai-agent-17.png)

    <Tip>
    Configure permissions carefully — only grant access to the tools and data sources that your agent legitimately needs to function properly.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Proceed to Configuration">
    Navigate to the configuration section to customize your agent's tools, knowledge base, communication channels, and billing preferences.

    ![Agent Configuration](/images/ai-agent-19.png)

    <Info>
    This is where you'll fine-tune your agent's capabilities before going live.
    </Info>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Configuration Options

After creating your agent, configure these essential components:

### Tools

Choose what your agent can actually do. Each tool is a capability you can enable:

| Tool | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Search Knowledge Base** | Agent looks up answers from your uploaded documents and website |
| **Send WhatsApp Messages** | Agent replies directly to customers |
| **Schedule Follow-ups** | Agent sets reminders and sends follow-up messages automatically |
| **Transfer to Human** | Agent hands off to your team when needed and stops responding |
| **Create/Update CRM Contacts** | Agent saves customer information to your CRM system |
| **Google Sheets, Calendar, Gmail** | Connect external apps for tasks like booking appointments or logging data |

<Tip>
    Enable only the tools you need for your specific use case. Each enabled tool gives your agent more capabilities but also more responsibility.
</Tip>

### Knowledge Base

Teach your agent about your business. The more knowledge you provide, the better it will respond:

- **Documents** — Upload PDFs, brochures, price lists, FAQs, product catalogs
- **Website URLs** — Paste your website link, help docs, or product pages. The system automatically reads and learns from them
- **WhatsApp Chat Sync** — Enable this to train the agent on your existing WhatsApp conversations. It learns your tone, style, and how you talk to customers

<Warning>
    Your agent uses this knowledge to answer customer questions accurately. Ensure the information you provide is current and accurate.
</Warning>

### WhatsApp Integration

Connect your agent to a WhatsApp number so it can start responding to customers:

- Choose any WhatsApp Business API or Coexistence number
- Your WhatsApp Business app continues working normally — no changes to your existing workflow
- Both you and the agent can reply to the same customer simultaneously
- Customers won't know they're talking to an AI
- Connection takes approximately 2 minutes

<Info>
    Once connected, every incoming message on that number goes through your agent first. You can override or intervene in any conversation at any time.
</Info>

### Billing Preferences

Control your spending by choosing the appropriate LLM model for each use case:

| Model | Best For | Cost | Speed |
|-------|----------|------|-------|
| **Light LLM** | Simple Q&A, FAQs, straightforward conversations | Lower | Faster responses |
| **Heavy LLM** | Complex conversations, negotiations, nuanced understanding | Higher | Smarter responses |

<Tip>
    You can switch between Light and Heavy anytime. The billing dashboard shows cost per message, total spend, and usage breakdown.
</Tip>

## Test Before Publishing

Before going live, test your agent:

1. Navigate to the **Test** tab
2. Input realistic test queries
3. Review the agent's responses
4. Adjust the prompt, knowledge base, or tools as needed

<Warning>
    Thorough testing ensures your agent behaves as expected in real customer scenarios.
</Warning>

---

## Publish and Activate

When satisfied with your agent configuration:

1. Click **Publish** to finalize the setup
2. Activate the AI Agent
3. Your agent is now live and ready to handle customer conversations
