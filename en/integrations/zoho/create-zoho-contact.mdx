How to create a new contact on Zoho

- 
#### Open the WhatsApp Chat
Open the WhatsApp chat of your Zoho contact you want to create.

- 
#### Click on the Zoho Icon
In the right sidebar of the EazyBe extension, click on the **Zoho icon**.

  

- 
#### Access the Contact Section
Click on the **Contact header** in the Zoho popup.

- 
#### Fill in the Details
Complete the necessary fields and customize the user properties by clicking the **Edit** button if needed.

  

  

#### 5. Create the Contact
Click on the **Create Contact** button, and your contact will be created.

#### 6. View Contact on Zoho
You can directly view the contact in Zoho by clicking on the **View Contact on Zoho** button in the contact's profile section
