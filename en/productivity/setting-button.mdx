title: "Setting Button"

---

The Settings button provides access to a comprehensive panel of customization options for your Eazybe experience, including privacy settings, display preferences, and personalization features.

## Accessing the Settings Panel

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click the Settings Button">
    Click on the settings button on the right-side bar to open the settings panel.

    ![Settings Button](/images/1112612815.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Setting Scope">
    You will see a list of sections. Admins can select whether settings apply to **everyone** or **everyone except admin** by clicking on the dropdown.

    ![Settings Scope](/images/1112613701.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Security Settings

### Blur Contact Name

Checking this checkbox will blur the contact name for all contacts.

![Blur Contact Name](/images/1112613851.png)

### Blur Contact Image

Checking this checkbox will blur the profile picture of all contacts.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Will blurred details be visible to others?">
    No, blurred details won't be visible to others after applying the settings.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What is the purpose of blurring?">
    This feature enhances privacy in chats and groups by hiding identifying information.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Where does the blurring apply?">
    The blurring feature applies to individual chats, group chats, and about sections.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I blur specific contacts only?">
    Currently, blurring settings apply universally to all contacts. You cannot selectively blur individual contacts.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I customize settings for admin vs. others?">
    Yes, you can customize settings for everyone or everyone except the admin using the dropdown selector.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Customizable Settings

### AI Button

Checking this checkbox will enable the AI button in your interface.

![AI Button Setting](/images/1112614457.png)

### Make Full Screen

Checking this checkbox will make WhatsApp cover the entire screen for a distraction-free experience.

### Add Signature

You can add a personalized signature that appears above every outgoing WhatsApp chat message.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable Signature">
    Check the **Add Signature** checkbox to enable the feature.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Signature">
    Enter your desired signature text in the text field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Signature">
    Click the tick mark to save your signature.
  </Step>
</Steps>

![Add Signature](/images/1112614800.png)

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is there a character limit for signatures?">
    There is no specified character limit for signature text. You can customize it as needed.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do signatures apply to all messages?">
    Signatures apply only to outgoing messages, not to incoming messages.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I use emojis in my signature?">
    Emojis are not currently supported in signatures.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I disable my signature?">
    You can disable your signature by turning off the Add Signature checkbox.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Hide All Label

You can hide the **All** label from the top bar by checking this checkbox.

![Hide All Label](/images/1112615068.png)

<Tip>
Use the Settings button to quickly customize your Eazybe experience. Privacy settings like contact blurring are especially useful when sharing your screen during presentations or demos.
</Tip>
