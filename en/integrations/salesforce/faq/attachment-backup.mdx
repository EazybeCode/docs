---

Yes, Eazybe supports attachment backup! When syncing WhatsApp conversations with Salesforce, various types of attachments are automatically backed up.

## Supported Attachment Types

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Images" icon="image">
    Photos and screenshots shared in chats
  </Card>
  <Card title="Documents" icon="file-lines">
    Files such as PDFs, Word documents, and spreadsheets
  </Card>
  <Card title="Audio Files" icon="volume-high">
    Voice messages and other audio recordings
  </Card>
  <Card title="Videos" icon="video">
    Video clips shared in conversations
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  **Other Files**: Any other types of files that may be shared in WhatsApp chats are also backed up.
</Note>

## Storage Location

Attachments from WhatsApp conversations are stored in the **"WhatsApp Chats by Eazybe"** custom activity object on both Leads and Contacts pages.

![Attachment Backup in Salesforce](/images/1196820000.png)

<Tip>
  You can click the links created for attachments to download your backed-up files directly from Salesforce.
</Tip>
