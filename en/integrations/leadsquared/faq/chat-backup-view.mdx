Your WhatsApp conversations are automatically backed up to LeadSquared through EazyBe. Here's where you can find these chat backups within LeadSquared.

## Backup Locations

WhatsApp chat backups with LeadSquared leads are accessible in two primary locations:

### 1. Leads Page

Chat backups appear on the individual lead's dedicated page within LeadSquared. Navigate to any lead's profile to view their complete WhatsApp conversation history.

![Lead Page Chat Backup](/images/1188146542.png)

### 2. Lead Opportunities Page

Conversations are also backed up within the Opportunities section of each lead's profile. This allows you to view chat history in the context of specific opportunities.

![Opportunities Page Chat Backup](/images/1188146266.png)

<Info>
  Both locations display the same WhatsApp conversation data, allowing you to access chat history from whichever view is most relevant to your workflow.
</Info>

<Tip>
  Use the Leads Page for general conversation review and the Opportunities Page when you need to reference chats in the context of a specific deal or opportunity.
</Tip>
