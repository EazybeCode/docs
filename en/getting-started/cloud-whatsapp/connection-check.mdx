---
title: "Connection Check"
description: "How to ensure that your EazyBe Cloud WhatsApp has been successfully connected"
---

There are two ways to verify that your EazyBe Cloud is successfully connected:

## Method 1: Check the Cloud Icon

Look for the cloud symbol in the EazyBe WhatsApp extension. A **green indicator** signifies successful connection status.

![Cloud Icon Status](/images/1203765301.png)

<Tip>
The green cloud icon confirms your Cloud WhatsApp is actively connected and syncing.
</Tip>

---

## Method 2: Check Your Workspace

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open EazyBe Workspace">
    Navigate to your EazyBe Workspace dashboard.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Locate Your Account">
    Find the account name in the workspace interface.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify Status">
    Examine the adjacent status field. The text **"Connected"** confirms successful cloud linking.
  </Step>
</Steps>
