Manage deals associated with your Zoho Contacts directly from WhatsApp Chat. Edit deal properties, update stages, and keep your sales pipeline current without leaving your conversations.

## How to Manage Contact Deals

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the WhatsApp Chat">
    Access the WhatsApp conversation with the Zoho Contact whose deals you want to manage.

    ![Open WhatsApp Chat](/images/1217633106.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click on the Zoho Icon">
    In the right sidebar of the EazyBe extension, locate and click on the **Zoho icon** to access your CRM features.

    ![Click Zoho Icon](/images/1223646693.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access the Deals Section">
    Under the Contact header, select the **Deals Section** to view all existing deals associated with this contact.

    ![Deals Section](/images/1223646317.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Edit a Deal">
    To modify an existing deal:
    1. Click the **three dots** (â‹®) next to the specific deal you want to edit
    2. Click the **pencil icon** to open the deal details
    3. Edit existing properties or add new ones as needed

    ![Edit Deal](/images/1223646935.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save Your Changes">
    After making your modifications, click the **Save** button to update the deal with your changes.

    ![Save Changes](/images/1223647779.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Deal Management Options

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Edit Properties" icon="pen-to-square">
    Modify deal values, stages, amounts, and custom fields
  </Card>
  <Card title="View Chat Backup" icon="clock-rotate-left">
    Access chat history synced with the deal record
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Info>
  You can view the chat backup in the **5 most recent deals** of your Zoho Contact, making it easy to reference past conversations related to each opportunity.
</Info>

<Tip>
  Regularly updating your deal stages helps maintain an accurate sales pipeline and ensures your team has visibility into the latest opportunity status.
</Tip>
