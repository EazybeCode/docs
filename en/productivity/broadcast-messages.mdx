Want to send a message to multiple contacts at the same time? Eazybe's broadcast message feature makes it easy to reach your entire audience with a single action.

## How to Send Broadcast Messages

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Broadcast Messages">
    Click on the **Broadcast Messages** button in the top bar.

    ![Broadcast Messages Button](/images/1112608994.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a Quick Reply">
    Select the Quick Reply from the dropdown that you want to send as a broadcast.

    ![Select Quick Reply](/images/1112609104.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Target Label">
    Select the Label for which you want to send the quick reply to. This allows you to target specific groups of contacts.

    ![Select Label](/images/1112609231.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send or Create New">
    You have two options:
    - Click **Send Message** to send the selected quick reply
    - Click **Create New Quick Reply** to create a new message for this broadcast

    ![Send Message](/images/1112609389.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
This feature should be used with caution and in a limited manner. Make sure to read the disclaimer carefully after clicking on the Send Message button.
</Warning>

<Tip>
Organize your contacts with Labels before using broadcast messages to ensure your message reaches the right audience.
</Tip>
