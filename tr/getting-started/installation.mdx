## Installation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Chrome Web Store">

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/getting-started/extension-install.png" alt="Chrome Web Store installation" />
</Frame>

    Visit the Eazybe extension page
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add to Chrome">
    Click **Add to Chrome** and confirm
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open WhatsApp Web">
    Go to web.whatsapp.com
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify">
    Eazybe panel appears on the right
  </Step>
</Steps>
