## How to Create Lead Notes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the WhatsApp Chat">
    Launch the WhatsApp conversation with your Zoho Lead that you want to add notes to.

    ![Open WhatsApp Chat](/images/1112962785.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click on the Zoho Icon">
    In the right-side panel of the EazyBe extension, locate and click on the **Zoho icon** to access your CRM features.

    ![Click Zoho Icon](/images/1112962881.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access the Notes Section">
    The **Notes section** is located as the second header under the Lead section. Click on it to view existing notes or create new ones.

    ![Notes Section](/images/1112963181.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create a New Note">
    Click on the **New Note** button to open the note creation form.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fill in the Details and Save">
    Enter your note details in the form and click the **Save Note** button to save your entry.

    ![Save Note](/images/1190994468.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing Your Notes

Once created, you can perform the following actions on your notes:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="View" icon="eye">
    Review all your saved notes for the lead
  </Card>
  <Card title="Edit" icon="pen">
    Modify existing notes to update information
  </Card>
  <Card title="Delete" icon="trash">
    Remove notes that are no longer needed
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
  Regularly documenting your conversations helps maintain a complete history of your interactions with each lead, making follow-ups more effective.
</Tip>
