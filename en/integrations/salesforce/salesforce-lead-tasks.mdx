Do you have a **pending task** that you need to save and track later? Don’t worry! The **Task Section** of EazyBe’s Salesforce integration has got you covered. Follow these simple steps to stay on top of your tasks.

- 
#### Open the WhatsApp Chat
Open the WhatsApp chat of your **Salesforce lead**.

- 
#### Click on the Salesforce Icon
Click on the Salesforce icon in the right sidebar of the extension.

  

- 
#### Access the Tasks Section
Under the **Lead Section**, locate the **third header** labeled **Tasks** and click on it to access the section.

- 
#### Create a New Task
Click on the **New Task** button.

  

- 
#### Save the task
Enter the task details and then click **Save Task**.

  

- 
#### Manage your Created Task
You can view your created task. You can also **Edit** or **Delete** a created task by clicking on the three dots.
