Let’s learn how to **log calls** for your **HubSpot contacts** directly through the **EazyBe WhatsApp extension**

- ### Select the ContactOpen the WhatsApp chat of the contact for whom you want to create a call log. Make sure that the contact is created in HubSpot.

- ### Access the Call Logs SectionClick on the **HubSpot icon** in the right sidebar. The fifth button in the top bar will take you to the **Call Logs section**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1112632101.png" alt="" />
</Frame>

- ### Create a New Call LogClick on the New Call Logs button.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1112632305.png" alt="" />
</Frame>

- ### Fill Out the Details You’ll see several fields to complete: 

**Call Outcome**: Choose a suitable option from the dropdown menu.

**Direction**: Select the direction of the call (e.g., inbound or outbound).

**Date & Time: **Specify when the call took place.

**Call Description:** Enter relevant details about the call in the provided text box.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1112632477.png" alt="" />
</Frame>

- ### Save your Call LogOnce you've filled in all the necessary fields, click on the **Save Call Log** button to save the entry. To create additional call logs, simply click on the **Add** button as given below.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1112632645.png" alt="" />
</Frame>
